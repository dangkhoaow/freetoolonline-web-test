SVG Sang PNG - Khi Nao Rasterize

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 2026-04-27. Mo SVG sang PNG de rasterize trong trinh duyet cua ban ma khong tai len tep.

Cau tra loi 30 giay. SVG thuong nen giu SVG - no sac net hon va nho hon cho logo, icon, va minh hoa. Rasterize sang PNG chi khi dich khong the render SVG: phan mem cu (PowerPoint cu hon, mot so trinh tao PDF), preview chia se mang xa hoi, ung dung email, va upload den he thong chi cho phep raster.

Tai sao SVG thuong la mac dinh tot hon

SVG la vector - tep mo ta cac duong, duong cong, va lap day, khong phai pixel. Trinh duyet render no o kich thuoc ban hien thi, va ket qua luon sac net. Mot logo SVG 4 KB trong giong y nhu o 24 px va o 2400 px.

Nam lan ban can rasterize

  1. Phan mem cu. PowerPoint, Word, va nhieu trinh chinh sua van ban CMS cu khong xu ly SVG. Rasterize truoc.
  2. Preview chia se mang xa hoi. Specs OG va Twitter Card cho phep PNG/JPG cho og:image; SVG duoc render khong nhat quan. Rasterize sang PNG 1200x630.
  3. Ung dung email. Hau het van loai bo <svg> tu HTML inline vi an ninh.
  4. He thong upload voi whitelist. Marketplace, app store chi chap nhan JPG/PNG.
  5. Pipeline in. Neu nha cung cap rasterize tat ca sang PDF 300 DPI, hay tu xuat o do phan giai muc tieu.
