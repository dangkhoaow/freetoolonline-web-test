Cach nen tep ZIP xuong 100 KB

"Lam cho ZIP nay duoi 100 KB" la mot cau hoi ve kich co ma dinh dang archive khong phai luc nao cung co the tra loi. Liec mot gioi han 100 KB co the dat duoc khong phu thuoc vao nhung gi ben trong archive: mot thu muc nhat ky van ban thuan, xuat CSV hoac ma nguon thuong nen duoc duoi 100 KB khi DEFLATE loai bo cac mau lap lai; mot thu muc anh JPG, clip MP4 hoac PDF da nen thuong khong, vi codec rieng cua moi tep da ep ra su du thua ma vo boc ZIP se loai bo neu khong.

Chon dau vao co the dat 100 KB mot minh

Con duong thuc te den archive 100 KB la thu nho dau vao truoc, khong dua vao muc do nen ZIP. Dau vao van ban thuan nen quyet liet vi co cac doan dai cua token lap lai; mot thu muc 1 MB nhat ky truy cap, xuat CSV hoac ma nguon thuong nen duoi 200 KB va doi khi duoi 100 KB ben trong mot .zip duy nhat. Anh JPG, video MP4, am thanh MP3 va hau het PDF den voi archive da nen; vo boc ZIP khong the giam chung them va doi khi se them mot vai phan tram chi phi dong goi. Neu dau vao nhieu phuong tien, muc tieu 100 KB thuong khong thuc te ma khong ma hoa lai phuong tien truoc - thu nho JPG hoac xuat lai video o toc do bit thap hon truoc khi luu tru.

"Muc do nen" lam gi va khong lam gi

Trinh tao tai /zip-tools/zip-file.html phoi bay mot thanh truot muc do nen. Muc cao hon (Toi da, Cuc dai) tieu hao nhieu thoi gian CPU hon de tim them cac mau lap lai nhung hiem khi cat nhieu hon mot vai phan tram them tu archive nhieu van ban khi qua DEFLATE tieu chuan da chay. Muc thap hon (Luu tru, Nhanh) chi giup khi toc do quan trong hon kich co - vi du mot thu muc rat lon duoc tai len qua ket noi cham. Thanh truot muc do chu yeu thay doi thoi gian xay dung archive, khong phai liec no nen duoi muc tieu nhu 100 KB hay khong. Kha nang nen cua dau vao dat tran, khong phai nut muc do.

Khi muc tieu 100 KB khong dat duoc, chia goi

Cho gioi han dinh kem 100 KB hoac han ngach luu tru cung kich co, cau tra loi kha thi doi khi la chia tach giao hang thay vi chien dau voi archive. ZIP cac dau vao nen duoc cung nhau - cac tep dang van ban - va chung thuong dat duoi 100 KB mot minh; gui cac tep phuong tien trong mot kenh rieng (lien ket chia se tep, URL luu tru dam may hoac mot archive tiep theo nho hon) noi gioi han 100 KB khong ap dung. Giao hang hai goi thuong nho hon va dang tin cay hon mot ZIP duy nhat that bai kiem tra kich co.
