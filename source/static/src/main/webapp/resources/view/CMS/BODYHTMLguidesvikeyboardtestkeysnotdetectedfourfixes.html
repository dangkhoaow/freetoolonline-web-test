Phim Khong Phat Hien Trong Kiem Tra Ban Phim: 4 Cach Khac Phuc Thuc Su

Lan ra soat cuoi 2026-05-03. Neu cong cu kiem tra ban phim trinh duyet mo nhung mot so phim khong bao gio sang khi ban bam, ban phim hiem khi bi hong - mot trong bon nguyen nhan duoi day kha nang xay ra cao hon nhieu. Di tu tren xuong; moi cach sua mat duoi mot phut.

Cau tra loi 30 giay. Phim duoc bam nhung khong bao gio sang trong kiem tra ban phim trinh duyet gan nhu luon co nghia trang da mat focus, he dieu hanh gui scancode khac voi cai bai test mong, mot bo loc tro nang dang chan su kien, hoac phim bi khoa o lop phan cung - khong phai phan cung loi. Buoc 1: nhap bat ky cho nao tren trang kiem tra ban phim de no co tab focus. Buoc 2: xac nhan layout ban phim he dieu hanh khop voi nhan vat ly (Quyen rieng tu va Bao mat → Ban phim / Vung). Buoc 3: tat Sticky Keys, Filter Keys, va bat ky driver ban phim ao nao co the hap thu su kien. Buoc 4: kiem tra mot toggle hang Fn, mot den LED Insert/ScrollLock, hoac mot van de ket noi USB. Hau het bao cao "phim khong phat hien" giai quyet trong buoc 1 hoac buoc 3.

Cach 1 - Trang da mat focus ban phim

Trinh duyet dinh tuyen su kien phim den phan tu nao co focus. Kiem tra ban phim ve mot layout tren man hinh, nhung tru khi ban nhap vao trang truoc, cac phim cua ban di den thanh dia chi, popup mo rong, hoac tab khac. Trieu chung: moi phim co ve hoat dong (OS bat duoc), nhung khong gi sang trong bai test.

Nhap vao bai test: nhap mot khu vuc trong cua trang test ban phim mot lan. Trang gio so huu dinh tuyen phim; bam mot phim va no se sang ngay lap tuc.

Dong popup va overlay: nhac autofill, mo rong quan ly mat khau, va cong cu screenshot (Snagit, Greenshot) doi khi bat hotkey toan cuc. Dong moi popup, sau do test lai.

Chuyen tab va chuyen ve: neu mot client hop (Zoom, Teams) la cua so foreground truoc, no co the van giu phim toan cuc. Chuyen tab va ve lai bai test ban phim de buoc trinh duyet doi lai focus.

Neu nhap lai vao trang lam phim sang, focus la nguyen nhan. Tien len chi neu sang van that bai.

Cach 2 - Layout hoac ngon ngu ban phim he dieu hanh khong khop

Bai test ban phim dung JavaScript KeyboardEvent.code de nhan dien phim vat ly (vi du KeyA, Quote). Khi layout ban phim he dieu hanh khong khop voi nhan in tren phim, lan bam dang ky, nhung roi xuong mot vi tri tren man hinh khac - co ve "phim khong lam gi" vi sang xuat hien o o khac.

macOS: Cai dat He thong → Ban phim → Nguon dau vao. Xac nhan layout dang hoat dong khop voi ban phim vat ly (US, UK, Telex Viet, vv). Neu ban gan day chuyen ngon ngu, chuyen lai; bai test se sang o dung.

Windows 11 / 10: Cai dat → Thoi gian va ngon ngu → Ngon ngu va vung. Kiem tra phuong phap nhap dang hoat dong theo ngon ngu. Bam Win + Space de luan phien layout va test lai.

Linux (X11 / Wayland): chay setxkbmap -query de xem layout dang hoat dong; setxkbmap us reset ve layout US de test.

Kiem tra nhanh: bam phim Q o goc tren ben trai va xac nhan "Q" sang tren layout test. Neu sang "A" thay vi do, he dieu hanh nghi ban phim la AZERTY (Phap). Chuyen layout he dieu hanh, dung gan nhan lai phim.

Cach 3 - Bo loc tro nang dang chan su kien

He dieu hanh hien dai di kem cong cu tro nang (Sticky Keys, Filter Keys, Slow Keys, Mouse Keys) hap thu hoac tre cac phim truoc khi trinh duyet thay. Sticky Keys bien nhap chord (Ctrl + C) thanh nhap chuoi (Ctrl, sau do C); Filter Keys bo qua cac lan bam nhanh de giup nguoi dung run tay; ca hai cung anh huong den test ban phim bam nhanh.

Windows: Cai dat → Tro nang → Ban phim. Tat Sticky Keys, Filter Keys, va Toggle Keys. Test lai.

macOS: Cai dat He thong → Tro nang → Ban phim. Tat Sticky Keys va Slow Keys. Nhieu laptop vo tinh bat Slow Keys khi loi tat ho tro nguoi dung (5x Shift) kich hoat.

Driver ban phim ao: mot so ban phim di kem phan mem anh xa lai cua chinh chung (Logitech G HUB, Razer Synapse, ASUS Armoury Crate, Karabiner-Elements tren macOS). Cac driver nay co the chan su kien cho macro. Thoat ung dung phu va test lai; neu phim gio sang, ung dung phu co mot remap chi den noi khac.

Mo rong trinh duyet: Vimium, Surfingkeys, va Tridactyl gan lai phim o cap trang. Tat mo rong trinh duyet hoac dung profile khach.

Cach 4 - Khoa hoac ket noi o cap phan cung

Lop cuoi cung la chinh ban phim. Mot vai nguyen nhan phan cung pho bien lam phim khong bao gio den OS:

Toggle hang Fn: laptop doanh nghiep (ThinkPad, Latitude, EliteBook) di kem voi Fn-Lock chuyen hang F1-F12 giua phim da phuong tien (mac dinh) va phim chuc nang. Bam Fn + Esc (hau het ThinkPad) hoac Fn + Shift de chuyen. Neu phim F khong bao gio sang, day thuong la ly do.

Phim khoa (Caps / Num / Scroll): den LED tren ban phim cho ban biet toggle khoa co bat khong. Test: bat Num Lock ON va bam numpad - cac chu so se sang. Neu khong, numpad ban phim duoc noi day vao mot bo dieu khien USB rieng co the chua duoc liet ke.

Ket noi USB / Bluetooth: ban phim ngoai tren mot day cap khong on dinh co the mat cac phim rieng le. Cam lai day cap vao cong khac; cho Bluetooth, pair lai. Mot ban phim khong day voi pin gan het thuong hien chinh xac trieu chung nay.

BIOS HID tat: BIOS doanh nghiep Lenovo, Dell va HP phoi mot toggle "Ho tro ban phim USB" kiem soat ban phim co hoat dong trong moi truong truoc-OS va tat ca phim co duoc chuyen den OS. Khoi dong lai, vao setup (F1 / F2 / F12), tim Thiet bi → Cai dat USB hoac Bao mat → Truy cap cong I/O → USB, xac nhan da bat.

Van con phim thieu? - luong chan doan

  1. Thu mot trang khac. Mo en.key-test.ru trong cung phien trinh duyet. Neu trang do cung thieu cung cac phim, nguyen nhan o phia tren trang test (focus, layout, tro nang, hoac phan cung).
  2. Thu mot trinh duyet khac. Neu Chrome thieu phim nhung Firefox bat, nguyen nhan o cap trinh duyet (mo rong, profile, hoac listener su kien cu). Dung profile khach de xac nhan.
  3. Thu mot tai khoan nguoi dung khac. Chuyen sang nguoi dung OS moi tao va mo bai test ban phim. Neu hoat dong o do, nguyen nhan trong profile nguoi dung cua ban (remap, co tro nang, hoac driver ban phim ao dang ky cho nguoi dung).
  4. Thu mot may khac. Neu mot dien thoai hoac tablet hien bai test ban phim hoat dong ngay voi mot ban phim Bluetooth da pair, may that bai co van de phan cung hoac driver. Mo Device Manager (Windows) hoac System Information (macOS) va tim ban phim duoi Human Interface Devices.

Moi luot di tu noi trinh duyet bat duoc lan bam ve lai cong tac duoi ngon tay ban - focus truoc, sau do layout, sau do bo loc tro nang, sau do chinh ban phim - va hau het bao cao phim thieu giai quyet trong buoc mot (focus) hoac buoc ba (mot bo loc tro nang ban quen da bat). Neu mot phim van khong sang sau buoc bon, chinh cong tac la nghi pham; doi sang mot ban phim chac chan tot truoc khi gia su may bi hong.

Cac phim modifier (Shift, Ctrl, Alt, Cmd, Win)?

Phim modifier la cac phim "thieu" pho bien nhat trong test ban phim nhanh vi chung chi sang khi ban cung bam mot phim khong-modifier theo chord. Trang test ban phim xu ly moi modifier nhu mot lan bam doc lap: giu Shift (trai hoac phai) va o tuong ung tren bai test se sang - khong can phim khac. Neu chi mot ben sang, cong tac ben kia co the co tiep xuc bi ket - bam ca hai ben va tim kiem hanh vi bat doi xung.

Cac cau hoi thuong gap

Tai sao cung mot phim thinh thoang sang va thinh thoang khong?

Phat hien khong on dinh thuong la van de phan cung - bui hoac mon duoi nap phim. Lay nap phim ra, thoi sach bui voi khi nen, va test lai. Neu van de van con o nguoi dung OS khac (Chan doan Fix 3), phan cung ban phim can dich vu bao hanh.

Caps Lock co anh huong ket qua test ban phim?

Khong. Bai test doc KeyboardEvent.code, giong nhau bat ke trang thai shift / caps. Neu A va a deu sang o A, bai test dang hoat dong dung.

Tai sao phim multimedia (Play, Pause, Volume) khong sang?

Hau het phim multimedia cap OS bi OS hap thu truoc khi trinh duyet thay. Bai test ban phim khong the dang tin cay sang chung. Su vang mat cua chung khong co nghia phim bi hong - bam mot va kiem tra volume OS phan ung.

Toi thay phim dung sang tren bai test, nhung ky tu sai go vao truong van ban. Ban phim co bi hong?

Khong - phan cung ban phim on. Layout ban phim OS cua ban duoc dat sang ngon ngu khac (Cach 2). Bai test xac nhan phim vat ly hoat dong; layout quyet dinh ky tu nao no tao ra.
