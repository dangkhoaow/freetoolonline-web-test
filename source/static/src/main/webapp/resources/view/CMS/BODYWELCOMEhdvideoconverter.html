In-Browser HD Video Converter

Last reviewed: 2026-05-22

Convert HD video files to MP4, WebM, MOV, AVI, or MKV, or pull the audio track to MP3, AAC, OGG, FLAC, M4A, or WAV, all right in your browser. Files stay on your device; the converter runs locally via FFmpeg.wasm with no upload, no install, and no account. For the original FFmpeg online tool on this site, see video-converter.html.

Use this when you would rather not upload a clip to a server, when the network is slow, or when the source contains anything you want to keep local. The dropdown above the file picker lists the output formats; pick one before dropping the file.
