MP4 vs MOV vs MKV - Chon Container Nao Khi Nao

Lan ra soat cuoi 2026-04-27. Dung cong cu chuyen doi video de doi dinh dang trong trinh duyet ma khong can upload tep.

Cau tra loi 30 giay. Container la lop boc xung quanh du lieu video va audio; cac codec (H.264, H.265, VP9, AV1) ben trong moi la nguoi ma hoa thuc su. MP4 phat khap noi - lua chon mac dinh dung khi gui hoac upload. MOV la container cua Apple, gan giong MP4 ben trong, lua chon mac dinh dung cho luong iOS/Mac. MKV danh cho luu tru - mang nhieu track audio, phu de va chuong sach ma MP4 xu ly vung ve. Chuyen doi voi cong cu chuyen doi video.

"Container" nghia la gi vs "codec"

Container giu luong video, luong audio, cac track phu de va metadata trong mot tep. Codec ben trong ma hoa pixel va mau audio thuc su. Tep .mp4 thuong chua video H.264 va audio AAC; tep .mov thuong cung vay; tep .mkv co the chua video H.265, audio FLAC va ba track phu de. Container quyet dinh cai gi co the mang theo; codec quyet dinh cach ma hoa.

Hau het su co phat lai la su co codec, khong phai container. Mot video "khong phat duoc" co le co codec ma trinh phat khong ho tro, du container van on. Mot .mp4 voi video H.265 khong phat duoc tren trinh duyet cu; .mp4 voi H.264 phat khap noi.

Khi nao chon tung loai

MP4. Mac dinh cho bat ky video nao di toi nguoi nhan khong chuyen ky thuat hoac len web. Nhung vao HTML5 video, phat tren moi trinh duyet, phat tren moi dien thoai, phat trong moi ung dung nhan tin. Truong hop 95%.

MOV. Dung khi video o lai trong luong Apple - quay tren iPhone, chinh sua trong Final Cut, giao cho nguoi dung Mac khac. MOV ma hoa ProRes la tieu chuan cho chinh sua video vi codec la intra-frame (moi khung hinh dung mot minh, khong phu thuoc giua cac khung, scrubbing nhanh).

MKV. Dung de luu tru tai lieu nguon voi nhieu track ngon ngu, nhieu track phu de va dau chuong. Cac ban release scene phim lau dung MKV gan nhu doc quyen vi container xu ly moi thu ho can van chuyen. Sai khi gui cho nguoi nhan khong chuyen ky thuat vi hau het ung dung tieu dung khong phat duoc MKV.

Chuyen doi giua chung

Chuyen doi container thuong nhanh va khong mat du lieu vi codec ben trong khong can ma hoa lai. MOV sang MP4 voi cung codec H.264 ben trong mat vai giay va tao ra tep chat luong giong het voi gan nhu cung kich thuoc. MP4 sang MKV cung nhanh tuong tu.

Chuyen doi codec cham va co mat - ma hoa lai tu H.265 sang H.264 nghia la giai ma moi khung hinh va ma hoa lai, mat vai phut cho moi phut video. Tranh chuyen doi codec tru khi noi den yeu cau.

Cong cu chuyen doi video xu ly cac thay doi container thong dung trong trinh duyet. De co bo cuc sau hon xem MP4 vs WebM cho web hoac ffmpeg online vs ffmpeg cuc bo. Cong cu video tai trung tam cong cu video.
