PDF vs HEIC cho luu tru tai lieu - cai nao bao quan tot hon

Cong bo ngay 20 thang 4 nam 2026

Ban chup mot anh cua hoa don, mot mau don chinh phu, hoac mot bang trang voi iPhone - iOS luu duoi dang HEIC. Ban can luu vao mot thu muc chia se, gui qua email cho ai do, hoac giao cho ke toan vao cuoi nam. No nen giu HEIC, hay nen la PDF? Cau tra loi phu thuoc xem luu tru la cho ban, cho mot nguoi la, hay cho mot phan mem. Huong dan nay che do khac biet kich thuoc, thuc te ho tro cong cu, cau chuyen tim kiem/OCR, va ba quyet dinh thuc su cho luu tru dai han.


Moi dinh dang duoc toi uu cho gi

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) la mot dinh dang anh don hoac chuoi anh dua tren dac ta HEIF, dung nen HEVC. iPhone luu anh duoi dang HEIC theo mac dinh tu iOS 11 (2017). Dinh dang duoc thiet ke de luu anh o khoang 50% kich thuoc cua mot JPG tuong duong voi chat luong hinh anh tuong duong, cong them du lieu do sau theo khung, burst, va chuoi anh truc tiep. Day la dinh dang anh.

PDF (Portable Document Format) la mot dinh dang tai lieu dua tren trang. Mot PDF co the chua mot hoac nhieu trang; moi trang co the chua van ban, do hoa vector, anh raster, va van ban co the tim kiem (lop OCR). PDF bao quan ngu nghia tai lieu - ban co the sao chep van ban tu mot PDF, tim kiem trong do, ap dung chu ky so, va che cac vung ma khong can raster lai. Day la dinh dang tai lieu.

Khi HEIC thang

Album anh ca nhan o lai tren thiet bi Apple. iCloud Photos, ung dung Photos, AirDrop giua iPhone va Mac - HEIC la dinh dang ban dia va chuyen sang JPG am tham khi can. Tiet kiem luu tru quan trong tren mot thu vien 20.000 anh ca nhan.

Ban khong bao gio can gui tep cho nguoi dung khong-Apple. Mot tep HEIC mo ban dia tren iOS, iPadOS, va macOS (Big Sur+). Windows 10+ mo HEIC voi cai dat Microsoft HEVC Extension mien phi. Ho tro Linux khong on dinh; he thong doanh nghiep cu thuong tu choi HEIC am tham.

Anh hoan toan la tham khao truc quan. Mot snapshot cua mot san pham, thu cung, hoac diem moc - khong co van ban de tim, khong co chu ky de them, khong co metadata de bao quan ngoai EXIF.


Khi PDF thang

Bat ky cai gi chua van ban ban co the tim sau nay. Hoa don, hoa don ban hang, hop dong, mau chinh phu, don thuoc, danh thiep. Them lop OCR vao PDF nghia la grep qua 10.000 hoa don luu tru tim cai noi "Home Depot 2024" trong 200 mili giay. HEIC khong co lop OCR va khong co duong tim.

Tai lieu da trang. Mot hop dong thue 3 trang nen la mot PDF, khong phai ba tep HEIC rieng. PDF bao quan thu tu trang; HEIC thi khong.

Bat ky cai gi vuot qua bien gioi to chuc. Khi ban gui tep cho ke toan, chu nha, co quan thue, hoac ngan hang, PDF la ngon ngu chung. HEIC yeu cau nguoi nhan cai dat phan mem hoac chuyen doi tep.

Luu tru dai han (10+ nam). PDF/A la tieu chuan ISO (19005-1 den 19005-4) cu the cho PDF luu tru, voi han che dam bao tai lieu render giong het thap ky sau. Khong co tuong duong cho HEIC; HEVC la codec co ban quyen ma cong cu tuong lai khong duoc dam bao.

Chu ky so. Mot PDF da ky (PAdES) co the kiem chung mat ma. HEIC khong co be mat ky.


Ba quyet dinh luu tru thuc su

Quyet dinh 1 - Ky niem ca nhan (sinh nhat, du lich, gia dinh). → HEIC trong Apple Photos, JPG fallback cho chia se. Uu tien hieu qua luu tru va tich hop ung dung Photos. Khi chia se ngoai Apple, iOS cung cap "Copy as JPEG" trong share sheet, hoac dung cong cu HEIC to JPG cua chung toi cho chuyen doi hang loat. Xem huong dan chi tiet ve chuyen doi 100+ anh HEIC cho luong batch.

Quyet dinh 2 - Giay to kinh doanh (hoa don, hop dong, ID). → PDF, luon luon. Chup anh tren iPhone, mo trong Files (nhan giu), cham "Create PDF", roi Markup → "Scan with text" de them lop OCR. PDF ket qua co the tim kiem, chia se, va an toan luu tru. Neu ban da co thu muc hoa don HEIC, chuyen doi chung: cho tep don, ung dung Files iPhone; cho hang tram tep, xem huong dan HEIC hang loat cua chung toi roi dung images to PDF de ket noi.

Quyet dinh 3 - Tai lieu phap ly (hop dong da ky, don kien, gui chinh phu). → PDF, voi chu ky so khi can. Cho bao quan phap ly dai han (giu theo lenh toa, ho so thue), luu duoi dang PDF/A - dinh dang dam bao tai lieu render giong het trong 30 nam ngay ca khi moi cong cu doc quyen tao no da dong cua. HEIC khong duoc chap nhan cho luu tru phap ly o hau het khu vuc tai phan.


Danh doi kich thuoc tep khong nhu ve

"HEIC la mot nua kich thuoc cua JPG" la dung cho mot anh don. PDF goi cung mot anh trong cau truc bo sung (metadata trang, lop OCR, noi dung vector tuy chon), nen mot PDF cua mot anh nguon goc tu HEIC tieu bieu la 1,5× kich thuoc HEIC goc. Nhung luu tru khong phai la ve mot tep don; do la ve toan bo luu tru. Mot thu muc 500 hoa don HEIC roi tieu thu khoang 500 MB va khong the tim kiem. Mot PDF 500 trang voi OCR tieu thu khoang 1 GB va tim moi tu trong khoang 200 ms. Kich thuoc tung tep khong phai la metric dung.


Chuyen doi HEIC sang PDF trong trinh duyet

Cong cu HEIC to JPG cua chung toi xu ly buoc HEIC→anh trong trinh duyet voi giai ma wasm - khong tai len. Ket noi voi images to PDF de xay dung mot PDF luu tru da trang tu mot thu muc tep HEIC. Cho OCR, ung dung Files iOS (Markup → Scan text) xu ly tieng Anh va 20+ ngon ngu khac cuc bo. Cho luong cong viec co kich ban tren 1000+ tep, cai dat Tesseract cuc bo; pipeline trong trinh duyet gioi han khoang 2 GB RAM moi tab.


Quy tac quyet dinh trong mot cau

Neu tep co van ban, chu ky, hoac nguoi nhan khong-Apple, dung PDF. Neu la anh ca nhan o lai trong he sinh thai Apple cua ban, giu HEIC.
