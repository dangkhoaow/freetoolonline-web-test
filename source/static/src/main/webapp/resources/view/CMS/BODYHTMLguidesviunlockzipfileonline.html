"Mo khoa" mot ZIP co the co nghia la hai dieu khac nhau, va cong cu phu hop phu thuoc vao cai ma kho luu tru cua ban can. Mot ZIP duoc bao ve bang mat khau can mat khau duoc loai bo truoc khi mo sach trong he dieu hanh; mot ZIP thuong chi can mot bo trich xuat co the doc tieu de kho luu tru va tai xuong cac tep ben trong.
