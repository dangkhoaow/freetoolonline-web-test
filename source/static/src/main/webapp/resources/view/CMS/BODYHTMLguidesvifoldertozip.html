Khi nao thu-muc-sang-zip thuc su tiet kiem khong gian

Cung mot thu muc cac tep tro thanh mot .zip nho hon chi khi dau vao la nhieu van ban: nhat ky, CSV, JSON, ma nguon, hinh anh khong nen. Buoc DEFLATE ben trong archive loai bo cac doan dai cua cac mau lap lai - ma cac tep van ban thuan co rat nhieu - va do la noi cat giam kich co den. Mot thu muc 200 MB cac xuat CSV thuong nen xuong 30 den 50 MB ben trong mot archive duy nhat (cat giam kich co hai phan ba thuc su). Cac dinh dang da nen (JPG, MP4, MP3, hau het PDF) thuong giu xap xi cung kich co hoac tang vai phan tram tu chi phi dong goi, vi moi tep da co cac mau lap lai duoc loai bo khi camera, codec hoac trinh ghi luu lan dau.

Dinh tuyen mot cu nhap chuot den trinh tao trong trinh duyet

Ba cong viec chia se hub cong cu ZIP va moi cai song cach mot cu nhap chuot. Mo Nen Tep ZIP khi muc tieu la mot archive moi tu mot thu muc cac tep - do la quy trinh lam viec thu-muc-sang-zip ma huong dan nay mo ta. Mo Xoa Mat Khau ZIP khi ban da biet mat khau tren mot archive ma ban nhan duoc va muon mot ban sao khong co yeu cau mat khau. Mo Giai Nen Tep khi cong viec la trich xuat noi dung cua archive ma nguoi gui chuyen tiep. Chon nhan phu hop voi nhiem vu ban den va cong viec bat dau o trang tiep theo.

Thu-muc-sang-zip vs cac nhiem vu lien quan

Neu muc tieu la mot archive duoc bao ve bang mat khau thay vi mot cai don gian, cac dinh tuyen anh chi em la Zip tep voi mat khau (xay dung mot archive moi yeu cau mat khau de mo) va Xoa mat khau ZIP (loai bo mot mat khau da biet tu mot archive hien co). Tinh toan danh doi kich co tren huong dan nay khong thay doi voi lop ma hoa; DEFLATE chay truoc va mat khau goi ket qua sau, vi vay heuristic kha nang nen van chon dau vao nao thuc su se thu nho. Neu tu dong nghia ban danh la "nen ZIP" thay vi "thu muc sang zip", huong dan chi em tai /guides/vi/compress-zip.html bao gom cung cau hoi voi tu vung do; thuat ngu "trinh nen zip" dat o /guides/vi/zip-compressor.html. Ca ba trang dinh tuyen den cung trinh tao trong trinh duyet tai /zip-tools/zip-file.html.

Trinh tao lam gi (va khong lam gi)

Trinh tao trong trinh duyet tai /zip-tools/zip-file.html chap nhan mot thu muc cac tep, cho phep nguoi gui dat ten archive va viet mot .zip tuan thu tieu chuan ma bat ky he dieu hanh hien dai nao giai nen ma khong can cong cu ben thu ba. Dau ra la mot .zip thuan; ZIP64 tu dong bat khi archive vuot qua tran 4 GB cu nen cac thu muc lon hon van tao ra mot archive co the su dung duoc. Trinh tao khong be khoa mat khau khong xac dinh tren cac archive hien co, khong xu ly dau vao RAR, 7z hoac TAR.GZ va khong ma hoa lai cac tep ma no luu tru - cac anh, video va tai lieu ben trong van giong het byte voi nhung gi ban da bo vao.

Cac cau hoi thuong gap

Buoc thu-muc-sang-zip co luon lam archive nho hon khong?

Khong - no phu thuoc vao dau vao. Cac thu muc nhieu van ban (CSV, JSON, ma nguon, nhat ky) thu nho vi DEFLATE co cac doan dai cua cac mau lap lai de loai bo. Cac thu muc nhieu phuong tien (anh JPG, video MP4) thuong van trong vai phan tram kich co dau vao vi moi tep da duoc nen khi duoc luu lan dau. Cat giam kich co dat tren dau vao, khong tren archive.

Cong cu thu-muc-sang-zip thuc su o dau?

Trinh tao trong trinh duyet song tai /zip-tools/zip-file.html. Trang huong dan nay la trang dinh tuyen va boi canh; cu nhap chuot tiep theo la noi archive duoc xay dung. Khong cai dat, khong dang ky, khong chuyen huong ben thu ba.

Neu nguoi nhan khong the mo .zip thi sao?

Tren cai dat Windows hoac macOS tieu chuan, mot archive bi tu choi thuong la mot trong ba thu: tep tai xuong xong giua chung (tai xuong lai mot lan va thu lai), phan mo rong duoc doi ten trong khi tai xuong (doi ten ve .zip truoc khi giai nen), hoac archive duoc xay dung voi ZIP64 va cong cu nhan co truoc ZIP64 (tao lai duoi dang mot archive nho hon chia thanh hai phan). Trinh tao viet ZIP tuan thu tieu chuan ma trinh giai nen tich hop OS xu ly tren moi nen tang duoc ho tro, vi vay nguyen nhan thuong la van chuyen hoac doi ten thay vi dinh dang archive.
