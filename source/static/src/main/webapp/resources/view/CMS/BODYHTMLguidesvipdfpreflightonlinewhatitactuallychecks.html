Preflight thuc su nghia la gi

"Preflight" muon tu kiem tra truoc bay trong hang khong va buoc vao the gioi PDF qua Adobe dau nhung nam 2000, dau tien trong InDesign roi den Acrobat. Day la buoc xac thuc truoc in / truoc xuat ban tao ra mot BAO CAO liet ke cac van de cau truc ma mot tep PDF co so voi mot tieu chuan hoac luong cong viec muc tieu. Cac van de tieu bieu ma kiem tra preflight bao cao: font thieu, font khong nhung, anh RGB trong luong CMYK-only, anh do phan giai thap duoi DPI muc tieu, trong suot khong duoc lam phang, ma hoa (chan tiep nhan trong mot so he thong luu tru), hanh dong JavaScript (pha vo luu tru PDF/A), va loi cau truc PDF.

Preflight la buoc KIEM TRA. No khong KHAC PHUC tep. Doc gia (hoac cong cu phia sau) doc bao cao va hoac la sua tep trong cong cu layout / tac gia phia truoc, hoac chay mot luot khac phuc rieng.

/preflight-pdf.html kiem tra gi thuc su

Cong cu preflight freetoolonline bao cao hai muc tuan thu luu tru cu the: PDF/A-1A (bien the van ban tiep can / tagged) va PDF/A-1B (bien the chi do trung thuc thi giac). PDF/A la dinh dang luu tru ISO 19005 duoc kho luu tru quoc gia, thu vien, va bat ky luong cong viec nao can PDF render giong nhau hang chuc nam sau. Cac quy tac cau truc duoc dinh nghia ro rang:

  • Font phai duoc nhung dang tap con Type 1 / TrueType / OpenType / CFF / Type 3, de tep khong phu thuoc vao kha nang san co cua font tren trinh render tuong lai.
  • Khong trong suot tren trang (phai duoc lam phang thanh pixel da gop truoc).
  • Khong ma hoa (tep phai mo duoc khong mat khau).
  • Khong video nhung / khong JavaScript nhung / khong tham chieu tep ngoai (tep phai tu chua de luu tru).
  • Khong gian mau phai doc lap voi thiet bi (ICC-tagged, khong phai RGB tho hoac CMYK thiet bi khong co output intent).

Upload mot PDF len Preflight PDF, va bao cao cho ban biet quy tac cau truc nao tep cua ban dat hoac khong dat so voi PDF/A-1A va PDF/A-1B.

Cai gi no KHONG kiem tra

Cong cu trung thuc ve pham vi. Danh sach duoi day la cai /preflight-pdf.html KHONG lam, va di dau thay vao do.

  • Khong SUA loi. Preflight la buoc kiem tra. No bao cao van de; khong nhung font thieu, khong lam phang trong suot, khong chuyen RGB sang CMYK. De sua loi, buoc tiep theo dung la xuat lai tu cong cu layout / tac gia nguon voi cai dat da sua, hoac dung cong cu khac phuc offline.
  • Khong lam preflight in PDF/X. PDF/X-1a (chi CMYK, khong trong suot, khong van de mau spot), PDF/X-3 (cho phep RGB co ICC tag), va PDF/X-4 (cho phep trong suot truc tiep) la cac tieu chuan ISO 15930 khac PDF/A va doi hoi phan tich nhay cam CMYK. De tuan thu PDF/X, cong cu dung la dong co preflight day du cua Adobe Acrobat Pro (offline), hoac luong preflight cua xuong in.
  • Khong xac minh tinh hop le phap ly cua chu ky so. Mot chu ky PDF co the dung ve mat mat ma (byte khop voi hash da ky) nhung khong hop le phap ly (chung chi ky khong trong trust store CA cua ban, hoac thoi gian ky ngoai cua so co hieu luc cua chung chi). De xac minh phap ly chu ky, cong cu dung la Adobe Acrobat hoac trinh xac thuc chu ky chinh thuc cua khu vuc phap ly cua ban.
  • Khong sua chua PDF hong. Neu PDF cua ban khong mo (trinh doc PDF noi "tep bi hong" hoac "khong the mo tep nay"), luong byte bi hong va preflight co the se tu choi doc tep hoan toan. Buoc tiep theo dung la cong cu sua chua PDF tai tao bang tham chieu cheo.
  • Khong xac thuc kha nang tiep can PDF/UA. PDF/UA (Universal Accessibility, ISO 14289) la tieu chuan PDF tagged cho tuong thich trinh doc man hinh va cong nghe ho tro. No nghiem ngat hon yeu cau tag cua PDF/A-1A. De xac thuc PDF/UA, mot trinh xac thuc tiep can chuyen biet la cong cu dung.

Luong quyet dinh: tu PDF can kiem tra den cong cu dung

Neu ban co mot PDF truoc mat va khong chac /preflight-pdf.html co phai buoc tiep theo dung khong, luong bon buoc duoi day se dan ban den cong cu dung.

  1. Goi ten cau hoi thuc su. Ban dang xac thuc tuan thu luu tru (PDF/A)? Tuan thu in (PDF/X)? Sua mot tep hong? Khoa truong bieu mau? Xac minh chu ky so? Moi cau hoi dan toi mot cong cu khac.
  2. Ghep cau hoi voi cong cu. Luu tru PDF/A → Preflight PDF. In PDF/X → Adobe Acrobat Pro offline. Sua PDF hong → cong cu tai tao chuyen biet. Khoa truong bieu mau va chu thich vao trang → Flatten PDF. Tinh hop le phap ly chu ky → Adobe Acrobat hoac trinh xac thuc khu vuc.
  3. Thuc hien hanh dong tren cong cu dung. Upload toi /preflight-pdf.html cho luu tru PDF/A; hoac duyet hub rong hon PDF Tools de tim cai phu hop nhat tiep theo.
  4. Doc bao cao va quyet dinh khac phuc. Preflight tao ra mot bao cao. Quyet dinh xem co xuat lai tu cong cu tac gia phia truoc (cach sua thong dung nhat), chay mot luot khac phuc rieng, hoac chap nhan tep nhu khong tuan thu tieu chuan muc tieu.
