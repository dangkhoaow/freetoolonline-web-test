The "ms" and "millis" shorthand also work here. Same tool, same answer. Paste any of those into the box above and click Convert; the tool returns the matching UTC and local date/time. If your input is 10 digits long, it is almost certainly a Unix epoch in seconds, not milliseconds - multiply by 1000 first or use a seconds-to-date converter instead.
