LCD test

An LCD test runs full-screen solid colours - red, green, blue, white, and black - so backlight bleed, stuck pixels, and uneven illumination show against a uniform field within a few seconds. Open the tool, tap each colour swatch to fill the entire screen, and look for any dot, line, or patch that does not match the surrounding colour.

Often called a screen test or display test, this LCD check is the same quick diagnostic: show one solid colour at a time so dead or stuck pixels and backlight bleed stand out against a uniform background.
