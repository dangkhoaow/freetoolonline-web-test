Tai sao "giai nen tep truc tuyen" thuong nghia la mot trong ba thao tac

Cum tu "giai nen tep truc tuyen" la mot cau truy van tim kiem cho ba tac vu-doc-gia khac nhau. Tac vu thu nhat la "toi co mot .zip va toi muon cac tep ben trong" - huong nguoc thong thuong cua zip hoa. Tac vu thu hai la "toi co mot .rar hoac .7z va toi muon cac tep ben trong" - cung muc tieu, container kho luu tru khac. Tac vu thu ba la "toi quen mat khau mot .zip va toi khong the mo no" - khong phai giai nen ma la khoi phuc mat khau. Chon dung cong cu phu thuoc vao ban dang o trong tac vu nao trong ba, va tren trang nay cau tra loi khac nhau theo phan mo rong.

Ly do quan trong: dat mot .rar vao mot cong cu chi chap nhan .zip khong tra ve thong bao loi ma hau het doc gia thay huu ich - no tra ve "loai tep sai" hoac am tham tu choi tai len. Va thoi gian phi pham tai len lai cung tep khong thay doi cau tra loi. Bon quy tac dieu phoi duoi day bao quat cac truong hop thuc te, bao gom phai lam gi khi trang nay khong co bo giai nen cho dinh dang cua ban - vi doc "mo 7-Zip hoac Keka cuc bo" huu ich hon la tiep tuc bam vao mot cong cu khong xu ly dinh dang.

Quy tac dieu phoi 1 - kho luu tru cua ban la .zip: mo Unzip File (server-upload, mat khau tuy chon)

Neu kho luu tru tren dia cua ban co phan mo rong .zip, Unzip File la tuyen. Tha tep vao widget tai len, dich vu AWS freetoolonline giai nen kho luu tru phia may chu, va noi dung quay lai duoi dang mot lan tai ve. Cac dau hieu cu the rang ban dang o truong hop nay:

  • Ban nhan duoc mot .zip nhu mot tep dinh kem email va dien thoai hoac laptop cong viec cua ban khong co cong cu giai nen desktop tien tay. Cong cu trinh duyet tra ve noi dung trong mot vong di-va-ve.
  • .zip chua nhieu tep (mot thu muc du an, mot lo bien lai, mot thu muc anh) va ban muon kiem tra noi dung truoc khi quyet dinh giu gi. Unzip File tra ve cac tep duoc chua; ban luu nhung tep ban muon.
  • .zip duoc bao ve bang mat khau va ban biet mat khau. Unzip File phoi bay mot dau vao mat khau trong cung luong - nhap khi duoc nhac va viec giai nen tien hanh. Huong dan dong hanh cho huong nguoc lai (zip mot thu muc voi mat khau) la Cach zip thu muc truc tuyen tung buoc.

Quy tac dieu phoi 2 - kho luu tru cua ban la .rar hoac .7z: trang nay khong giai nen cac dinh dang do phia may chu

Neu kho luu tru tren dia cua ban co phan mo rong .rar hoac .7z, trang nay khong co bo giai nen phia may chu cho cac dinh dang do. Khong co tuyen /unrar.html hoac /un7z.html, va bo loc tep chap nhan cua Unzip File chi la .zip - tai len mot .rar hoac .7z vao tuyen do khong cho ket qua huu ich. Cau tra loi trung thuc la: mo kho luu tru cuc bo. Cac tuyen cu the theo he dieu hanh:

  • Windows: 7-Zip (mien phi, open-source, xu ly .zip / .rar / .7z), WinRAR (.rar-native, dung thu mien phi), hoac PeaZip (mien phi, ho tro rong cac dinh dang). Phai chuot tep, "Extract to a folder", xong.
  • macOS: Keka (mien phi / tra phi, App Store) hoac The Unarchiver (mien phi, App Store) xu ly .zip / .rar / .7z. Archive Utility tich hop cua macOS mo .zip; cho .rar / .7z ban can mot trong cac app o tren.
  • Linux: 7z x archive.7z (p7zip; apt install p7zip-full) cho .7z, unrar x archive.rar (unrar; apt install unrar) cho .rar.

Ly do: viet "chung toi giai nen RAR truc tuyen" khi trang khong co bo giai nen .rar la loai khang dinh sai pha phi thoi gian doc gia va lam mat long tin. Khi mot tuyen truc tuyen khong ton tai cho mot dinh dang, fallback ve cong cu cuc bo la cau tra loi dung, va huong dan huu ich hon khi noi vay truc tiep. So sanh dong hanh ZIP truc tuyen vs 7z vs RAR - chon cai nao di qua dinh dang nao de chon khi ban kiem soat ben san xuat; huong dan nay danh cho ben tieu thu (ban da co kho luu tru truoc mat).

Quy tac dieu phoi 3 - phan mo rong hon hop hoac khong ro rang: chon theo thuc su tep la gi

Cac truong hop ro rang o tren bao quat hau het cau truy van tim kiem. Cac truong hop hon hop can mot cau hoi them - tep that su la gi? Cac tieu-truong hop cu the:

  • Phan mo rong noi .zip nhung Unzip File tu choi: tep bi gan nhan sai (ai do doi ten mot .rar thanh .zip) hoac .zip bi loi. Luong chan doan o Tuy chon khoi phuc tep ZIP bi hong - san xep ba thung (thuc su hong, gan nhan sai, can mat khau-nhung-nhac-khong-kich-hoat).
  • Tep khong co phan mo rong, hoac co .tar / .gz / .tar.gz / .bz2: trang nay khong co tuyen TAR / GZIP. Fallback ve cong cu cuc bo cua quy tac 2 ap dung - 7-Zip xu ly .tar.gz tren Windows; tar -xzvf archive.tar.gz tren macOS / Linux thao ca hai lop trong mot buoc.
  • Tep la mot .zipx (bien the WinZip): hau het cong cu giai nen desktop xu ly .zipx (7-Zip, WinRAR, Keka) nhung Unzip File cua trang nay chi chap nhan bo loc .zip tieu chuan - thu mot lan; neu tu choi tai len, recourse ve cong cu cuc bo.
  • "Kho luu tru" thuc ra la mot trinh cai dat (.dmg, .pkg, .msi, .exe): nhung cai do khong phai kho luu tru theo nghia tuong tu. Khong tai chung len Unzip File. Mo chung voi trinh cai dat native hoac dung cong cu desktop biet dinh dang cu the.

Ly do: cau truy van tim kiem "giai nen tep truc tuyen" quet vao cac dinh dang kho luu tru ma trang nay khong giai nen. Chon theo thuc su tep la gi (thay vi theo phan mo rong noi) tranh duoc vong tai len-va-that bai. Huong dan dong hanh ZIP vs ZIPx vs RAR vs 7z dinh dang kho luu tru giai thich la bo giai thich dinh dang; huong dan nay la quy tac dieu phoi van hanh.

Quy tac dieu phoi 5 - tep khong lo hoac nhay cam: giai nen cuc bo thay vi tai len

Unzip File tai kho luu tru cua ban len dich vu AWS freetoolonline va viec giai nen chay phia may chu. Day la tuyen dung cho hau het moi tac vu doc-gia cong-cu-trinh-duyet, nhung hai truong hop goi den fallback cong cu cuc bo. Thu nhat, cac kho luu tru rat lon (mot kho luu tru du an nhieu gigabyte, mot backup .zip trong khoang gigabyte) tai len cham va tai ve cham ngay tren ket noi tot - neu ban kiem soat mot may desktop, bo giai nen OS tich hop hoac app cuc bo (quy tac 2) nhanh hon dau-cuoi vi cac byte khong bao gio roi khoi dia cua ban. Thu hai, cac tep ban khong the tai len vi ly do bao mat (hinh anh y khoa, bang chung phap ly, code duoi NDA) can o cuc bo - ngay khi tep nho, cong cu cuc bo la tuyen dung.

Dieu gi xay ra voi kho luu tru cua ban sau khi tai len (va khi nao bo qua tai len)

Khi ban tha mot .zip vao Unzip File, kho luu tru duoc tai len dich vu AWS freetoolonline (vung us-east-1), duoc giai nen phia may chu, va noi dung duoc viet tro lai bucket dau ra cua cung pipeline tai len de trinh duyet cua ban co the tai ve. Kho luu tru va dau ra duoc giai nen khong duoc giu vo han - pipeline tai len chay mot don dep da lap lich xoa tep sau mot cua so luu giu co dinh (cau hinh site-wide; theo bac chuc phut, khong phai gio hoac ngay). Day la cung cua so luu giu ma moi cong cu khac dua tren tai len tren trang nay su dung (Compress Image, Zip File, HEIC to JPG va vv). Hai he qua thuc te cho ban voi tu cach doc gia:

  • Tai ve dau ra truoc khi ban dong tab. Khi cua so don dep di qua, cung upload-id khong con resolve thanh tep co the tai ve nua - neu ban dong tab ma khong luu, ban tai len lai tu ban sao cuc bo cua kho luu tru. Danh dau URL ket qua khong phai giai phap thay the; cua so luu giu ap dung cho tep, khong phai URL.
  • Voi kho luu tru rat lon hoac nhay cam ve bao mat, bo qua tai len hoan toan. Kho luu tru nhieu gigabyte cham hon tai len so voi giai nen cuc bo, va noi dung ban khong the tai len (y khoa, phap ly, NDA) can o dia. Fallback OS cuc bo cua quy tac 2 la tuyen dung trong ca hai truong hop - 7-Zip, Keka, hoac lenh nen tang 7z x / unrar x giai nen kho luu tru ma khong gui cac byte den may chu, va khong co cua so luu giu de lap ke hoach.

Neu tai len bi treo hoac dau ra sai: troubleshoot truoc khi chay lai

Neu Unzip File chap nhan tai len cua ban nhung khong tai ve nao xuat hien trong vai phut, dung tai len lai. Tai len lai cung tep khong sua nguyen nhan goc - no them mot cong viec thu hai dap vao cung buc tuong. Cac treo thong dung la: gioi han kich thuoc dau vao bi dat tren tai len (kho luu tru lon hon tran moi-tep; quy tac 5 ap dung), tab trinh duyet bi lam moi trong pipeline chuyen doi (ket qua tai ve den tab da dong va bi mat), hoac mot dau vao khong-.zip duoc dua cho bo loc chi-.zip (quy tac 1 vs quy tac 2 la lua chon sai - doc lai phan mo rong va dieu phoi cho phu hop).

Neu dau ra co hinh dang sai - vi du .zip da giai nen nhung cac tep ben trong la nhung kho luu tru - day la truong hop kho luu tru long nhau: giai nen .zip ngoai truoc, sau do dieu phoi moi tep noi theo phan mo rong cua chinh no (mot .rar trong mot .zip van la mot .rar va quy tac 2 ap dung). Neu nguoi nhan khong the mo .zip ban san xuat tu huong nguoc lai (zip mot thu muc), ho co the can Unzip File trong trinh duyet cua ho, va neu mot mat khau dang chan duong Remove ZIP Password bao quat luong khoi phuc.
