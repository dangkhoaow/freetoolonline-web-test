Cach Nen Thu Muc Thanh Zip Online, Tung Buoc

Cap nhat lan cuoi 2026-04-27. Mo cong cu zip de nen mot thu muc trong trinh duyet ma khong can tai len.

Cau tra loi trong 30 giay. Tren desktop, keo thu muc vao cong cu zip - trinh duyet di qua cay thu muc va giu nguyen no ben trong tep luu tru. Tren di dong, trinh duyet khong the doc thu muc truc tiep, vi vay giai phap thay the la nhan giu thu muc, chon "compress" (co san trong iOS va Android hien dai), hoac keo thu muc qua trinh quan ly tep. Duong dan desktop nhanh hon va hoat dong tren moi trinh duyet hien dai.

Nam buoc tren desktop

  1. Chon thu muc. Keo thu muc vao vung tha trong cong cu zip. Trinh duyet su dung File System Access API hoac fallback chon thu muc va doc moi tep ben trong, bao gom cac thu muc con long nhau.
  2. Giu cau truc. Cong cu mac dinh giu nguyen bo cuc thu muc ben trong zip. Nguoi nhan giai nen va thay my-folder/sub/file.txt chinh xac nhu ban gui. Khong lam phang tru khi ban thuc su can mot bo cuc phang - cac cong cu giai nen xu ly cau truc long nhau mot cach tu nhien, va metadata khien tep luu tru tu giai thich.
  3. Dat muc nen. Mac dinh (muc 6) phu hop cho noi dung hon hop. Tot nhat (muc 9) giup cho thu muc chu yeu la tai lieu, ma nguon hoac log. Luu tru (muc 0) la lua chon dung cho thu muc anh, video hoac am thanh - moi thu ben trong da duoc nen.
  4. Dat ten tep ket qua. Su dung ten tep mo ta: project-handover-2026-04.zip tot hon archive.zip. Tranh dau cach va ky tu dac biet khi gui qua email - mot so client van ma hoa lai ten tep.
  5. Tai xuong. Tep zip duoc tao cuc bo. Thu muc goc khong bi dong vao.

Giai phap thay the cho di dong

Trinh duyet di dong khong the cap cho mot trang web quyen truy cap truc tiep vao thu muc - chung chi phoi bay tung tep rieng le. Ba con duong hoat dong:

  • Ung dung Files cua iOS. Nhan giu thu muc, chon Compress. Ket qua la mot tep zip o cung vi tri. Mo no trong ung dung bat ky hoac chia se.
  • Android. Hau het cac ung dung quan ly tep (Files by Google, Solid Explorer, MiXplorer) deu co hanh dong Compress cung dang - nhan giu, Compress.
  • Chon nhieu phia trinh duyet. Chon tat ca cac tep ben trong thu muc (tung tep mot tren iOS Safari, chon nhieu tren hau het cac trinh duyet Android) va tha chung vao cong cu zip. Ban mat cau truc long nhau tru khi tao lai thu cong.

Cac cai bay ve kich thuoc can tranh

Mot thu muc voi mot tep khong lo ben trong (video, ban dump co so du lieu) nen lai rat it vi tep do la nut that. Nen hoac cat bot truoc. Mot thu muc voi 20.000 tep nho nen rat nhieu nhung tao ra mot luu tru cham - mot so cong cu giai nen het thoi gian voi luu tru co qua nhieu muc. Neu ban thay mau hinh do, gom cac tep nho vao thu muc con hoac dong goi truoc thanh tar truoc khi zip.

Xac minh zip truoc khi gui

Mo tep zip da tai xuong cuc bo de xac nhan cau truc va moi tep mong doi deu o ben trong. Giai nen mot tep trong trinh duyet se liet ke noi dung ma khong giai nen. De hieu cac danh doi cua dinh dang, xem online zip vs 7z vs rar hoac trung tam cong cu zip.
