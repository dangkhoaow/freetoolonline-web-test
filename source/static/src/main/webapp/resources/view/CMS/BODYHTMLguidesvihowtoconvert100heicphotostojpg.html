Cach chuyen doi 100 anh HEIC sang JPG - mien phi, khong upload

Cong bo ngay 20 thang 4 nam 2026

Ban co anh cua mot chuyen di tu iPhone de chia se voi ai do tren Windows, mot thu muc anh HEIC tu camera ma phan mem chinh sua anh tu choi mo, hoac mot nam hoa don va anh san pham can phai la JPG de luu tru. Tai len 100 tep cho mot bo chuyen doi mien phi cham va thu dich voi quyen rieng tu. Huong dan nay che do quy trinh hang loat dua tren trinh duyet giu moi thu tren thiet bi ban, bao quan EXIF, va xu ly 100 tep trong ~3 phut tren mot laptop hien dai.


Tai sao HEIC -> JPG la nhu cau hang ngay

iOS luu anh duoi dang HEIC theo mac dinh tu 2017. Mac Preview, iOS Photos, va phien ban Windows moi hon (voi HEVC Extension) mo HEIC binh thuong. Nhieu cong cu khac - Windows cu, Linux, hau het bieu mau upload web, nhieu trinh chinh sua anh, gan nhu moi client email cu hon 3 nam - tu choi HEIC am tham hoac chi hien thi xem truoc thumbnail. Chuyen doi sang JPG ne tranh moi khoang cach tuong thich voi chi phi la tep lon hon mot chut.


Quy trinh hang loat trong trinh duyet (khong upload)

Cong cu HEIC to JPG cua chung toi chay giai ma HEIC wasm hoan toan trong tab trinh duyet cua ban. Khong gi duoc tai len; khong gi roi khoi thiet bi cua ban. Quy trinh cho 100 tep:

  1. Chon tat ca tep HEIC trong mot thao tac. Keo va tha mot thu muc, hoac nhap "Chon tep" va chon nhieu voi Shift/Cmd. Cong cu chap nhan toi vai tram tep moi lot tuy thuoc kich thuoc tep trung binh.
  2. Chon chat luong dau ra. Mac dinh (chat luong 85) khop voi diem ngot duoc chap nhan rong rai cua JPG va cat ~10% kich thuoc giai ma tho. Chat luong 95 gan nhu khong mat; chat luong 75 tiet kiem ~40% voi chi phi nho cho chi tiet tinh te.
  3. Bat dau chuyen doi. Cong cu lap qua tep tung cai mot, giu su dung RAM trong gioi han. Thanh tien do hien tep hien tai va tong lot.
  4. Tai ZIP. Tat ca JPG da chuyen doi duoc dong goi vao mot ZIP duy nhat de ban khong phai chap nhan 100 yeu cau tai xuong rieng le.

Thong luong tieu bieu tren mot laptop nam 2023: ~2-3 giay moi tep HEIC 3 MB → 100 tep hoan thanh trong 3-5 phut. Cham hon cho anh chup 48 MP / ProRAW (pixel nguon lon hon de giai ma va ma hoa lai).


Bao quan EXIF va metadata

Metadata EXIF - mau camera, ong kinh, ngay chup, toa do GPS - duoc bao quan theo mac dinh. Cong cu sao chep cac the EXIF lien quan tu tep HEIC vao phan metadata cua JPG dau ra. Neu ban chia se anh cong khai va muon loai bo toa do GPS, tich tuy chon "Bo GPS" truoc khi chuyen doi; phan con lai cua EXIF (ngay chup, camera) duoc giu de sap xep va nhap vao thu vien.

Luu y: metadata dac trung HEIC (ban do do sau, phoi sang theo khung cho Live Photos, chuoi burst) khong the bao quan trong JPG - JPG khong co dinh dang tuong ung. Khung chinh duoc chuyen doi; du lieu phu duoc bo.


Lay tep HEIC tu iPhone truoc

Tep HEIC nguon can den laptop cua ban truoc khi cong cu co the lam viec voi chung. Ba duong:

AirDrop (chi Mac). Chon tat ca anh trong iOS Photos, Chia se → AirDrop sang Mac. iOS se hoi xem gui duoi dang HEIC hoac chuyen doi sang JPG; chon HEIC neu ban muon chat luong goc (cong cu trinh duyet ma hoa lai voi chat luong da chon; chuyen doi JPG hai lan lam mat chi tiet).

iCloud Photos web (moi nen tang). Mo icloud.com/photos trong trinh duyet tren may dich, dang nhap, chon anh, nhap Tai xuong. Tuy thuoc cai dat Mac ("Tai va giu ban goc" vs "Toi uu hoa"), iCloud se dua cho ban ban goc HEIC hoac JPG tu chuyen doi.

Cap USB (Windows dang tin cay nhat). Ket noi iPhone voi PC bang cap USB-C hoac Lightning, tin tuong may tinh, mo thu muc DCIM trong File Explorer. iPhone hien tep HEIC truc tiep. Sao chep chung sang thu muc cuc bo va keo thu muc do vao cong cu trinh duyet.


Khi nao dung chat luong 95 vs 85 vs 75

Chat luong 95 (mac dinh cho luu tru). Dung cho anh gia dinh, cong viec portfolio, hoac bat cu thu gi ban co the in. Tep dau ra la ~1,3× kich thuoc chat luong 85; khac biet truc quan vs khong mat khong cam nhan duoc tren bat ky man hinh nao.

Chat luong 85 (mac dinh cho chia se). Diem ngot lau doi cua web. Giam mot nua kich thuoc tep vs chat luong 95 ma khong co khac biet hien thi cho xem tren man hinh.

Chat luong 75 (mac dinh cho rang buoc email / bieu mau upload). Dung khi he thong nhan co gioi han kich thuoc (gioi han dinh kem email, truong bieu mau web 2 MB). Kich thuoc tep giam ~40% vs chat luong 85; ban se thay mat mat rat tinh te trong cac khu vuc giau chi tiet o zoom day man hinh.


Xu ly su co

"Trinh duyet het bo nho" sau ~40 tep. Dong cac tab khac, tai lai bo chuyen doi, chay trong lot nho hon (50 moi lan). Tab trinh duyet co tran heap; chay nhieu tab bo nho cao cat vao ngan sach cua bo chuyen doi.

"Xem truoc thumbnail hien nhung chuyen doi that bai" tren mot so tep. Thuong la Live Photos noi khung chinh hien dien nhung du lieu phu/do sau bi loi. Xuat lai tu iOS Photos duoi dang "Giu Ban Goc" va thu lai.

Tep hoan tat nhung EXIF bi thieu. Kiem tra "Bao quan EXIF" duoc tich. Mot so phan EXIF la dac trung cong cu; EXIF app Photos nen round-trip sach, nhung EXIF camera ben thu ba thinh thoang bi roi.
