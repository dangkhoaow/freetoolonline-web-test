Khi nao nen zip thuc su giam kich thuoc

Kich thuoc tep .zip tro nen nho hon tong cua cac dau vao chi khi nhung dau vao do that su co the nen duoc. Van ban thuan, log may chu, CSV, JSON, ma nguon va bitmap chua nen thuong giam xuong khoang 30 den 60 phan tram kich thuoc goc khi duoc goi vao mot kho luu tru, vi cac byte lap lai co the duoc nen boi thuat toan DEFLATE ben trong ZIP. Anh JPG, video MP4, am thanh MP3 va hau het PDF da duoc nen boi cac codec rieng cua chung, do do vo ZIP chi bao boc cac tep do voi thay doi kich thuoc khoang 0 den 5 phan tram va doi khi tang nhe do tai phi cua viec dong goi. Biet ban dang doi voi phia nao truoc khi nen la su khac biet giua "kho luu tru nay co kich thuoc bang mot nua ban dau" va "kho luu tru nay lon hon mot chut so voi thu muc goc".

Cach nen zip trong trinh duyet, tung buoc

  1. Mo /zip-file.html trong mot tab moi.
  2. Keo hoac chon cac tep ban muon goi. Mot thu muc cung duoc chap nhan; cac thu muc con duoc giu lai trong kho luu tru.
  3. Tuy chon: mo bang Settings, dat ten kho luu tru va them mat khau cung phuong phap ma hoa (Tieu chuan, AES-128 hoac AES-256) neu nguoi nhan da co kenh rieng cho mat khau.
  4. Nhan nut tao kho luu tru va doi vai giay. Lien ket tai xuong xuat hien khi kho luu tru san sang.

Mau giao hang theo giai doan khi kich thuoc la rang buoc

Khi muc tieu cuoi cung la dap ung gioi han dinh kem email 20 MB hoac 25 MB, mau phan chia goi la cach don gian nhat. Goi cac dau vao van ban (log, CSV, ma nguon, tai lieu Office) thanh mot kho luu tru thuc su giam kich thuoc, de phuong tien da nen (anh, video, am thanh) lam dinh kem rieng hoac tai len mot lien ket chia se. Hai nua cua viec giao hang vuot qua kiem tra kich thuoc rieng biet ngay ca khi mot ZIP ket hop khong qua. Cong cu tao tai /zip-file.html hien thi kich thuoc kho luu tru cuoi cung truoc khi lien ket tai xuong xuat hien, do do nguoi gui thay con so truoc khi may chu email cua nguoi nhan co co hoi tu choi tin nhan.
