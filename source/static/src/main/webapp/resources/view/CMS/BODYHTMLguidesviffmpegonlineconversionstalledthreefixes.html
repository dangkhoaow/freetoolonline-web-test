Chuyen Doi FFmpeg Truc Tuyen Dung? Ba Cach Khac Phuc (va Test 30 Giay)

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 2026-05-03. Ban tai video len trinh chuyen ffmpeg truc tuyen, nhan "convert," va thanh tien do dong cung - hoac khong bao gio bat dau di chuyen. Huong dan nay neu ten ba nguyen nhan pho bien (gioi han bo nho trinh duyet, tab nen, codec build WASM khong ho tro), dua ra cach khac phuc mot dong moi nguyen nhan, va cho thay test 30 giay cho biet ban gap cai nao.

Dap an 30 giay. Trinh duyet het bo nho vi tep qua lon cho WASM trong tab, HOAC tab bi nen va he dieu hanh dinh chi worker, HOAC nguon dung codec ma build WASM ffmpeg khong bao gom. Tai lai tab va tha tep nho test (vi du clip 5 giay 720p) vao cong cu ffmpeg truc tuyen. Neu clip nho chuyen doi trong vai giay, su co ban dau la Nguyen nhan 1 (bo nho) hoac Nguyen nhan 3 (codec). Neu ca clip nho cung dung, do la Nguyen nhan 2 (tab nen).

Nguyen nhan 1: gioi han bo nho trinh duyet tren WASM ffmpeg

WASM ffmpeg trong trinh duyet la mot ffmpeg tu dung duoc bien dich sang WebAssembly chay hoan toan trong tab. Danh doi: module WASM cua tab thuong nhan 2 GB khong gian dia chi (4 GB voi build memory64 ro rang), va mot video nguon 4K cong voi buffer bo ma hoa dang bay va mang chuyen doi khong gian mau co the vuot qua do tren clip dai. Khi dieu do xay ra, ffmpeg hoac chet am tham hoac dung o mot phan tram khong bao gio tien len. Tep nguon 4K dai la yeu to kich hoat pho bien nhat, nhung nguon 1080p H.265 dai voi am thanh 5.1 cung co the day gioi han tren dien thoai va laptop cu.

Khac phuc. Cat nguon thanh clip ngan hon truoc, sau do chuyen doi. Hoac chay ffmpeg cuc bo cho tep vuot khoang 500 MB - binary ffmpeg desktop co truy cap toan bo RAM he thong, khong chi sandbox 2 GB. Huong dan dong hanh chu dong FFmpeg truc tuyen vs FFmpeg cuc bo - khi nao moi cai thang di qua quyet dinh kich thuoc va runtime truoc khi tai len, de ban co the chon duong dung ngay lan thu dau.

Nguyen nhan 2: tab bi nen; he dieu hanh dinh chi worker

Trinh duyet hien dai (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) dieu tiet hoac hoan toan dinh chi Web Worker trong cac tab nen de tiet kiem pin. Chuyen doi ffmpeg dai chay khi tab bi an co the dong cung vo thoi han tren desktop va gan nhu luon dong cung tren di dong (iOS Safari dac biet hung hang). Chuyen doi khong thuc su hong - he dieu hanh da tam dung no - nhung voi doc gia no trong giong het dung cung.

Khac phuc. Giu tab o tien canh trong suot thoi gian chuyen doi. Tren laptop, dung de nap dong (ngu cung tam dung worker). Tren dien thoai, dung chuyen ung dung; khoa man hinh voi tab van trong tieu diem neu phai, nhung tab nen khi man hinh bat van thuong bi dieu tiet. Mot so cong cu ffmpeg truc tuyen ghi nhan thu thuat "keep-alive" (vi du vong lap am thanh im lang, hoac nhip `noSleep` dinh ky) - doc ghi chu cua tac gia cong cu neu chuyen doi du dai de can.

Cach nhanh nhat de xac nhan Nguyen nhan 2 la test tep nho tu dap an 30 giay o tren: neu clip 5 giay cung dung khi tab bi nen, nhung chuyen doi ngay khi ban dua tab vao tien canh, nguyen nhan la dinh chi tab. Neu ngay ca clip nho tien canh cung dung, van de o noi khac (Nguyen nhan 1 bo nho hoac Nguyen nhan 3 codec).

Nguyen nhan 3: tep nguon dung codec ma build WASM khong bao gom

Browser WASM ffmpeg ship mot bo decoder duoc tuyen chon de giu kich thuoc tai xuong co the quan ly - thuong la H.264, H.265 (HEVC), VP9, AV1, AAC, MP3, Opus, va mot so it khac. Codec nguon la (ProRes, DNxHR, JPEG 2000, AC-3 tren 5.1 kenh, Dolby Atmos trong mot so container MOV) co the khong giai ma. Chuyen doi hoac that bai ngay voi dong "codec not found" trong log, hoac dung o 0% vi decoder khong the keo mot khung tu nguon.

Khac phuc. Ma hoa lai nguon thanh codec pho bien cuc bo truoc - ngay ca luot ffmpeg desktop nhanh sao chep am thanh va ma hoa lai video sang H.264 chi mat mot phan thoi gian ghi goc. Hoac chon container ma build duoc biet la ho tro; huong dan MP4 vs MOV vs MKV - container nao, khi nao bao gom cac ket hop container/codec nao an toan nhat cho ffmpeg dua-tren-trinh-duyet, va MP4 vs WebM cho web bao gom container dau ra dung cho phan phoi web.

Kiem tra dung 30 giay: clip nho o tien canh

Ba buoc cho biet bo nho, tab bi nen, hay codec da lam dung chuyen doi cua ban - khong can toan hoc, chi can clip nho va tab tien canh:

  1. Tai lai tab (lap module WASM va xoa bat ky trang thai zombie nao tu lan thu truoc).
  2. Tha tep rat nho - clip 5 giay 720p, ~5 MB. Giu tab o tien canh.
  3. Xem.
    • Clip nho chuyen doi trong vai giay → su co ban dau la Nguyen nhan 1 (bo nho) neu nguon cua ban lon, hoac Nguyen nhan 3 (codec) neu codec nguon la. Cat hoac ma hoa lai nguon.
    • Clip nho dung o tien canh → van de la Nguyen nhan 3 (codec) tren ca tep nho, hoac xung dot trinh duyet/tien ich sau hon (thu cua so rieng tu khong co tien ich).
    • Clip nho chuyen doi o tien canh nhung dung khi ban chuyen tab → Nguyen nhan 2 (tab bi nen). Giu tab nhin thay trong suot chuyen doi.

Neu clip nho thanh cong va nguon goc la 4K hoac dai hon 10 phut, Nguyen nhan 1 (bo nho) la kha nang cao nhat; cat nguon thanh manh ~5 phut hoac chay ffmpeg cuc bo.

Cau hoi thuong gap

Safari co ho tro WASM ffmpeg khong?

Co, Safari gan day ho tro ket hop WebAssembly + Web Worker ma build ffmpeg trinh duyet su dung, nhung Safari nghiem khac hon Chrome ve dieu tiet tab nen va gioi han bo nho trong duyet rieng tu. Neu chuyen doi cua ban dung trong Safari nhung hoat dong trong Chrome tren cung may, nguyen nhan thuong la Nguyen nhan 2 (tab bi nen) hoac ap luc bo nho trong che do rieng tu.

Tai sao cung tep chuyen doi cuc bo trong 10 giay nhung dung truc tuyen?

FFmpeg cuc bo dung ma may ban dia va RAM day du cua host; ffmpeg trinh duyet dung WASM (thuong cham hon moi lenh) va sandbox 2 GB. Cho tep vua thoai mai trong sandbox, khoang cach la khoang 2-5x cham hon trong trinh duyet. Cho tep tiem can hoac vuot gioi han sandbox, chuyen doi cuc bo hoan thanh va chuyen doi trinh duyet het bo nho va dung. Huong dan chu dong FFmpeg truc tuyen vs FFmpeg cuc bo bao gom nguong kich thuoc noi moi cai thang.

Toi co the tang gioi han bo nho trinh duyet khong?

Khong tu phia trang. Gioi han duoc dat boi engine trinh duyet va cau hinh bo nho cua build WASM. Cho trinh duyet dua-tren-Chromium, gioi han thuong la 2 GB moi tab; mot so co thu nghiem cho phep pool lon hon, nhung khong than thien nguoi dung. Cach khac phuc dang tin cay la cat nguon de tap lam viec vua.

Gioi han kich thuoc cho chuyen doi truc tuyen la gi?

Cu the theo cong cu. Hau het cong cu browser-ffmpeg ghi nhan gioi han mem giua 100 MB va 2 GB. Tren 500 MB, mong doi dung thuong xuyen tren dien thoai va laptop cu; tren 1 GB, mong doi dung tren hau het desktop cung vay. Neu nguon la 4K va dai hon 10 phut, len ke hoach hoac cat hoac chay cuc bo.

Incognito / Duyet Rieng tu lam te hon khong?

Doi khi. Cua so rieng tu trong Chrome va Firefox mang cung gioi han bo nho, nhung tien ich thuong bi tat (co the giup neu tien ich dang can thiep). Duyet Rieng tu cua Safari co gioi han luu tru va bo nho nghiem hon co the kich hoat Nguyen nhan 1 som hon. Neu chuyen doi dung trong Rieng tu nhung hoat dong trong duyet thuong, nguyen nhan thuong la sandbox nghiem hon trong che do Rieng tu.
