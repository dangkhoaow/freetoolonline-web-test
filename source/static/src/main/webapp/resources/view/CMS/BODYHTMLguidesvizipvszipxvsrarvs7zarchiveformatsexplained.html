Zip vs Zipx vs Rar vs 7z, Giai Thich

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 2026-04-27. Mo cong cu zip de tao kho luu tru tuong thich pho quat trong trinh duyet cua ban.

Cau tra loi 30 giay. Bon dinh dang, bon ky nguyen nen. Zip (1989, Deflate) mo o moi noi ma khong can cai dat. Zipx (2008, container cua WinZip cho cac thuat toan moi hon) chi mo voi WinZip hoac cac cong cu tuong thich. Rar (1993, doc quyen) chu yeu duoc nhan, hiem khi tao moi. 7z (1999, LZMA2) nen nho nhat nhung can tai xuong. De gui: dung zip tru khi ca hai ben co thu gi do tot hon duoc cai dat.

Moi cai dung de lam gi

Zip. Mac dinh pho quat. Duoc tich hop trong moi he dieu hanh va dien thoai tu 2010. Dung cho bat cu cai gi gui den nguoi nhan khong-ky-thuat.

7z. Open-source, hien dai, nho nhat. Lua chon dung cho luu tru dai han va cho bat ky chuyen giao lon nao noi ca hai ben deu co 7-Zip hoac The Unarchiver duoc cai dat.
