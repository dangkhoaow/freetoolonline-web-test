Cac Loai Noi Dung Ma QR: URL vs vCard vs Wi-Fi vs Van Ban - Chon Loai Nao

Cap nhat lan cuoi 2026-05-06. Nham vao thoi diem ban ngoi xuong tao ma QR dau tien va trinh tao hoi "loai payload nao?". Dieu huong viec tao thuc su sang QR Code Generator (trong trinh duyet, khong tai len).

Cau tra loi trong 30 giay. Bon loai noi dung bao tru gan nhu moi ma QR trong thuc te: URL (mot lien ket web - pho bien nhat), vCard (mot the lien lac yeu cau trinh quet luu mot lien lac), Wi-Fi (mot thong tin dang nhap mang yeu cau trinh quet ket noi), va van ban thuan (mot ghi chu ngan hien thi nhu van ban). Ung dung trinh quet tren moi dien thoai doc mot tien to dac biet o dau payload (https://, BEGIN:VCARD, WIFI:) va chuyen doi hanh vi tuong ung. Chon theo dieu ban muon trinh quet lam: mo mot trang (URL), luu mot lien lac (vCard), ket noi mot mang (Wi-Fi), hoac chi hien thi mot it van ban.

Cai gi thuc su o ben trong mot ma QR?

Mot ma QR, ve co hoc, la mot hinh vuong cua cac module den va trang ma hoa mot chuoi byte. Dac ta QR (ISO/IEC 18004) khong dinh nghia chuoi nao de dat vao - no chi dinh nghia cach ma hoa bat ky chuoi nao thanh mau truc quan. "Loai noi dung" cua mot ma QR thuan tuy la mot quy uoc duoc thoa thuan giua cac trinh tao QR va ung dung trinh quet ve tien to ma chuoi bat dau. Khi mot trinh quet doc https://example.com, no thay tien to https:// va xu ly payload nhu mot URL; khi no doc BEGIN:VCARD, no xu ly payload nhu mot the lien lac. Chinh ma QR khong "biet" loai; tien to noi cho trinh quet.

Ba noi dinh nghia cac quy uoc tien to trong thuc te. Thu nhat, cac luoc do URI duoc dang ky boi RFC (http, https, mailto, tel, geo, sms) la cac chuan Internet chinh thuc ma trinh quet tin cay khong hoi. Thu hai, RFC 6350 vCard 4.0 (va RFC 2426 vCard 3.0 cu hon) dinh nghia dinh dang van ban the lien lac - neu payload QR bat dau bang BEGIN:VCARD, no theo dinh dang do. Thu ba, wiki cua du an ZXing - trinh giai ma QR ma nguon mo chinh tac duoc dung trong nhieu ung dung trinh quet - tai lieu mot tap nho cac tien to thuc te khong co dac ta IETF chinh thuc nhung duoc thuc hien rong rai; quan trong nhat la luoc do URI WIFI: ma iOS Camera, Android Camera va Google Lens deu hieu nhu mot yeu cau ket noi mang. Hieu nguon nao trong ba nguon ung ho loai noi dung ban chon cho biet no se quet dang tin cay nhu the nao tren cac dien thoai tu cac nha san xuat khac nhau.

Loai 1: URL - loai pho bien nhat

Mot ma QR URL la payload don gian nhat: cac ky tu ten cua URL, bao gom tien to https://. Vi du, https://freetoolonline.com/utility-tools/qr-code-generator.html duoc ma hoa truc tiep. Moi ung dung trinh quet tren moi dien thoai hien dai nhan ra dieu nay va cung cap mo URL trong trinh duyet mac dinh; nhieu cai cung tu dong mo khi camera duoc tro vao ma tu loi nhac "viewfinder" ma nguoi dung khong cham ro rang.

Tien to https:// quan trong: payload chi co freetoolonline.com ma khong co luoc do la ve ky thuat la mot payload van ban thuan, va hau het trinh quet hien thi no nhu van ban thay vi cung cap mo. Luon bao gom luoc do. Voi cac ma ngan hon, https:// cung dai hon mot chut so voi http:// o muc byte, nhung su khac biet la mot byte va trinh quet hien dai yeu cau https:// de canh bao bao mat hanh xu dung - khong bao gio bo s de tiet kiem mot byte.

Khi nao chon URL. Bang bia ban hoi nghi mo trang dich su kien; thuc don nha hang mo thuc don truc tuyen; bao bi san pham mo video huong dan; to roi mo mau dang ky; chu ky email mo portfolio cua ban. Bat ky kich ban nao ma hanh dong mong muon la "mo trang web nay trong trinh duyet dien thoai" la mot ma QR URL.

Loai 2: vCard - cho the lien lac

Mot ma QR vCard ma hoa mot the lien lac trong dinh dang vCard 4.0 RFC 6350 (hoac RFC 2426 vCard 3.0 cu hon; ca hai van dang duoc su dung tich cuc). Payload bat dau bang BEGIN:VCARD va ket thuc bang END:VCARD, voi cac truong ten, dien thoai, email, to chuc va URL o giua. Mot vCard 3.0 toi thieu trong nhu sau:

BEGIN:VCARD
VERSION:3.0
N:Smith;Jane
FN:Jane Smith
ORG:Example Corp
TEL:+1-555-0100
EMAIL:jane@example.com
URL:https://example.com/jane
END:VCARD

Khi trinh quet doc dieu nay, no cung cap luu lien lac - thuong la bang cach mo ung dung lien lac cua dien thoai duoc dien truoc voi cac truong, san sang xac nhan. iOS Camera, Android Camera, Google Lens va hau het cac ung dung trinh quet QR ben thu ba deu ho tro payload vCard.

Danh doi la do dai payload: mot vCard hoan chinh voi ten, dien thoai, email, to chuc, dia chi va URL thuong la 200-350 byte, tao ra mot ma QR day ma kho quet hon o kich thuoc in nho. Hai bien phap giam thieu thuc te: (a) giu vCard toi thieu (ten, dien thoai don, email don - bo dia chi va to chuc neu chung khong thiet yeu), va (b) in ma QR voi kich thuoc it nhat 3 cm x 3 cm de mau hinh day van de doc o khoang cach quet thong thuong. Huong dan dong hanh ve kich thuoc payload tai QR Code Error Correction and Scan Failures bao tru danh doi kich thuoc-vs-payload chi tiet.

MECARD: lua chon thay the vCard nho hon. Mot dinh dang cu hon goi la MECARD (xuat phat tu NTT DoCoMo cho dien thoai feature Nhat Ban) su dung tien to MECARD: va cu phap truong gon hon: MECARD:N:Smith,Jane;TEL:5550100;EMAIL:jane@example.com;;. Payload MECARD thuong ngan hon 30-40% so voi vCard tuong duong, tao ra mot ma thua hon, de quet hon. Hau het trinh quet hien dai (iOS Camera, Android Camera, Google Lens, ung dung dua tren ZXing) nhan ra MECARD; trinh quet cu hon co the khong. Neu khan gia cua ban la toan cau va chu yeu tren dien thoai phien ban hien tai on dinh, MECARD la lua chon tot khi kich thuoc in bi gioi han; neu khong, vCard 3.0 la mac dinh an toan hon.

Khi nao chon vCard hoac MECARD. Danh thiep voi mot QR "quet de luu lien lac cua toi"; day deo o mot hoi nghi; phu hieu nhan vien su kien cho phep nguoi tham du lay lien lac cua to chuc; to roi bat dong san in voi lien lac cua dai ly duoc dien truoc. Bat ky kich ban nao ma hanh dong mong muon la "luu chi tiet lien lac cua nguoi nay vao dien thoai" la mot ma QR vCard hoac MECARD.

Loai 3: Wi-Fi - de ket noi mot mang

Mot ma QR Wi-Fi ma hoa thong tin dang nhap mang yeu cau trinh quet ket noi mang. Dinh dang duoc tai lieu trong wiki cua du an ZXing va duoc thuc hien tu nhien boi iOS Camera (tu iOS 11), Android Camera (tu Android 10 tren Pixel; thay doi tren cac nha san xuat khac) va Google Lens. Cu phap payload la:

WIFI:S:<ssid>;T:<auth_type>;P:<password>;H:<hidden>;;

Trong do S: la ten mang (SSID), T: la loai xac thuc (WPA cho WPA / WPA2 / WPA3, WEP cho WEP cu, nopass cho mang mo), P: la mat khau (bo cho mang mo), va H:true danh dau mang la an (hau het mang bo qua truong nay). Mot ma Wi-Fi quan cafe dien hinh trong nhu sau: WIFI:S:CafeGuest;T:WPA;P:welcome2026;;. Luu y dau cham phay doi cuoi - no ket thuc payload.

Dinh dang Wi-Fi khong co RFC IETF chinh thuc; no la mot tieu chuan thuc te duoc tai lieu trong wiki ZXing va duoc thuc hien boi cac ung dung camera chinh. Dieu nay co nghia la tren iPhone hoac Pixel hien tai on dinh ma "chi hoat dong", nhung tren dien thoai cu hon (pre-iOS 11; pre-Android 10 tren hau het cac nha san xuat khong phai Pixel) payload co the hien thi nhu van ban tho thay vi kich hoat loi nhac ket noi mang. Voi khan gia hon hop, in SSID va mat khau nhu van ban thuan ben duoi ma QR de nguoi dung dien thoai cu hon van co the ket noi thu cong.

Ky tu dac biet trong mat khau. Neu SSID hoac mat khau chua mot ky tu ten ;, :, ,, ", hoac \, cac ky tu do phai duoc thoat bang dau gach cheo nguoc trong payload (\\;, \\:, vv.). Hau het trinh tao QR xu ly dieu nay tu dong, nhung neu ban dang chinh sua payload thu cong do la nguon pho bien nhat cua bao cao "ma da tao nhung dien thoai cua toi tu choi ket noi".

Khi nao chon Wi-Fi. Mang khach quan cafe tren the ban; goi chao mung khong gian coworking; to roi green-room hoi nghi; danh sach Airbnb chia se truoc ma trong thong diep chao mung. Bat ky kich ban nao ma hanh dong mong muon la "cho khach truy cap ket noi Wi-Fi cua toi ma khong can go mat khau" la mot ma QR Wi-Fi.

Loai 4: Van ban thuan - cho ghi chu ngan

Mot ma QR van ban thuan la cac ky tu ten cua van ban, khong co tien to dac biet. Khi mot trinh quet doc no, van ban duoc hien thi trong bang "noi dung da quet" va nguoi dung co the sao chep no sang clipboard. Khong co tu dong mo, khong co tu dong luu, khong co tu dong ket noi - chi van ban tren man hinh.

Van ban thuan la lua chon dung khi khong loai nao khac phu hop: mot mat khau Wi-Fi noi ban khong muon tu dong ket noi (ban muon nguoi dung sao chep va dan no ho minh), mot so seri ngan tren mot thiet bi, mot ma ngay su kien, mot dia chi Bitcoin, mot ma phieu giam gia. Danh doi la tren hau het trinh quet van ban thuan nhan duoc UI it ma sat nhat - nguoi dung phai doc van ban va quyet dinh phai lam gi voi no - dieu nay chinh xac la dung cho mot so payload (ma phieu) va chinh xac la sai cho cai khac (URL 150 ky tu se tot hon dang ma QR URL).

Khi nao chon van ban thuan. Ma phieu tren to roi in; so seri tren thiet bi; mat khau Wi-Fi hoi nghi noi SSID duoc thong bao to va QR chi giu mat khau; dia chi tien dien tu; ghi chu ngan ("hoi quay le tan xac nhan dau xe"). Bat ky kich ban nao ma hanh dong mong muon la "nguoi dung doc van ban va go thu cong o noi khac" la mot ma QR van ban thuan.

Cac loai noi dung khac dang biet

Ngoai bon loai chinh, mot so tien to it pho bien hon co ho tro trinh quet pho quat va co the la su phu hop dung cho cac kich ban hep:

  • Email (mailto:) - mo ung dung email mac dinh voi dia chi va chu de/than tuy chon duoc dien truoc. Dinh dang: mailto:hello@example.com?subject=Inquiry&body=From%20conference%20booth. Cac ky tu dat truoc trong chu de va than phai duoc ma hoa phan tram theo RFC 3986 (ma hoa URL).
  • Dien thoai (tel:) - mo trinh quay so voi so duoc nhap truoc (nguoi dung van nhan Goi). Dinh dang: tel:+15551234567. Luon bao gom ma quoc gia voi + o dau.
  • SMS (smsto: hoac sms:) - mo ung dung tin nhan voi nguoi nhan va than tuy chon duoc dien truoc. Dinh dang: smsto:+15551234567:Hello,%20I%20saw%20your%20flyer hoac sms:+15551234567?body=Hello. Hai dinh dang cung ton tai; smsto: cu hon nhung van duoc ho tro rong rai.
  • Vi tri dia ly (geo:) - mo ung dung ban do mac dinh tai vi do/kinh do cung cap. Dinh dang: geo:48.8566,2.3522?q=Notre+Dame+Paris. Nhan ?q= la tuy chon; khong co no ung dung chi hien thi mot ghim.
  • Su kien lich (BEGIN:VEVENT tu iCalendar RFC 5545) - yeu cau trinh quet them mot su kien vao lich. Dinh dang bat dau bang BEGIN:VEVENT va ket thuc bang END:VEVENT. Duoc ho tro pho quat it hon vCard - kiem tra tren dien thoai cua khan gia muc tieu truoc khi in o quy mo.

Quyet dinh: loai nao cho kich ban nao

Neu ban biet dieu ban muon trinh quet lam, loai thuong la ro rang. Anh xa:

  • Mo mot trang web trong trinh duyet → URL.
  • Luu mot lien lac vao dien thoai → vCard (hoac MECARD neu kich thuoc in nho).
  • Ket noi mot mang Wi-Fi → Wi-Fi. In SSID va mat khau nhu van ban thuan ben canh cho dien thoai cu hon.
  • Hien thi mot ma hoac ghi chu cho nguoi dung sao chep → van ban thuan.
  • Soan mot email → mailto:. Dien truoc chu de va than de ro rang.
  • Goi mot cuoc dien thoai → tel:. Luon bao gom ma quoc gia quoc te.
  • Gui mot SMS duoc dien truoc → smsto:.
  • Mo mot ban do tai mot vi tri → geo:.
  • Them mot su kien lich → VEVENT (kiem tra ho tro trinh quet truoc).

Khi ban phan van giua hai lua chon, hay chon cai co payload ngan hon - payload ngan hon tao ra cac ma thua hon, dang tin cay hon. Mot URL ngan chuyen huong den mot URL dai hau nhu luon dang tin cay hon viec ma hoa URL dai truc tiep; chuyen huong mang theo cac tham so theo doi o ben may chu.

Kich thuoc payload, sua loi, va danh doi do tin cay quet

Ma QR co payload toi da co dinh o moi kich thuoc; payload cang dai, mau cang day, va kich thuoc in ban can de giu no quet duoc cang lon. Theo huong dan tho: mot URL 25 ky tu tao ra mot ma thua quet tu mot met xa tren camera dien thoai; mot vCard 200 ky tu tao ra mot ma day can it nhat 3 cm x 3 cm va duoc quet tu khong qua 30 cm. Huong dan chuyen biet tai QR Code Error Correction and Scan Failures bao tru bon muc sua loi (L / M / Q / H) va danh doi kich thuoc-vs-payload, voi khuyen nghi kich thuoc in cu the cho moi kich ban.

Hai mac dinh thuc te hoat dong cho gan nhu moi kich ban in: muc sua loi M (~15% kha nang chiu dung hu hong) cho su dung trong nha va muc Q (~25%) cho ngoai troi hoac be mat bi mai mon; kich thuoc toi thieu 2 cm x 2 cm cho payload ngan (URL duoi 50 ky tu), 3 cm x 3 cm cho payload trung binh (vCard, Wi-Fi), va 4 cm x 4 cm cho URL dai tren 100 ky tu. Bat ky kich thuoc nho hon va mot phan dang ke dien thoai se khong quet duoc.

Quyen rieng tu: ma QR ma hoa cai gi va khong ma hoa cai gi

Mot ma QR chi ma hoa chuoi payload ten. Khong co dia chi IP, khong co ID thiet bi, khong co dau thoi gian, khong co vi tri, khong co du lieu phan tich an trong mau - ma chinh xac la cai ban dat vao, khong gi hon. Tao mot ma QR cuc bo trong trinh duyet (do la dieu QR Code Generator tren trang nay lam) giu payload hoan toan tren thiet bi cua ban; khong co gi ve ma QR cua ban duoc gui den bat ky may chu nao trong qua trinh tao.

Neu ban ma hoa mot URL tro den mot trinh theo doi phan tich ben may chu hoac mot URL co tham so UTM, phan tich xay ra o dau nhan khi trinh quet mo URL - chinh ma QR chi la byte tinh. Ma Wi-Fi mang theo mat khau ten; doi xu voi cac ma Wi-Fi in tuong tu nhu cach ban doi xu voi mat khau van ban thuan (khong de chung o cac noi co the chup anh cong khai neu mang nhay cam).
