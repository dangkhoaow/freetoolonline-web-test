Video maker assembles a sequence of images (and optional audio) into a single MP4 video directly in your browser.

  1. Upload the images you want in the slideshow.
  2. Set the per-image duration and toggle audio on or off.
  3. Click Make video and download the resulting MP4.

Note: The number of images controls the encoding time; expect a few seconds per batch on a modern laptop.
