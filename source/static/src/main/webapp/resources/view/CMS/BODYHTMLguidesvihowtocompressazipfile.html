Cau hoi cua doc gia"cach nen tep zip" - cong thuc tung buoc

Ba buoc

  1. Mo trinh tao ZIP trong trinh duyet tai /zip-file.html. Keo thu muc (hoac cac tep roi) vao khe tai len.
  2. Chon muc tieu nen. .zip thong thuong la mac dinh; neu ben nhan chap nhan cac kho luu tru duoc bao ve bang mat khau, hay dung zip voi mat khau thay vi do. Cong cu dong goi muc tieu duoc chon inline.
  3. Tai ve .zip khi thanh tien trinh hoan tat. Kho luu tru la tep ban tai len, dinh kem hoac chia se.

Khi nao nen ZIP thuc su tiet kiem byte

Cau tra loi trung thuc ngay tu dau: dong goi ZIP thu nho dau vao co the nen (van ban thuan, ma nguon, CSV, JSON, hinh anh khong nen) mot cach ro rang; no khong thu nho phuong tien da nen (JPG, MP4, MP3, PDF) qua vai phan tram, va overhead cua dong goi ZIP tham chi co the lam kho luu tru lon hon mot chut so voi tong dau vao trong truong hop do. Neu muc tieu la mot kho luu tru nho hon cua cac tep phuong tien, loi byte den tu ma hoa lai phuong tien truoc - huong dan lien quan Nen ZIP - khi nao tiet kiem khong gian di qua quy tac quyet dinh. Neu muc tieu la mot kich thuoc muc tieu cu the (duoi 25 MB cho email, duoi 100 KB cho mot bieu mau), huong dan anh chi em Cach nen tep ZIP den mot kich thuoc cu the bao quat su danh doi chat luong-vs-kich thuoc.

Cau hoi thuong gap

Tep co duoc tai len mot may chu khong?

Pipeline tai len tai /zip-file.html chay server-side - cong cu trong trinh duyet gui cac tep den dich vu xu ly qua HTTPS, kho luu tru duoc ghi o do, va lien ket tai ve duoc gui tro lai trang. Cac tep duoc xoa khoi dich vu xu ly sau mot khoang luu giu ngan. Hop dong tai len chia se cho moi cong cu server-side duoc ghi lai tren moi trang cong cu.

Kich thuoc tep toi da toi co the nen la bao nhieu?

Gioi han kich thuoc moi lan tai len duoc dat trong bootstrap cua trang (gia tri duoc render vao trang cong cu khi tai). Voi mot kho luu tru thong thuong than thien voi email, gioi han thuc te la tran dinh kem cua hop thu nhan (Gmail 25 MB, Outlook va hop thu doanh nghiep thuong 10-20 MB). Voi mot kho luu tru lon hon, hay chia se qua lien ket o dam may thay vi dinh kem email.

Toi nen dung ZIP hay 7z hay RAR?

ZIP la lua chon an toan nhat de chia se da nen tang vi moi he dieu hanh chinh co the mo .zip ma khong can cai dat phan mem them. 7z nen chat hon tren mot so dau vao nhung ben nhan co the can trinh giai nen. RAR hiem khi la lua chon dung cho chia se an danh vi no khong mo duoc theo mac dinh tren hau het he thong. Huong dan quyet dinh ZIP truc tuyen vs 7z vs RAR - chon cai nao bao quat su danh doi chi tiet hon.
