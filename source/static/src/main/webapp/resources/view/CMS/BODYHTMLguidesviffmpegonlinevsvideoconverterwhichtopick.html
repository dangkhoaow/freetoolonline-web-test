Quy tac dinh tuyen 1 - cai dat mac dinh, dinh dang pho bien: mo tuyen duong dac thu dinh dang

Neu nhiem vu cua ban la mot trong nhung dieu nay va ban muon mac dinh cua bo ma hoa, mo cong cu dac thu dinh dang va bo qua FFmpeg Online:

Tha .mov vao, nhap chuyen doi, tai xuong .mp4. Codec mac dinh la dung cho gan nhu moi nguoi doc huong dan nay; ban khong can danh -c:v libx264.
  • MP4 to WebM cho web: Video Converter voi WebM la dinh dang dau ra. Cai dat mac dinh tao ra mot tep ma cac trinh duyet chinh (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari 14.1+) deu phat lai inline. Cau hoi lua chon dinh dang duoc bao boc trong MP4 vs WebM cho web.
  • MOV to MP3 trich xuat am thanh: /mov-to-mp3.html giai quyet thong qua Video Converter. Codec am thanh mac dinh la MP3 o toc do bit tieu chuan cua bo ma hoa; neu ban chi co giong noi, do la du.
  • Hoan doi container voi cung codec (MKV to MP4, AVI to MP4): hub Video Converter. Bo ma hoa sao chep luong video hien co khi cac codec tuong thich, vi vay viec chuyen doi nhanh va khong mat.

Ly do: cac tuyen duong dac thu dinh dang chon truoc bo ma hoa, codec va container de ban khong danh bat ky co nao. Moi cu nhap chuot anh xa den mot lenh FFmpeg duoc tuyen chon tren backend. Neu mac dinh khong khop voi nhung gi ban can, nhay den quy tac 2.

Quy tac dinh tuyen 2 - co khong mac dinh hoac codec hiem: mo FFmpeg Online

Mo FFmpeg Online khi ban can bat ky dieu nao sau day, vi tuyen duong dac thu dinh dang khong phoi bay chung nhu menu tha xuong:

  • Toc do bit cu the (vi du 1500 kbps cho 1080p, 800 kbps cho 720p, 192 kbps cho am thanh podcast). Co la -b:v 1500k hoac -b:a 192k.
  • Do phan giai hoac toc do khung hinh cu the (vi du thay doi kich thuoc thanh 1280x720, doi sang 30 fps tu nguon toc do bien doi). Co la -vf scale=1280:720 va -r 30.
  • Ma hoa hai lan cho muc tieu kich co tep chat hon, hoac dieu chinh he so toc do co dinh (-crf 23).
  • Cat mot clip theo thoi gian bat dau va thoi luong ma khong ma hoa lai (-ss 00:01:30 -t 00:00:15 -c copy).
  • Trich xuat mot khung hinh nhu JPG hoac PNG (-ss 00:00:05 -frames:v 1 output.jpg) - tuyen duong dac thu dinh dang khong co cai dat trich xuat mot khung; dong lenh cua FFmpeg Online co.
  • Chay ffprobe de doc metadata cua luong tu mot tep khong xac dinh. Cac tuyen duong dac thu dinh dang khong phoi bay ffprobe; FFmpeg Online thi co.
  • Downmix am thanh tu 5.1 sang stereo (-ac 2) hoac thay doi toc do mau (-ar 44100).
  • Codec hoac container hiem: bat cu thu gi ben ngoai menu tha xuong dac thu dinh dang - dau vao WMV, dau ra OGG, dau ra ProRes, DNxHR, v.v.

Ly do: FFmpeg Online chuyen tiep dong lenh ban danh vao cho cung bo ma hoa ma cac tuyen duong dac thu dinh dang su dung, nhung khong chon truoc bat ky co nao - bat cu thu gi ban danh la nhung gi chay. Do la cach duy nhat de co duoc ma hoa khong mac dinh ma khong tu cai dat FFmpeg cuc bo.
