The "i love zip" search query covers the everyday in-browser zip workflow: a sender has a folder or a stack of attachments and wants one archive that the recipient can drop into the email or chat without installing a desktop archiver. The creator at /zip-tools/zip-file.html handles that build via a private upload-then-build service, with the source files auto-deleted from the service shortly after the .zip is downloaded back to the browser.
