Last updated: Nov 13, 2024

JPEG images are one of the most common file formats for photos and graphics, but they can be large, which affects loading times on websites or sharing over the internet. This online JPEG compressor, powered by AI technology, allows you to reduce the file size of your JPEG images while maintaining high quality. Whether you need to compress jpg files for your website, email attachments, or social media, this tool provides an easy and effective way to optimize images.

Compress JPEG online reduces JPG file size for faster sharing and smaller uploads while keeping your photo looking good.

  1. Upload your JPEG image.
  2. Choose a quality level (or enable AI auto-compression).
  3. Click Compress, then download the optimized JPG.

Tip: If you need a specific size, try a couple of quality levels and compare before downloading.

Upload your image, select the compression level, and download your optimized image - without sacrificing quality.

Pick the format before you compress. Read JPG vs PNG for the web for the byte-count rule: photos almost always compress smaller as JPG; UI screenshots, logos, and anything with sharp edges or transparency almost always stay sharper (and often smaller) as PNG.
