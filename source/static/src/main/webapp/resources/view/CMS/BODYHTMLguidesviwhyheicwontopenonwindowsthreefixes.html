Tai Sao HEIC Khong Mo Tren Windows - Ba Cach Khac Phuc Nhanh

Lan ra soat cuoi 2026-05-04. Ban be AirDrop hoac gui email cho ban mot anh tu iPhone, ban nhap dup vao tep .heic tren PC Windows, va Windows tra loi "Windows khong mo duoc tep nay" hoac don gian hien icon chung va tu choi xem truoc. Ly do don gian: HEIC la dinh dang anh mac dinh tren iPhone tu iOS 11 (2017), nhung Windows khong di kem codec can thiet de giai ma no. Huong dan nay di qua ba cach khac phuc bao gom gan nhu moi bao cao "HEIC khong mo duoc tren Windows" - cai codec, dung trinh xem mien phi tu ben thu ba, hoac chuyen tep sang JPG online voi cong cu mien phi HEIC to JPG - va noi cho ban biet cach nao thang trong tinh huong nao.

Quyet dinh 30 giay. Neu ban chi can xem MOT anh va khong muon cai gi het, chuyen doi online voi HEIC to JPG - upload, tai JPG ve, xong. Neu ban thuong xuyen nhan anh HEIC tu ban iPhone va muon chung "chay binh thuong" trong ung dung Photos cua Windows, cai HEIF Image Extensions mien phi tu Microsoft Store (Cach 1). Neu ban khong co quyen admin hoac khong cai duoc tien ich Store tren PC cong viec bi quan ly, dung trinh xem ben thu ba dang portable nhu IrfanView voi plugin HEIF (Cach 2). Ba cach phu cho truong hop mot lan, dinh ky, va PC bi khoa tuong ung; bang so sanh gan cuoi trang nay anh xa moi truong hop sang lua chon dung.

Tai sao chuyen nay xay ra

Apple chuyen dinh dang anh mac dinh tren iPhone tu JPG sang HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) bat dau tu iOS 11 nam 2017. Loi ich chinh la kich thuoc tep giam khoang mot nua voi cung chat luong hinh anh, dieu nay quan trong khi iPhone luu hang chuc nghin anh. Danh doi la ho tro nen tang: HEIC dua vao bo giai ma HEVC (H.265), ma Microsoft cap phep rieng va trong lich su khong di kem voi Windows. Cai dat sach cua Windows 10 hoac Windows 11 vi vay biet tep .heic la gi - File Explorer hien duoi - nhung khong giai ma duoc pixel ben trong, nen ung dung Photos hien icon placeholder hoac loi.

Tin tot la day la van de codec, khong phai van de phan cung. Sau khi codec duoc cai (hoac sau khi tep duoc chuyen sang dinh dang Windows da hieu, nhu JPG), moi ung dung Windows dung stack hinh anh he thong - Photos, hinh thu nho File Explorer, Word, PowerPoint, file dinh kem Outlook, keo tha trinh duyet - hien anh khong can cau hinh them. Co nhieu duong di den "co codec san", va duong nao dung phu thuoc ban dung Windows 10 hay 11, ban co quyen admin khong, va ban can dieu nay cho mot anh hay cho luong anh nguon iPhone lien tuc. Xem cung guide lien quan ve dinh dang anh iPhone giai thich (HEIC, JPG, PNG, RAW) cho mat dinh dang.

Cach 1 - cai HEIF Image Extensions tren Windows 10 / 11

Duong chinh thuc la cai HEIF Image Extensions cua Microsoft tu Microsoft Store. Cai nay them bo giai ma anh HEIF vao stack imaging Windows de ung dung Photos, File Explorer, va cac thanh phan tieu thu anh he thong khac co the render tep .heic inline. Tien ich nay mien phi; tren hau het ban cai Windows 11, tien ich video HEVC duoc gui rieng cung da duoc cai, nen duong anh chay tu dau khi tien ich HEIF duoc them. Tren Windows 10, ban co the can them goi HEVC Video Extensions tu cung Store (cai nay hien co phi mot lan nho tu Microsoft cho giay phep codec video) truoc khi anh HEIC render dung.

Cac buoc:

  • Mo ung dung Microsoft Store tu menu Start.
  • Tim HEIF Image Extensions (nha xuat ban: Microsoft Corporation).
  • Nhap Get va de cho cai. Tai xuong nho (duoi vai megabyte) va khong can khoi dong lai.
  • Nhap chuot phai vao tep .heic - Open with - Photos. Anh gio nen render inline.
  • Neu anh van khong render tren Windows 10, cai them HEVC Video Extensions tu Store (luu y: goi nay co phi; ban "Device Manufacturer" mien phi chi co tren PC ma nha san xuat da cap phep truoc).

Cach nay la cau tra loi dung cho bat ky nguoi dung PC tai nha thuong xuyen nhan anh tu ban iPhone va muon ung dung Photos va hinh thu nho File Explorer "chay binh thuong" tu day. KHONG hoat dong tren PC cong viec bi khoa noi truy cap Microsoft Store bi chan boi group policy - neu Store bi mo cho ban, nhay sang Cach 2 hoac Cach 3.

Cach 2 - dung trinh xem mien phi ben thu ba (CopyTrans HEIC, IrfanView voi plugin HEIF)

Neu ban khong cai duoc tien ich Store - PC cong viec bi khoa, khong co quyen admin, may tinh truong hoc, kiosk, phien ban Windows cu hon khong co Store - trinh xem ben thu ba ne stack codec he thong hoan toan. Hai lua chon mien phi co uy tin:

  • CopyTrans HEIC cho Windows. Mot tai xuong mien phi nho tu CopyTrans them ho tro hinh thu nho va xem truoc HEIC vao Windows Explorer va Windows Photo Viewer tich hop. Sau khi cai, nhap dup vao tep .heic mo no trong Photo Viewer giong nhu JPG. Can quyen admin de cai (la tien ich shell muc he thong), nen khong giai quyet truong hop PC cong viec bi khoa.
  • IrfanView voi goi plugin HEIF. IrfanView la trinh xem anh mien phi lau doi cho Windows ho tro che do portable (khong installer, chay tu USB stick hoac mot folder trong home directory cua ban). Tai IrfanView va goi plugin tuong ung tu trang chinh thuc irfanview.com, tha vao folder, va mo tep .heic qua UI IrfanView. Che do portable hoat dong tren PC bi khoa vi no khong ghi vao folder he thong hay registry Windows.

Danh doi: trinh xem ben thu ba chi sua hien thi HEIC trong UI rieng cua chung. Ung dung Photos cua Windows, hinh thu nho File Explorer, Word, va PowerPoint van se thay .heic nhu dinh dang khong xac dinh tru khi ban cung cai Cach 1. Cho "toi chi can xem anh nay ngay" tren PC noi ban khong cai duoc codec muc he thong, IrfanView portable la cau tra loi sach se nhat.

Cach 3 - chuyen HEIC sang JPG online cho anh mot lan

Neu ban khong muon cai gi het, duong nhanh nhat la chuyen tep HEIC sang JPG mot lan va sau do mo JPG voi ung dung Photos, Paint, hay bat ky cong cu Windows nao luon xu ly duoc JPG. Cong cu mien phi HEIC to JPG chay chuyen doi o phia server: ban upload tep .heic, chuyen doi chay tren dich vu AWS cua freetoolonline, va ban tai JPG ve. Tep goc duoc xoa khoi luu tru dich vu sau cua so luu giu chuan, nen ban nen tai va luu JPG cuc bo neu muon giu.

Day la cach dung khi:

  • Ban chi can xem (hoac chia se cho ban khong dung iPhone, hoac dinh kem email) MOT anh, khong phai luong anh.
  • Ban dang tren PC bi khoa noi khong cai duoc codec hoac trinh xem ben thu ba, nhung co the dung trinh duyet web.
  • Ban muon anh o JPG du nao vi dich (mot website, CMS, tiem in) khong nhan HEIC.
  • Ban can chia se anh voi ai do co thiet bi khong ho tro HEIC (dien thoai Android cu hon, desktop Linux, phien ban macOS cu hon).

Neu ban co folder voi 50+ anh HEIC de chuyen mot lan, xem Cach chuyen 100 anh HEIC sang JPG cho luong batch - cung cong cu /heic-to-jpg.html xu ly dau vao batch. Cho mot anh hay vai cai, luong upload-download don gian la duong nhanh nhat.

FAQ

Goi HEIF Image Extensions co mien phi tren Windows 11 khong? Co - tien ich anh tu no la mien phi trong Microsoft Store. Tren Windows 10, goi HEVC Video Extensions tuong ung (can cho mot so tep HEIC) hien co phi mot lan nho tu Microsoft cho giay phep codec video, mac du nhieu PC Windows 10 ma nha san xuat cap phep truoc HEVC tu dong nhan build "Device Manufacturer" mien phi.

Co the doi ten .heic thanh .jpg va Windows se mo duoc khong? Khong. Duoi tep khong thay doi noi dung thuc cua tep - mot tep HEIC doi ten thanh photo.jpg van la tep HEIC, va Windows van khong giai ma duoc khong co codec. Doi ten se danh lua File Explorer thu mo tep voi handler JPG, nhung handler se that bai vi byte ben trong khong phai JPG. Dung Cach 3 (chuyen doi thuc) thay vao do.

Chuyen sang JPG co mat chat luong khong? Co, mot chut. HEIC luu anh voi nen HEVC; JPG dung codec cu hon, kem hieu qua hon. Chuyen vong tu HEIC sang JPG tao ra mat chat luong chi nhin thay duoc khi xem pixel-by-pixel cho hau het anh. De xem, chia se, va in, mat la khong dang ke. Neu ban can chat luong HEIC goc, giu tep HEIC ben canh JPG - chuyen doi tao tep moi va khong sua nguon. Xem HEIC vs JPG vs WebP cho danh doi dinh dang ben canh nhau.

Cai dat "Most Compatible" tu phia iPhone co sua chuyen nay tu goc khong? Co - tren iPhone, Cai dat - Camera - Formats - Most Compatible chuyen anh moi sang JPG thay vi HEIC. Cai nay sua anh tuong lai tu chinh iPhone do nhung khong sua anh HEIC da chup. Cho anh HEIC hien co trong Camera Roll, chuyen doi (Cach 3) la duong duy nhat.

Huong dan nay KHONG phai cai gi

Hai tac vu doc gia lien quan duoc co tinh dat ngoai pham vi:

  • "Cach mo HEIC tren macOS / Linux / Android?" macOS mo HEIC nguyen ban (la dinh dang cua Apple). Phan phoi Linux khac nhau - hau het ban desktop hien dai di kem libheif va co the xem truoc HEIC trong trinh quan ly tep, nhung ban cu hon khong. Android 10+ ho tro HEIC nguyen ban; Android cu hon can ung dung gallery ben thu ba. Huong dan nay co pham vi Windows.
  • "Tep HEIC cua toi mo nhung trong sai (xoay, mau nhat, thieu trong suot)." Do la chan doan khac - codec hoat dong nhung dau ra khong khop voi cai iPhone hien. Metadata huong sensor, ho mau gamut rong, va du lieu chuyen dong Live Photo la nghi pham thuong gap; huong dan tuong lai se che do.
