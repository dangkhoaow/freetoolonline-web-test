FAQ

Is this a dead pixel test, a screen test, or a display test?
All three. The tool cycles solid colours full-screen so dead pixels, stuck pixels, and backlight bleed become visible regardless of whether you searched for "lcd test", "screen test", "display test", "panel test", or "monitor test".

Does it work on OLED and LED screens, not just LCD?
Yes. The full-screen colour cycle (red, green, blue, black, white, yellow) reveals dead and stuck pixels on LCD, OLED, and LED panels alike. Backlight-bleed checks apply to LCD panels specifically — OLED and LED panels show the same test as uniform-emission checks instead.

Why a full-screen colour cycle rather than a single colour?
A dead pixel stays black on every colour; a stuck pixel stays one colour while the rest of the screen changes. Cycling red → green → blue → black → white → yellow surfaces both defect types in under thirty seconds. The black and white frames also reveal backlight bleed at the edges of LCD panels.
