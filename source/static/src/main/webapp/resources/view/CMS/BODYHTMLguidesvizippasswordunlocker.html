Cong cu mo khoa mat khau ZIP: khi nao mot cong cu online co the xoa mat khau (va khi nao no khong the be khoa)

"Cong cu mo khoa mat khau ZIP" tach ro thanh hai tac vu nguoi doc rat khac nhau. Tac vu mot: ban co mot file .zip VA ban biet mat khau (ban sao luu cua chinh ban, ban giao du an, ban gui cua dong nghiep), va ban muon mot ban sao cua archive khong co lop mat khau de file mo ma khong can go lai. Tac vu hai: ban co mot file .zip va ban KHONG biet mat khau, va ban muon mot dich vu online khoi phuc no. Tac vu mot la cai ma cong cu trong trinh duyet tai /remove-zip-password.html lam. Tac vu hai khong giai duoc boi bat ky cong cu online trung thuc nao, du quang cao the nao. Trang nay giai thich ca hai truong hop, chi den click tiep theo dung cho cai dau tien, va neu ten cac tuy chon thuc te cho cai thu hai.

Cong cu mo khoa tren site nay thuc su lam gi

Cong cu Remove ZIP Password tai /remove-zip-password.html la cong cu "boc lop mat khau khoi mot archive ma ban da kiem soat", khong phai trinh doan. Ban tai .zip len, go mat khau vao o mat khau, click Remove, va trang gui ca hai den dich vu xu ly ngan han qua HTTPS. Dich vu mo archive bang mat khau ban cung cap, dong goi lai cung danh sach file khong co mat khau, va gui ve link tai xuong cho ban sao da mo khoa. Neu mat khau ban go khong khop voi cai archive duoc xay, trang hien thi "Couldn't unlock this ZIP. The password you entered did not match. Try again, or scroll to the FAQ for what to do next." thay vi am tham tao file hong. File .zip goc tren may ban khong bao gio thay doi; trang khong bao gio luu mat khau o noi nao co tinh persistent.

Neu ban quen mat khau nhung archive la cua ban

Ba buoc tiep theo thuc te theo thu tu xac suat thanh cong. Mot, thu cac mau ban hay tai su dung: ten du an, mot nam, mat khau-mac-dinh-vut-di cua ban, ten nguoi nhan. Nguoi ta huong toi cung nam hoac sau mau khi nen vot, nen doan mat khau cua ban thuong la viec go nam mat khau hon la chay mat ma hoc. Hai, neu archive den tu mot dong nghiep hoac nha cung cap, hoi nguoi gui - cuoc tro chuyen do thuong hoan thanh trong duoi nam phut va danh bai moi cong cu. Ba, neu archive la ban sao luu cua file ban va ban goc van con, dong goi lai vao mot archive moi tai trinh tao ZIP trong trinh duyet; ban khong can khoi phuc mat khau cu neu ban co the xay lai archive tu file nguon. Luu y: cong cu trong trinh duyet KHONG su dung vu luc, KHONG doan, va KHONG khoi phuc mat khau khong biet - day la thiet ke, khong phai tinh nang thieu.

Neu ban khong biet mat khau va chua bao gio biet

Day la truong hop ma bat ky cong cu nao duoc quang cao la "cong cu mo khoa mat khau ZIP hoat dong ma khong can biet mat khau" deu dang noi qua kha nang. Ma hoa ZIP hien dai (AES-256 trong WinZip / 7-Zip, hoac ngay ca ZipCrypto cu hon) duoc xay sao cho khong co mat khau, loi duy nhat den noi dung la doan moi ung vien chong lai khoi xac thuc cua archive. Voi mat khau tam ky tu hon hop chu hoa-thuong, khong gian tim kiem la khoang bay muoi nghin ty ung vien; voi mat khau manh muoi hai ky tu khong gian vao sextillion. Khong dich vu online nao co the gia bo brute-force dieu do tren file da upload. Cai ma cac san pham khoi phuc mat khau that su (offline, tra phi) lam la chay tan cong kieu tu dien chong lai cac danh sach mat khau co kha nang tren may cua chinh nguoi dung, dieu nay trung thuc nhung cham va chi thanh cong khi mat khau yeu hoac theo cong thuc. Neu noi dung quan trong, do la buoc tiep theo thuc te; dung dan archive rieng tu vao mot "cong cu mo khoa" online khong xac dinh hua hen ket qua nhanh.
