When to use this microphone test

Use this microphone test when you are about to join a video call, record a voice memo, or stream and you want a quick check that your mic is wired up and your input device is the right one. Press Start Microphone Capture, allow the browser microphone permission, and the page draws a live waveform from your input. If the waveform moves when you speak, your mic is working; if it stays flat, the OS-level device picker is on the wrong source. Tick Play sound from your microphone to also hear yourself through the speakers - useful for confirming the input level. The whole check runs in your browser using FileReader, canvas, and a blob URL on the client-side path. Free, no installs.
