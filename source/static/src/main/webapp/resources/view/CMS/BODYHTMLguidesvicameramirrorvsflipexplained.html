Mirror vs flip: cac tu co nghia khac nhau

Hai phep bien doi khac nhau de nham lan, va hau het app tieu dung (va ngay ca mot so bai ho tro) dung cac tu thay doi cho nhau. Biet cai nao dang xay ra voi hinh anh thay doi chan doan:

  • Mirror (lat trai-phai) dao hinh anh theo chieu ngang. Ben phai canh xuat hien o ben trai man hinh, va bat ky chu nao trong khung doc nguoc. Day la dieu ma hau het xem truoc webcam lam mac dinh.
  • Flip (xoay doc / 180°) lat hinh anh nguoc dau. Dinh canh xuat hien o day man hinh. Dieu nay hiem trong xem truoc webcam va hau nhu luon cho thay cai dat huong camera sai tren webcam USB duoc gan nguoc dau.
  • Xoay (90° hoac 270°) nghieng hinh anh sang mot ben. Dieu nay pho bien nhat khi camera dien thoai duoc giu o ngang nhung he dieu hanh doc huong EXIF nhu doc. Gan nhu khong bao gio xuat hien trong xem truoc webcam may tinh de ban.

Neu xem truoc cua ban hien thi chu dao nguoc nhung phan con lai cua canh dung dung, ban dang thay mirror, khong phai flip hay xoay. Cach sua va loi giai thich khac nhau cho moi cai.

Tai sao xem truoc camera mirror (va tep luu thuong khong)

UI xem truoc webcam mirror feed truc tiep vi do la cach guong vat ly hoat dong. Khi ban gio tay phai len, ban mong thay "tay phai" o ben phai hinh anh guong. Khong co bien doi mirror, xem truoc truc tiep se hien thi tay phai cua ban o ben trai man hinh, cam giac sai - ban se phai nghi tay nao di chuyen moi lan chinh toc, dieu chinh goc camera, hay gio mot vat.

Ghi la khac. Toan bo diem cua video ghi la chup canh nhu that, khong phai nhu nguoi noi thay chinh minh trong guong. Vay tep ghi duoc luu khong mirror. Bat ky ai sau do phat lai tep deu thay nguoi noi nhu nguoi thu ba se thay ho ngoai doi, bao gom bat ky chu nao tren ao hay bien trong khung. Su phan chia nay (mirror trong xem truoc, khong mirror khi luu) nhat quan trong OBS, Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, app Camera macOS, va app Camera Windows.

Hai chi tiet trien khai co dinh hanh vi:

  1. Xem truoc truc tiep la bien doi CSS, khong phai chinh sua khung. Hau het UI xem truoc webcam ap dung transform: scaleX(-1) tren phan tu <video>. Cac byte tu camera la khong mirror; trinh duyet hay app chi ve chung lat tren man hinh. Cong cu /camera-test.html tren trang nay dung cung phuong phap - scaleX(-1) o xem truoc truc tiep, khung luu vao canvas la huong goc.
  2. Tep da luu doc khung khong bien doi. Khi ban nhap ghi, OBS / Zoom / Teams / Meet doc truc tiep tu buffer khung camera (ben khong mirror) va ghi do vao tep. Bien doi CSS xem truoc khong bao gio cham cac byte da luu. Do la ly do xem truoc chu nguoc cua ban tro thanh chu dung khi phat lai.

Bang chung 30 giay: gio trang in va ghi ba giay

Neu ban muon chac chan ve setup cu the cua ban truoc thuyet trinh hay ghi, chay quy trinh bang chung nay. Mat khoang 30 giay va tra loi cau hoi dut khoat:

  1. Mo bat ky cong cu nao cho phep ghi (app camera tren Windows / Mac, OBS, Zoom o che do ghi, hay bat ky trinh ghi nao trong trinh duyet). Trang Test Camera tot cho nua xem truoc truc tiep cua kiem tra; no khong ghi, nhung no cho ban biet xem truoc trong nhu the nao.
  2. Gio trang in hay mot manh bia voi chu dam truoc camera. Chu nen chiem it nhat 30% khung de de doc.
  3. Nhin xem truoc. Neu chu nguoc, xem truoc duoc mirror (day la mac dinh; dung hoang).
  4. Nhap ghi. Chup ba giay. Dung.
  5. Phat lai tep da ghi. Neu chu trong phat lai doc dung (xuoi), duong luu cua ban khong mirror va tep da luu on de chia se. Neu chu trong phat lai CUNG doc nguoc, encoder cua ban duoc cau hinh de ghi stream mirror - khong pho bien, nhung co xay ra trong mot so setup OBS tuy chinh.

Neu buoc 5 hien chu dung chieu, ban co the bo qua xem truoc mirror hoan toan - nguoi xem thay canh nhu ban mong. Neu buoc 5 hien chu nguoc, nhay den phan OBS duoi day; do la app pho bien duy nhat cho phep nguoi dung vo tinh ghi xem truoc mirror.

Khi mirror giup (va khi gay hai)

Mirror la mac dinh vi ly do. Do la lua chon dung trong hau het tinh huong, nhung mot so luong pho bien huong loi tu viec tat no:

  • Mirror giup khi ban chinh khung, sua toc, gio mot vat nho ban muon nguoi xem thay, hay bat ky tac vu "nhin minh trong guong" nao. Truc giac tay-phai-ben-phai lam xem truoc de dung hon.
  • Mirror gay hai khi ban doc tu giay nho dan canh camera (chu xuat hien nguoc trong xem truoc, lam kho doc), trinh bay dieu ma trai-phai quan trong (thu cong hay ky hieu tay), hay tu ghi minh viet tren bang trang trong suot cho huong dan (khan gia mong chu doc dung cho ho).
  • Mirror la tuy chon cho goi video binh thuong. Cac thanh vien khac luon thay stream khong mirror; toggle mirror cuc bo chi anh huong den cai ban thay tren man hinh cua ban. Chon cai cam thay thoai mai hon.

Cau hoi thuong gap

Tai sao ban ghi Zoom cua toi hien toi voi tay phai o ben sai?

Hau nhu luon, xem truoc Zoom cuc bo cua ban duoc mirror (vay tay phai xuat hien o ben phai cua man hinh cua ban) nhung ban ghi luu stream khong mirror (vay khi phat lai tay phai cua ban o ben trai, noi no thuc su o so voi camera). Day la ban ghi hien thi thuc te, khong phai Zoom lat cai gi. Neu mat phuong huong, hoac tat "Mirror my video" trong Cai dat Zoom → Video de xem truc tiep khop ban ghi, hoac chap nhan rang ban ghi la goc nhin nguoi thu ba.

Nguoi khac trong cuoc goi video co thay toi dao nguoc neu xem truoc cua toi mirror khong?

Khong. Zoom, Google Meet, va Microsoft Teams luon gui stream camera khong mirror cho cac thanh vien khac bat ke toggle mirror cuc bo cua ban. Toggle chi thay doi cai BAN thay tren MAN HINH cua ban. Cac thanh vien khac thay ban cung cach nguoi dung truoc ban se thay ban ngoai doi.

Toi da tat mirror trong Zoom nhung ban ghi cua toi van mirror. Da xay ra dieu gi?

Hai kha nang: (a) ban tat mirror trong Zoom nhung ban ghi duoc tao qua trinh ghi man hinh (OBS, QuickTime) chup cua so Zoom, trong truong hop do trinh ghi chup goc nhin mirror cuc bo; (b) ban co mirror cap phan cung duoc bat trong app nha cung cap camera (Logitech G HUB, Razer Synapse) de cai dat Zoom. Kiem tra app nha cung cap truoc khi gia dinh Zoom la van de.

OBS ghi xem truoc mirror cua toi - lam sao sua ma khong mat goc nhin mirror toi thich?

OBS khong co toggle rieng "chi mirror xem truoc" - cai ban dat tren nguon la cai duoc ghi. Giai phap hai nguon: them camera nhu nguon Video Capture Device HAI lan. Ap dung Flip Horizontal cho mot (dung cai nay lam xem truoc "monitor" cua ban), va dung cai khong lat trong canh dau ra. An cai khong lat khoi canvas neu ban khong muon thay. Dieu nay cho phep tham khao ghi chu voi monitor mirror dong thoi gui stream khong mirror.

Camera cua toi hien toi nguoc dau, khong phai mirror. Day co la cung van de?

Khong, do la flip (doc) hay xoay 180 do, khong phai mirror. Nguyen nhan pho bien nhat la webcam USB duoc gan nguoc dau (cap o tren thay vi duoi). Hoac xoay vat ly camera hoac, trong OBS, ap dung Transform → Rotate 180° cho nguon. Zoom, Meet, va Teams khong hien thi toggle xoay; neu viec gan camera la van de, ban phai sua o phan cung hoac trong app nha cung cap camera.

Cong cu test camera o tren co ghi hay luu gi khong?

Khong. /camera-test.html la kiem tra hoan toan client-only - trang duoc dung quanh navigator.mediaDevices.getUserMedia va dung canvas.toDataURL("image/jpeg") cho snapshot cuc bo, ca hai chi chay trong trinh duyet cua ban. Khong co khung nao duoc tai len, truyen, hay luu tren may chu. Huong dan nay khong thay doi dieu do - no la giai thich viet khong co xu ly media nao.
