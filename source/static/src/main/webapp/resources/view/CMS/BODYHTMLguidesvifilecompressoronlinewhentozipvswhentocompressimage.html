Cong cu nen file online - khi nao ZIP mot thu muc, khi nao nen mot anh don le?

Cap nhat lan cuoi 2026-05-04. Mot quy tac dinh tuyen 30 giay cho truy van "cong cu nen file": khi nao click dung la Zip File (bien mot thu muc cac file thanh mot archive nho hon de chuyen) va khi nao la Compress Image (giam kich thuoc byte cua mot anh de email hoac trang tai nhanh hon). Hai cai la hai thao tac khac nhau tren dau vao khac nhau - chon sai cong cu chi them vong di-lai. Ca hai loi deu upload file cua ban len dich vu AWS cua freetoolonline, nen lua chon o day la ve thao tac phu hop voi dau vao, khong phai ve noi convert chay.

Cau tra loi trong 30 giay. Ban dang muon lam mot thu muc file nho lai de gui hoac luu? Mo Zip File - no gom moi file trong thu muc thanh mot archive ZIP, co the kem mat khau. Ban dang muon lam mot anh don le nho lai de no khong lam phinh email hoac trang load cham? Mo Compress Image - no ma hoa lai JPEG / PNG voi kich thuoc byte nho hon ma giu duoc chat luong nhin thay. Neu dau vao la mot anh chup man hinh, mot PDF, hoac hon hop cac loai file, xem quy tac 3. Neu file nhay cam hoac qua lon den muc upload kho khan, xem quy tac 4.

Tai sao "cong cu nen file" la hai thao tac khac nhau (va tai sao ca hai deu upload tren server)

Cum tu "cong cu nen file" bao gom hai tac vu nguoi doc khac nhau co mot diem chung - dau ra nho hon dau vao - nhung ngoai ra hau nhu khong chia se gi. Tac vu nen thu muc lay nhieu file (hoac mot file cong voi cau truc thu muc cua no) va gom chung thanh mot container archive duy nhat nhanh hon de gui va de luu hon. Zip File la cong cu cho tac vu do: no nen thu muc va file thanh archive ZIP online va them bao ve bang mat khau tuy chon truoc khi tai xuong, nen mot lan upload tra ve mot .zip. Tac vu anh don le lay mot JPEG hoac PNG va ma hoa lai pixel o muc tieu chat luong thap hon de file nho lai - kich thuoc giu nguyen nhung file nho hon tren dia. Compress Image la cong cu cho tac vu do: keo anh vao, chap nhan muc tieu chat luong mac dinh, va tai xuong ban nho hon. Hai dau vao khac nhau, hai dau ra khac nhau, hai cong cu khac nhau.

Ca hai loi deu upload dau vao len dich vu AWS cua freetoolonline - buoc convert chay tren server va ket qua tai ve trinh duyet cua ban. File dau ra duoc luu o S3 va xoay vong sau cua so giu chuan cua dich vu. Neu ban dac biet can mot loi khong upload, huong dan nay khong phai quy tac dung - xem cong cu online mien phi hoat dong khong upload file de biet cac loi khong-upload (la mot tap khac va khong bao gom /zip-file.html hoac /compress-image.html).

Quy tac dinh tuyen 1 - dau vao la mot thu muc file: mo Zip File

Neu dau vao la nhieu hon mot file - hoac mot file cong voi thu muc no nam trong - Zip File la cong cu. Cac dau hieu cu the cua truong hop nay:

  • Mot gioi han dinh kem email chan ban gui mot thu muc hoa don, anh, hoac ban thao hop dong. ZIP gop thu muc thanh mot .zip ma client email coi nhu mot dinh kem don le.
  • Mot upload "tha thu muc vao day" tren dich vu khac tu choi thu muc vi chi nhan file - container ZIP lam thu muc trong nhu mot file.
  • Ban muon chia se mot du an (mot website nho, mot thu muc bai tap, mot mau code) duoi dang mot link thay vi nhieu. Nguoi nhan giai nen bang OS hoac bang Unzip File trong trinh duyet.
  • Ban muon them mat khau truoc khi gui. Zip File phoi bay bao ve bang mat khau tuy chon trong cung loi; nguoi nhan co mat khau dung Remove ZIP Password hoac OS de mo. Bo dung cu ZIP day du (nen / giai nen / dat mat khau / xoa mat khau) o ZIP Tools.

Ly do: ZIP duoc thiet ke chinh xac cho tac vu nguoi doc "nhieu file, mot phong bi". Ban than viec nen la mot tac dung phu cho nhieu loai dau vao hon hop (anh va video da nen khong co nho them trong container ZIP), nhung viec gom - mot phong bi, mot upload, mot tai xuong - la gia tri ma trang duoc index dem lai. Huong dan di kem Cong cu nen file vs ZIP - chon cai nao di qua so sanh song song; Cach nen mot thu muc di qua loi click-tung-click. Neu dau vao la mot anh (khong phai thu muc), day khong phai quy tac cua ban - nhay sang quy tac 2.

Quy tac dinh tuyen 2 - dau vao la mot anh va ban muon it kilobyte hon: mo Compress Image

Neu dau vao la mot JPEG hoac PNG don le va dau ra ban muon la cung anh do voi kich thuoc byte nho hon, Compress Image la cong cu - khong phai ZIP. Cac dau hieu cu the:

  • Mot blog hoac trang san pham tai cham tren mobile vi anh hero hai megabyte. Compress Image ma hoa lai JPEG o muc tieu chat luong thap hon (dien hinh: 75-85 chat luong tren tuong duong slider JPEG 4 truc) va dua xuong vai tram kilobyte ma khong thay doi anh nhin thay.
  • Mot gioi han dinh kem email tu choi mot anh lon (vi du HEIC iPhone da chuyen thanh JPG 6 MB). Compress Image giam kich thuoc byte; neu format la HEIC, chay qua HEIC to JPG truoc. Trade-off (chat luong nhin thay vs kich thuoc file) o Nen JPEG khong mat chat luong - chat luong vs kich thuoc.
  • Mot cong cu web hoac mang xa hoi tu choi mot anh vi vuot qua gioi han byte tren moi anh. Kich thuoc anh on; chi byte la cai chan. Compress Image nham vao chinh dieu do.

Ly do: dat mot JPEG vao trong ZIP hau nhu khong bao gio thu nho duoc, vi byte JPEG da duoc nen o muc codec - container ZIP chi them vai byte cua phong bi. Loi anh don le thay doi muc tieu chat luong cua chinh codec, la thao tac duy nhat tao ra file nho do duoc. Neu anh trong mo sau khi qua cong cu chuyen biet, loi chan doan o JPG da nen trong mo - ba nguyen nhan. Neu ban co nhieu anh cung luc, xem quy tac 3.

Quy tac dinh tuyen 3 - dau vao hon hop (anh chup, PDF, nhieu anh): chon theo hinh dang dau ra

Cau tra loi hai cong cu o tren bao quat cac truong hop sach. Cac truong hop hon hop can mot cau hoi them - ban muon dau ra hinh dang nao?

  • Nhieu anh, ban muon mot phong bi de gui: ZIP truoc qua Zip File. Moi anh trong ZIP giu kich thuoc byte rieng; ZIP chi tiet kiem cho ban mot slot dinh kem. Neu anh tung cai qua lon, nen tung cai voi Compress Image truoc, roi ZIP cac ban nen.
  • Nhieu anh, ban muon tung cai nho hon: khong ZIP - nen tung cai voi Compress Image. ZIP khong ma hoa lai JPEG. Neu ban can xu ly batch nhieu anh trong mot upload, gan nhat la Photo Editor cho loi tuong tac, hoac ImageMagick Online voi pipeline go tay (de cap trong ImageMagick Online vs Task-Specific Tools - chon cai nao).
  • Mot PDF qua lon cho email: nen PDF la thao tac khac voi nen anh. Loi chuyen biet gan nhat tren site la Compose PDF de gop lai; quy tac toi uu chi PDF (subsetting font, downsampling anh trong PDF) khong duoc Zip File hoac Compress Image phoi bay, nen ca hai cong cu khong ap dung sach.
  • Anh chup man hinh (PNG): nen anh chup o muc codec thuong nghia la chuyen PNG sang JPG truoc vi codec PNG giu moi pixel chinh xac. Compress Image xu ly viec do; nen tang lua chon format o JPG vs PNG cho web.

Ly do: loai dau vao va hinh dang dau ra quyet dinh cong cu nao. Loi can tranh la "toi se ZIP het" khi cai ban thuc su muon la tung anh nho hon, hoac "toi se nen anh" khi cai ban thuc su muon la mot phong bi chua nhieu file. Chon theo hinh dang dau ra, khong theo tu trong o tim kiem.

Quy tac dinh tuyen 4 - file lon hoac nhay cam: nghi ky truoc khi upload

Ca Zip File va Compress Image deu upload dau vao len dich vu AWS cua freetoolonline. Day la loi dung cho hau nhu moi tac vu nguoi doc cong cu trinh duyet, nhung hai truong hop doi hoi cau tra loi dinh tuyen khac. Mot, dau vao rat lon (mot thu muc anh RAW co gigabyte, file video mot tieng trong thu muc, mot archive du an da vuot vai tram megabyte) cham de upload va cham de download ngay tren ket noi tot - neu ban co mot may desktop, cong cu ZIP tich hop cua OS (click phai - "Compress" tren macOS, click phai - "Send to - Compressed folder" tren Windows, zip tren Linux) nhanh hon tu dau den cuoi vi byte khong bao gio roi khoi dia. Hai, file ban khong the upload vi ly do bao mat (anh y te, bang chung phap ly, anh hop dong, source code duoi NDA) can o lai local - cong cu ZIP cap OS la loi dung ngay ca khi file nho.

Trade-off co hinh dang giong cac quyet dinh dinh tuyen video va anh tuong duong: FFmpeg online vs FFmpeg local - khi nao moi cai thang va ImageMagick Online vs Task-Specific Tools - chon cai nao deu di qua bien "online vs local" chi tiet. Bien o day la giong: loi online thang ve tien loi cho dau vao kich thuoc binh thuong, khong nhay cam; loi local thang ve toc do va quyen rieng tu cho dau vao lon hoac nhay cam.

Quy tac dinh tuyen 5 - dau ra sai hoac upload bi treo: chan doan truoc khi chay lai

Neu Zip File hoac Compress Image chap nhan upload nhung khong co download xuat hien trong vai phut, dung upload lai. Upload lai cung file khong sua duoc nguyen nhan goc; no chi xep hang mot job thu hai dap vao cung buc tuong. Cac treo thuong gap co cung hinh dang tren ca hai cong cu: gioi han kich thuoc dau vao bi cham khi upload, tab trinh duyet bi refresh trong pipeline convert, hoac rang buoc format dau ra khong dat (vi du, co gang ZIP mot thu muc co tong kich thuoc vuot quy mo upload).

Neu dau ra sai hinh dang, ban co the da chon sai cong cu. Neu .zip da den nhung tung anh ben trong van lon, ban muon Compress Image truoc, roi Zip File - .zip chi gom, khong ma hoa lai. Neu JPG da nen da den nhung gio trong mo thay vi chi nho hon, muc tieu chat luong dat qua thap - loi chan doan o JPG da nen trong mo - ba nguyen nhan. Neu nguoi nhan khong mo duoc .zip, ho co the can Unzip File trong trinh duyet, va neu co mat khau chan duong Remove ZIP Password bao quat loi do. Voi moi cai khac - mot thao tac thuong gap, cai dat mac dinh, cong cu dung cho hinh dang dau vao - quyet dinh dinh tuyen la mot trong bon quy tac o tren: ZIP cho thu muc, Compress Image cho anh don le, dau vao hon hop theo hinh dang dau ra, cai dat local cho file lon hoac nhay cam. Cau tra loi dung mat 30 giay; cau tra loi sai mat them mot phut upload ma ban khong can ton.
