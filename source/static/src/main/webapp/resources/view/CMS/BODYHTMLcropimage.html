Image cropper and rotator trims an image to a specific aspect ratio and rotates it in 90-degree steps or at a free angle.

  1. Upload a JPG, PNG, or WebP image.
  2. Drag the crop box to your target frame, or pick an aspect-ratio preset; rotate with the controls if needed.
  3. Download the cropped image.

Tip: Aspect-ratio presets cover the common social sizes (1:1, 4:5, 16:9) and avoid manual pixel math.
