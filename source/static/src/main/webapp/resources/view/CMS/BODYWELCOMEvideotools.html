Three video tools cover the common browser-based workflows

Pick the tool that matches the task and skip the others. All Video Converter handles one-off format switches — MP4 in, MOV or WebM out, no install. Video Maker stitches a slideshow from a folder of stills with adjustable per-frame duration. FFmpeg Online runs the command-line filter chains that GUI tools cannot express — concat, crop, two-pass bitrate caps, audio remap — directly in the browser. All three keep the source file in the page so a free video converter that runs online needs no upload to a third-party server.
