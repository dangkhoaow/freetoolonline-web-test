Vi sao hau het tep ZIP khong nho di

Cong viec cua mot tep ZIP la hai thu: dong goi nhieu tep vao mot container va (tuy chon) nen chung. Buoc nen su dung DEFLATE, no doc tung tep dau vao tim cac mau byte lap lai va thay the chung bang token ngan hon. Van ban thuan - log, CSV, JSON, ma nguon, dinh dang anh khong nen nhu BMP hoac TIFF khong nen - chua rat nhieu lap lai, vi vay DEFLATE nen rat manh; giam 50-70% kich thuoc la binh thuong tren cac luu tru chu yeu van ban. JPG, MP4, MP3 va hau het PDF da qua mot bo nen chuyen biet linh vuc (luong tu hoa JPEG cho anh, codec AAC hoac MP3 cho am thanh, H.264 / H.265 cho video), vi vay luong byte trong giong nhieu doi voi DEFLATE. Ket qua la ZIP chi them chi phi container (header tep, ban ghi muc luc) ma khong tim duoc tiet kiem. Dau ra thuong nho hon 0-5%, doi khi lon hon 1-2%.

Quy trinh thuc su giam kich thuoc

Neu muc tieu la "lam giao hang nay nho hon", tach noi dung co the nen voi noi dung da nen truoc khi ZIP:

  1. Xac dinh cac tep nang. Sap xep thu muc theo kich thuoc. Mot hoac hai tep dau bang hau nhu luon chiem 80% tong so, va chung thuong la anh, ghi man hinh hoac PDF.
  2. Giam cac tep nang rieng le. Doi kich thuoc anh 4000x3000 thanh 2000x1500 voi Resize Image; nen lai voi chat luong thap hon voi Compress Image; cat video voi cac cong cu video tai /video-tools.html; lam phang PDF nang voi Compress PDF.
  3. Bay gio ZIP cac tep da toi uu. Mo /zip-tools/zip-file.html, tha cac tep thay the (nho hon) cung voi cac tep thuc su chu yeu van ban, va de DEFLATE xu ly phan con lai.

Ket qua cuoi cung la mot tep ZIP co kich thuoc bi chi phoi boi cac tep phuong tien moi, nho hon. Buoc ZIP la 5% cuoi cung, khong phai 95% dau tien.

The con thanh truot muc nen thi sao?

Mot so cong cu ZIP hien thi mot muc (Store / Fast / Normal / Maximum / Ultra). Thanh truot chu yeu danh doi thoi gian CPU o ben gui de lay vai phan tram giam kich thuoc them:

  • Store - khong nen. Chi huu ich khi toc do quan trong hon kich thuoc (mot thu muc rat lon duoc tai len qua ket noi cham noi CPU la nut that).
  • Fast / Normal (DEFLATE mac dinh) - cai dat tieu chuan. Can bang tot nhat giua thoi gian build va kich thuoc. Su dung cai nay tru khi co ly do khong dung.
  • Maximum / Ultra - thoi gian CPU dai hon de lay vai phan tram them tren mot luu tru chu yeu van ban. Hau nhu khong bao gio xoay kim tren luu tru chua phuong tien.

Neu ZIP cua ban khong nho di, thanh truot muc la nut sai de xoay. Dau vao khong the nen, het - va ban can buoc xu ly truoc o tren.

FAQ

ZIP cua toi lon hon mot chut so voi thu muc goc. Co gi do bi loi khong?

Khong - do la ket qua mong doi khi moi dau vao da duoc nen (JPG / MP4 / MP3 / hau het PDF). Container ZIP them mot ban ghi muc luc nho cho moi tep; DEFLATE khong tim duoc gi de nen; delta rong tu 0 den vai phan tram lon hon.

Co nen dung 7z hoac RAR thay vi ZIP de co ket qua giam kich thuoc thuc su?

7z va RAR su dung thuat toan nen manh hon (LZMA2, RAR5) va co the nen them phuong tien da nen 5-10%. Danh doi la tam voi: khong phai nguoi nhan nao cung co trinh mo 7z / RAR, va nhieu trinh quet dinh kem webmail hoac widget tai len tu choi bat ky thu gi ngoai .zip. ZIP mac dinh ve toi da tinh tuong thich; chi thay doi neu ban kiem soat ca hai dau cua viec giao hang.

Toi co the chi nang muc nen len "Ultra" va bo qua xu ly truoc khong?

Ban co the thu, nhung ket qua thuong gay that vong. Tren mot thu muc anh va mot video, "Ultra" co the cat them 1-2% voi cai gia la thoi gian build dai. Doi kich thuoc anh va cat video truoc se cat 70-80%. Kha nang nen cua dau vao dat tran; thanh truot muc chi quyet dinh ban tien gan tran do bao xa.

Bao ve mat khau ZIP co thay doi kich thuoc khong?

Bao ve mat khau (mat khau mo + ma hoa AES) them mot chi phi co dinh nho - thuong vai tram byte - nhung khong thay doi kha nang nen cua noi dung. Kich thuoc bi chi phoi boi dau vao, khong phai ma hoa.

Tom tat mot dong

De giam kich thuoc tep ZIP: dung bat dau o buoc ZIP. Sap xep thu muc theo kich thuoc, thu nho cac tep phuong tien nang rieng le (doi kich thuoc, ma hoa lai), sau do ZIP cac tep da toi uu o muc nen mac dinh. ZIP la goi; loi the kich thuoc xay ra o thuong nguon cua no.
