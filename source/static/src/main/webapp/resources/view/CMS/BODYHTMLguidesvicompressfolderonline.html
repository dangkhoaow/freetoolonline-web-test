Nen Thu Muc Truc Tuyen

Nen thu muc truc tuyen nghia la giao toan bo mot thu muc cho cong cu web va nhan lai mot tep .zip duy nhat, san sang gui email hoac tai len duoi dang mot tep don. Trinh tao tai /zip-file.html nhan thu muc qua HTTPS, dung tep luu tru tren dich vu tai len roi dung rieng tu, va tra tep .zip ve trinh duyet cua ban. Thu muc con duoc giu nguyen, nen nguoi nhan giai nen tep luu tru tro ve cung bo cuc thu muc ban da gui.

Cong thuc ba buoc

Mo trinh tao, keo thu muc vao vung tai len, va nhan Convert. Cau truc thu muc giu nguyen ben trong tep luu tru ket qua, mot thuoc tinh dang xac nhan khi ban ban giao du an: nguoi nhan giai nen .zip va tim thay cung bo cuc long nhau ban co tren dia, khong phai dong phang moi tep tu moi thu muc con. Dat ten tep luu tru trong hop Cai dat truoc khi dung de nguoi nhan thay dieu gi ro rang khi den noi ("tai-chinh-q3.zip" hon "archive.zip"). Dau ra la mot tep .zip duy nhat, tra ve trinh duyet cua ban duoi dang tai xuong.

Khi nao ban can mat khau tren tep luu tru

Neu thu muc di chuyen qua mot kenh noi ban khong the tin hoan toan vao danh sach nguoi nhan - email chuyen tiep, hop thu chung, nhom chat - hop Cai dat cung cap truong mat khau tuy chon voi ba lua chon ma hoa. Ma hoa Standard ZIP duoc chap nhan rong rai nhung yeu theo tieu chuan hien tai. AES-128 la mac dinh hien dai, duoc Windows Explorer (chuot phai Extract All) va Archive Utility macOS chap nhan. AES-256 la muc cao nhat voi cung tran tuong thich nhu AES-128. Gui mat khau qua kenh rieng - gui mat khau va tep cung nhau se vo hieu muc dich. Mat khau duoc ap dung tai thoi diem dung, nen nguoi nhan duoc nhac khi giai nen.

Trinh tao khong lam gi

Trinh tao dung tep luu tru moi - no khong be khoa ma hoa tren .zip do nguoi khac dung. No khong tao 7z hoac RAR (ca hai dac hon ZIP nhung yeu cau cai dat ben thu ba tren hau het may nguoi nhan, nen trinh tao giu ZIP de phu da nen tang). No khong loai metadata khoi tep dau vao: EXIF tren anh JPG, truong tac gia tren tai lieu Word, va dau thoi gian sua doi deu di vao tep luu tru khong doi. Neu metadata do can lam sach, hay lam truoc khi tai thu muc len.

Cac huong dan lien quan tren trang nay

  • Cach nen mot thu muc - huong dan tung buoc tap trung vao truong hop thu muc-lam-dau-vao.
  • Zip thu muc truc tuyen mien phi - cong thuc mien phi dua tren trinh duyet thu muc-thanh-zip.
  • Thu muc thanh zip - cung trinh tao duoi goc nhin luong thu muc-lam-dau-vao.
  • Cach nen thu muc de gui email - truong hop gioi han dinh kem email (gioi han 25 MB).
