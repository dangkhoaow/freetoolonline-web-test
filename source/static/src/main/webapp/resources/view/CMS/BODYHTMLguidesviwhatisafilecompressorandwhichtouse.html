Cong Cu Nen File La Gi va Nen Dung Loai Nao

Cap nhat lan cuoi 2026-04-27. Tat ca cong cu ben duoi chay trong trinh duyet tren chinh thiet bi cua ban - khong file nao roi khoi may tinh hay dien thoai cua ban.

Cau tra loi trong 30 giay. "Cong cu nen file" khong phai mot cong cu duy nhat. Do la mot nhom sau thao tac khac nhau, va lua chon dung phu thuoc vao loai file ban co. Mot thu muc co file hon hop: nen thanh ZIP. Anh roi: nen anh. Anh HEIC tu iPhone ma nguoi nhan Windows khong mo duoc: chuyen HEIC sang JPG. Nhieu anh gop thanh mot file de chia se: gom vao mot PDF. Nhieu PDF gui cho cung mot nguoi: noi chung lai thanh mot. Mot file rat lon (video 4K, anh RAW, file nguon thiet ke): dung link chuyen file thay vi dinh kem. Khong co cong cu nen file van nang vi toan hoc nen phia sau khac nhau cho moi loai file.

Tai sao "cong cu nen file" la tim kiem chia ra nam huong

Cum tu "cong cu nen file" nam o dinh phieu khach. Hai phan ba nguoi go cau nay vao Google chua quyet dinh loai file minh can nen. Mot so dang co gui mot PowerPoint 30 MB. Mot so co gang nhet mot thu muc bien lai duoi gioi han upload 5 MB. Mot so dang gui 200 anh iPhone den may tinh Windows. Mot so co gang lam mot MP4 drone 4 GB du nho de chia se. Cau tra loi non not - "dung cong cu nen file mien phi" - dan ca nam nguoi do toi cung mot cong cu, va bon trong so ho nhan ket qua te hon so voi viec chon cong cu chuyen biet dung.

Cay quyet dinh don gian khi ban da biet. Phan kho la khong cong cu tim kiem nao phan biet duoc nam y dinh kia tu cum tu "cong cu nen file" mot cach dang tin cay. Vi vay huong dan nay la lop lam ro y dinh. Doc bang, nhay den phan phu hop voi du lieu cua ban, va bo qua phan con lai.

Sau loai nen, sau loai toan hoc khac nhau

Nguoi ta noi "nen" co the la bat ky cai nao trong nhung cai sau. Moi cai dung mot thuat toan can ban khac nhau va cho muc giam khac nhau voi cung mot du lieu vao.

  • Nen container khong mat (ZIP, 7z, RAR). Gom nhieu file thanh mot archive va nen dong byte bang DEFLATE / LZMA / LZMA2. Co the dao nguoc - file giai nen giong het ban goc theo tung bit. Tuyet voi voi text va source code (giam 50-80%). Gan nhu khong tac dung voi media da nen (JPG / MP4 / MP3 / PDF). Lua chon dung khi ban can giu nguyen file chinh xac.
  • Nen anh co mat (chat luong JPEG, WebP). Loai bo thong tin hinh anh ma mat nguoi it co kha nang nhan ra va ma hoa lai ket qua. Giam JPG 5 MB xuong 1 MB o chat luong 80 khong co khac biet thay duoc tren da so anh. Khong dao nguoc duoc - mot khi nen o chat luong 60 ban khong khoi phuc duoc ban goc. Lua chon dung khi file la mot anh va nguoi nhan khong quan tam do trung thuc pixel-perfect.
  • Chuyen dinh dang anh (HEIC sang JPG, PNG sang JPG, TIFF sang JPG). Doi dinh dang ma hoa. Thay doi kich thuoc la tac dung phu cua viec di tu dinh dang kem hieu qua sang dinh dang hieu qua hon. PNG sang JPG giam buc anh 12 MB xuong 1 MB; HEIC sang JPG giu kich thuoc tuong tu nhung mo khoa moi thiet bi Windows / Android. Lua chon dung khi file o sai dinh dang voi nguoi nhan.
  • Nen video co mat (ma hoa lai voi bitrate thap hon). Ma hoa lai luong video voi muc tieu bit-per-second thap hon. Giam MP4 4K 1 GB xuong 200 MB o 1080p H.264 voi chat luong chap nhan duoc. Cong cu trinh duyet xu ly clip ngan; video dai chay qua phan mem chuyen biet nhu FFmpeg hoac HandBrake.
  • Nen am thanh co mat (MP3 / AAC / OGG). Y tuong giong video nhung cho am thanh. WAV 50 MB tro thanh MP3 5 MB o 192 kbps. ZIP tren trinh duyet khong giup duoc; nuoc di dung la chuyen dinh dang.
  • Luu tru link chuyen file. Ve ky thuat khong phai la nen - file van giu nguyen kich thuoc, nhung nguoi nhan tai ve tu mot dich vu thay vi nhan dinh kem. Day la cau tra loi thuc te duy nhat cho file lon hon ~25 MB, gioi han tren cua hau het he thong email.

ZIP thuc su lam gi (va khong lam gi)

ZIP la thao tac dau tien hien len trong dau vi day la dong tu "nen cai nay cho toi" pho quat tren Windows tu nam 1997 va tren macOS tu 2003. No xuat sac trong mot cong viec cu the va yeu trong hau het cong viec khac.

ZIP gioi o cho: gom nhieu file, giu cau truc thu muc, dat mat khau cho goi, nen noi dung nhieu text (source code, CSV, tai lieu thuan) giam 50-80%. Dau ra la mot file .zip ban co the dinh kem email hoac upload len portal khong cho upload thu muc. Cong cu ZIP File xu ly viec nay trong trinh duyet ma khong upload gi.

ZIP yeu o cho: nen media da nen. JPG, PNG, MP3, MP4, HEIC, PDF deu da duoc nen ben trong; thuat toan DEFLATE khong the ep them. Zip mot thu muc 100 anh iPhone thuong tiet kiem 1-3% tong kich thuoc, khong phai 50%. Neu thu muc cua ban chu yeu la file media, ZIP la sai cong cu. Cach lam file zip nho hon noi ve nhung gi can lam khi ZIP khong dat.

Khi chuyen dinh dang thang nen

Voi file don le, chuyen dinh dang thuong thang nen voi chenh lech lon vi file nguon o sai dinh dang cho truong hop su dung:

  • HEIC sang JPG. HEIC 3 MB tu iPhone chi mo duoc tren Windows voi codec tra phi; ban JPG la 1,5-3 MB nhung mo o moi noi. Chuyen doi la ve tinh tuong thich, khong phai kich thuoc. Chay qua HEIC to JPG. De biet them ve HEIC xem HEIC vs JPG vs WebP - khi nao dung dinh dang nao.
  • PNG sang JPG (cho anh). Mot buc anh PNG 12 MB tro thanh JPG 1,2 MB o chat luong 85. PNG la dinh dang khong mat danh cho anh chup man hinh va do hoa; dung cho anh chup lang phi 90% kich thuoc file. Giu PNG cho trong suot, hinh ve net, hoac anh chup pixel-perfect.
  • TIFF / BMP sang JPG. Ca hai deu lon hon JPG 5-10 lan cho cung mot noi dung thay duoc. Chuyen truoc khi gui; nguoi nhan tren dien thoai thuong khong mo duoc TIFF.
  • WAV sang MP3. WAV 50 MB tro thanh MP3 5 MB o 192 kbps. ZIP tren trinh duyet khong giup duoc; nuoc di dung la chuyen dinh dang trong phan mem am thanh chuyen biet.

Rieng tu: tai sao "chi trinh duyet" quan trong

Da so ket qua cua "cong cu nen file mien phi" upload file cua ban len server, chay nen tren cloud cua ho, va gui email link tai ve. Tot voi anh meo. Khong tot voi mau HR, ho so y te, hop dong, source code, hoac bat cu thu gi thuoc dien GDPR / HIPAA. Nam cong cu liet ke trong huong dan nay deu chay tren chinh thiet bi cua ban bang API natif cua trinh duyet (cong cu ZIP dung API JavaScript Streams + Compression Streams; cong cu anh dung API Canvas + FileReader; chuyen HEIC dung libheif bien dich sang WASM). Khong gi roi khoi may tinh hay dien thoai cua ban.

De biet them ve nhom nay xem cong cu online mien phi hoat dong khong upload file.

Buoc tiep theo

Chon phan ben tren phu hop voi du lieu cua ban va click sang dung cong cu. Neu ban co mot thu muc cho email va muon buoc-tung-buoc thay vi tour cong cu, di thang den cach nen mot thu muc cho email. Neu du lieu cua ban la mot file thay vi mot thu muc, xem cach nen mot file online. Neu ban da co mot ZIP qua lon, xem cach giam kich thuoc file zip online.
