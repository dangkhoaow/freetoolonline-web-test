Cai "giai ma MD5" thuc su lam gi tren site nay

Bo chuyen MD5 tai /md5-converter.html phoi bay hai nut: To MD5 xay hash tu chuoi ban dan, va To Text kiem tu dien tim plaintext cap doi voi hash ban dan. To Text la cai gan nhat ma site co voi "giai ma", nhung do la tra cuu, khong phai tinh nguoc. No truoc tien kiem localStorage cua trinh duyet nay tim cap doi ban da tao truoc do tren cung thiet bi nay; neu khong tim thay, hoi tu dien dat truoc tren server tim mot khop. Neu hash cua ban chua bao gio duoc cap doi voi plaintext tren bat ky phia nao, tra cuu tra ve khong gi - day la hanh vi mong doi, khong phai loi, vi ban than MD5 khong the dao nguoc.

Neu hash ban co la pho bien

Chuoi pho bien - tu trong tu dien, mat khau pho thong, cum tu noi tieng - co the da nam trong tu dien MD5 cong cong vi ai do da hash chung tai mot diem nao do. Tra cuu To Text chong lai nhung cai do thuong thanh cong. Chuoi dai, ngau nhien, hoac dac trung nguoi dung hau nhu khong bao gio duoc hash truoc, nen tra cuu tra ve khong gi. Ti le thanh cong phu thuoc hoan toan vao viec ai do truoc do co tao va luu cung mot cap doi; khong phu thuoc vao do manh cua bat ky thuat toan nao. Neu ban muon viec khoi phuc thanh cong cho chuoi ban tu tao, hash mot lan voi nut To MD5 trong khi van trong tay ban; bo chuyen luu cap doi do trong localStorage de mot tra cuu To Text tuong lai cua cung hash tra ve chuoi goc tren cung trinh duyet.

Neu tac vu thuc cua ban la luu mat khau

Neu ban dang tim "giai ma MD5 online" vi ban da luu mat khau duoi dang MD5 va gio can xac minh, cach sua dung khong phai giai ma MD5; ma la ngung dung MD5 cho cong viec do. MD5 nhanh va mac dinh khong co salt, khien brute-force offline kha thi tren phan cung hien dai. Huong dan chuyen biet ve cac lua chon thay the giai thich khi nao bcrypt, argon2id, hoac scrypt la su thay the dung cho loi mat khau cu the ban dang xay, va loi migration nao moi cai ho tro cho cot MD5 hien co. Ban than cot MD5 van o nguyen vi tri trong khi migration; mat khau moi duoc re-hash voi thuat toan moi o lan login ke tiep, va gia tri MD5 cu duoc loai bo dan khi nguoi dung login.
