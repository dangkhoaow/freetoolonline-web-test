Nen ZIP

"Nen ZIP" la quy trinh lay mot thu muc tep va ghi mot tep .zip duy nhat chua chung. Tep ket qua nho hon tong cua cac tep dau vao chi khi nhung tep do co the nen duoc (van ban thuan, ma nguon, anh chua nen); cac dinh dang da nen nhu JPG, MP4 hoac MP3 thuong tang vai phan tram do chi phi dong goi ZIP.

Neu ban dang co lam mot ZIP nho hon, hay tach cac tep "co the nen" voi cac tep "da nen". Cac tep nhieu van ban (log, CSV, JSON, ma nguon) thuong thu nho dang ke; anh JPG, video MP4, am thanh MP3 va nhieu PDF thuong khong, va co the lon hon mot chut khi duoc boc trong ZIP. De co duoc thang loi thuc su ve dung luong, gop cac tep co the nen lai voi nhau va xu ly truyen thong rieng truoc khi luu tru.

Bo tao ZIP trong trinh duyet o tai /zip-tools/zip-file.html.
