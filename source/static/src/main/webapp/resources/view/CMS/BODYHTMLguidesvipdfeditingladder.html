Thang Chinh Sua PDF - Chon Dung Cong Cu Theo Y Dinh (2026)

Lan ra soat cuoi 2026-04-24. Muoi hai cong cu PDF dua tren trinh duyet anh xa sang nam y dinh chinh sua PDF thong dung nhat. Khong cai, khong dang ky, tep khong bao gio roi thiet bi cua ban.

Quyet dinh 30 giay. Tra loi cau hoi nay mot lan: ban da co PDF, hay ban dang lam mot tu cac mieng? Neu ban da co - ban muon tach, trich xuat, bao ve, chuyen doi, hoac lam phang. Neu ban dang lam - ban muon soan tu anh hoac gop PDF co san. Thang ben duoi anh xa moi y dinh sang cong cu chinh xac.

Dau thang: hai cau hoi nhap canh

Moi luong cong viec PDF bat dau voi mot trong hai tien de. Tra loi cai nao ap dung va phan con lai cua quyet dinh sup do nhanh:

  • "Toi da co PDF." Ban dang sua doi, trich xuat tu, bao ve, hoac chuyen doi tai lieu co san. Nhay sang chinh sua / trich xuat tu PDF co san.
  • "Toi can lam mot PDF." Ban dang lap rap tai lieu moi tu anh, trang rieng le, hoac PDF khac. Nhay sang tao PDF tu cac mieng.

Hai duong chia se gan nhu khong cong cu - chon dung cai dau tien tiet kiem 90% met moi quyet dinh "toi thuc su can cong cu nao".

Lua chon tinh te la giua Images to PDF va Compose PDF. Ca hai lap rap anh thanh PDF, nhung Compose PDF phoi bo cuc tung trang (le, quy tac ngat trang, ghi de metadata) trong khi Images to PDF dung mac dinh thong minh. Quy tac thuc te: album anh va portfolio dung Images to PDF; bao cao quy va hoa don voi anh va van ban hon hop dung Compose PDF.

Duong B: Chinh sua / trich xuat tu PDF co san

Nam tac vu con anh xa sang tam cong cu. Cong cu dung phu thuoc vao PDF nguon trong nhu the nao va ban muon co gi ra:

1. Tach PDF co san
  • Trich xuat khoang trang lien tuc (vi du trang 5-12 thanh tep moi) → Split PDF by range.
  • No moi trang thanh tep rieng (vi du goi quet 50 trang thanh 50 PDF mot trang) → Split PDF to each page.

Duong tach-theo-khoang la cai ban muon khi nguoi tieu thu phia sau chi can mot phan; duong tach-thanh-tung-trang la cai ban muon khi quy trinh phia sau la tung-trang (luu tru, xem theo tung ban ghi, ky duyet rieng).

2. Trich xuat noi dung tu PDF
  • Lay van ban ra (de sao, tim, OCR) → PDF to Text.
  • Lay anh ra (rasterize tung trang hoac xuat anh nhung) → PDF to Images.
  • Chuyen sang HTML than thien web (chon sao chep, nhung duoc, SEO crawl duoc) → PDF to HTML.

Quy tac chon: van ban de doc va sao chep; anh cho luu tru truc quan hoac thu nho tung trang; HTML cho xuat ban lai web hoac tiep can.

3. Bao ve hoac bo bao ve PDF
  • Them mat khau (han che mo hoac chinh sua) → Protect PDF by Password.
  • Xoa mat khau da biet (xoa mat khau khoi PDF ban co quyen) → Remove PDF Password.

Huong dan dong hanh PDF password types: owner vs user phan biet giua hai lop mat khau ma moi PDF thuc su co; doc mot lan truoc khi khoa bat cu thu gi ban se chia se ben ngoai.

4. Lam phang PDF
  • Nuong chu thich, truong bieu mau, va lop vao noi dung trang (mot chieu, giu trang thai truc quan) → Flatten PDF.

Lam phang la buoc dung truoc luu tru: chu thich va du lieu bieu mau o trong lop co the chinh sua cho den khi duoc lam phang, co nghia la mot PDF chua ky co the trong nhu da ky o khung nhin lop. Sau khi lam phang, trang thai truc quan la trang thai pixel - huu ich cho luong phap ly / luu tru / ban sao da ky.

5. Preflight PDF
  • Kiem tra metadata, font, anh nhung, va cau truc truoc khi xuat ban hoac in → Preflight PDF.

Cac chuoi luong cong viec thong dung

Cong viec PDF thuc te hiem khi chi dung mot cong cu. Ba chuoi xuat hien du thuong xuyen de ghi nho:

  1. Xoa mat khau → chinh sua → khoa lai. Remove PDF Password → sua doi trong trinh chinh sua ban chon → Protect PDF by Password. Dung huong dan ky-sau-khi-xoa-mat-khau cho bien the ky.
  2. Gop → lam phang → luu tru. Join PDF → Flatten PDF → luu. Lam phang sau khi gop ghim chu thich va chu ky cua moi nguoi vao noi dung trang cuoi cung.
  3. Tach-theo-khoang → trich-van-ban → sao chep. Split PDF by range → PDF to Text → dan vao tai lieu. Huu ich de trich dan cac phan cua bao cao dai trong tom tat.

Khi nao HTML / dinh dang luu tru thang luong dua tren PDF

PDF khong phai luon la container dung. Hai tinh huong noi dinh dang khac thang:

  • Xuat ban lai web cua kho luu tru cu. Chuyen sang HTML qua PDF to HTML de lam noi dung tim kiem va lap chi muc duoc. Xem PDF vs HEIC for document archival cho so sanh chon dinh dang.
  • Tai lieu nang ve anh noi nen dominates chi phi luu tru. Cho luu tru kieu album anh, giu tep HEIC rieng le cong voi mot chi muc mong nho hon mot PDF nguyen khoi. Xem when to compress vs convert an image.

Cau hoi thuong gap

Toi co PDF bao ve mat khau va can tach. Thu tu la gi?

PDF bao ve mat khau khong the tach truc tiep vi cong cu khong doc duoc noi dung bao ve. Thu tu la: Remove PDF Password truoc (ban can mat khau hoac truy cap hop phap), roi Split PDF by range hoac Split PDF to each page. Sau khi tach, neu nguoi nhan se nhan ban sao bi khoa, chay Protect PDF by Password tren moi tep dau ra da tach.

Cong cu nao giu hyperlink noi va dau trang nguyen ven?

Cac thao tac gop va tach giu lai lien ket noi trang-den-trang va dau trang mien la trang muc tieu van con trong dau ra. Neu Split PDF by range tach trang 1-10 tu PDF 50 trang nhung dau trang trang 3 lien ket den trang 40, lien ket se giai quyet thanh trang bia cua tep dau ra sau khi tach. Lien ket URL ben ngoai luon mang theo. Cho do trung thanh muc luu tru noi moi chu thich quan trong, Flatten PDF truoc.

Toi can PDF bieu mau voi truong dien duoc - cong cu nao?

Khong cong cu nao trong danh sach o day tao truong bieu mau PDF tuong tac (AcroForm / XFA). Cac cong cu trinh duyet bac mien phi xu ly bo cuc, gop, tach, trich xuat va quan ly mat khau. Cho tao truong bieu mau ban can mot trinh chinh sua desktop (Acrobat Pro hoac tuong duong); sau khi bieu mau duoc tao o noi khac, ban van co the dung cac cong cu nay cho vong doi xung quanh (gop bieu mau da ky vao goi, lam phang truoc luu tru, trich van ban cho cau truc co so du lieu submissions).

Duong nhanh nhat tu "50 anh JPG" den "mot PDF luu tru" la gi?

Tha tat ca 50 anh vao Images to PDF voi cai dat mac dinh, tai PDF ve, roi chay Flatten PDF tren dau ra de ghim bat ky metadata lop anh nao vao noi dung da render. Tong thoi gian: duoi hai phut tren laptop hien dai. Neu anh la HEIC tu iPhone, chuyen chung qua HEIC to JPG truoc de nguoi tieu thu phia sau khong co codec HEIC co the xem PDF ma khong can phan mem them.

Tom tat

Thang chinh sua PDF gom 12 cong cu thanh 5 nhom y dinh: lam-tu-mieng, tach, trich xuat, bao ve, va lam phang. Bat dau voi cau hoi dau thang (da co PDF vs lam mot), roi xuong tung y dinh. Moi cong cu chay trong trinh duyet ma khong cai, vi vay thu nghiem voi cong cu sai khong ton gi - nhung thang co nghia ban se hiem khi can.
