Cac cau hoi thuong gap

Timestamp Unix mili giay la gi?

So mili giay tinh tu nua dem UTC ngay 1 thang 1 nam 1970. Mot gia tri 13 chu so bao phu timestamp tu cuoi nam 2001 toi cuoi nam 5138; bat cu thu gi ngoai khoang do la giay Unix (10 chu so) hoac micro giay (16 chu so).

Gia tri cua toi co 10 chu so chu khong phai 13 - lam sao day?

Day gan nhu chac chan la giay Unix. Hay nhan 1000 de chuyen sang mili giay, hoac dung truc tiep bo chuyen doi giay sang ngay. Loi he so 1000 la su lan lon pho bien nhat trong "ms sang ngay".

Viec chuyen doi co gui gia tri cua toi den server khong?
