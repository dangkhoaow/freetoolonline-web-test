Why this matters

Reach for the HD video converter when the source file is already in good shape and the only thing standing between you and a usable copy is a container or codec mismatch - an MP4 a teammate cannot open, a WebM a slide deck refuses to embed, a MOV the editing app wants as MKV. The tool repackages between MP4, WebM, MOV, AVI, and MKV without re-encoding, so the original bitrate, frame rate, and resolution survive the trip. Skip the tool when the goal is to shrink the file, trim a clip, or change its resolution - those jobs need a dedicated compressor or editor. Pull the audio track out as MP3, AAC, OGG, FLAC, M4A, or WAV when the picture is no longer needed, or render a short GIF preview at 10 frames per second and 480 px wide for chat-friendly sharing. The work runs locally in the browser, so the file never leaves the device.
