Cach nen mot tep ZIP den kich thuoc cu the (2 MB, 25 MB, 100 KB)

Soat lai lan cuoi 2026-05-05. "Nen ZIP nay xuong duoi 25 MB de toi co the gui email" nghe nhu mot thao tac, nhung la ba thao tac. Kich thuoc dau ra cua mot tep ZIP duoc quyet dinh boi nhung gi ben trong tep, boi muc do nen duoc cua cac byte do, va boi muc deflate duoc chon tai thoi diem ghi. Khong cai nao trong so do duoc dieu chinh ve "chinh xac N MB". Huong dan nay goi ten nhung gi thuc su kiem soat kich thuoc dau ra ZIP, va thu tu ap dung cac cong cu tren trang nay de ban dat duoc tran muc tieu (2 MB, 25 MB tran dinh kem Gmail / Outlook, 100 KB gioi han tai len) trong lan thu nhat hoac thu hai.

Cau tra loi 30 giay. ZIP khong the duoc bao "tao dau ra 25 MB". Kich thuoc ban nhan duoc la bat ke deflate tinh co tao ra tren cac byte ban da cap. De dat duoc mot tran cu the, hay lam dieu nay theo thu tu: (1) thu nho cai dong gop lon nhat truoc - anh, video, PDF - su dung cong cu moi tep dung (Compress Image cho JPG/PNG, HEIC to JPG cho nguon iPhone, Photo Editor neu ban cung can cat hoac doi kich thuoc). (2) Sau do ZIP cac tep nho hon voi ZIP File. (3) Neu kho luu tru van vuot tran, chia ra: ZIP co the chia thanh cac phan multi-volume moi cai vua voi giao thong cua ban, va hau het cac trinh doc kho luu tru se ghep lai chung.

Tai sao kich thuoc dau ra ZIP khong xac dinh duoc

Mot tep ZIP la mot dinh dang container voi nen deflate moi tep. Ba thu quyet dinh so byte ban nhan duoc o dau ra, va ban chi kiem soat hai cai dau:

  1. Cai gi co trong kho luu tru. Neu ban dat mot JPG 10 MB vao ZIP, ban nhan duoc cai gi do gan 10 MB ra. JPG da duoc nen; deflate khong the thu nho dang ke cac byte da nen. Dieu nay cung dung cho MP4, HEIC, PNG, MP3, ODT, DOCX, XLSX, PDF hien dai - tat ca chung luu tru du lieu trong mot dinh dang noi bo da nen. Boc ZIP gan nhu khong giam kich thuoc.
  2. Cac byte thuc su nen duoc nhu the nao. Van ban thuan, CSV, log tho, BMP, WAV, TIFF chua nen, ma nguon - nhung cai nay rat nen duoc. Mot log van ban 10 MB co the ha xuong gan 1 MB sau deflate. Vi vay cung kho luu tru co the thu nho 10x hoac 1.05x tuy thuoc vao nhung gi ben trong; khong co mot "ti le ZIP" duy nhat.
  3. Muc deflate. Hau het cac cong cu ZIP truc tuyen va "Send to compressed folder" o cap OS dung muc 6 (mac dinh). Muc 9 (toi da) mat them thoi gian tinh va cho ban them vai phan tram giam tren noi dung nen duoc - khong bao gio hon 5-10%. Muc 1 (nhanh nhat) tra lai ban gan nhu cung kich thuoc nhu khong nen. Slider muc KHONG dat duoc mot tran muc tieu mot cach ky dieu.

Ket hop: mot thu muc anh JPG se khong thu nho dang ke bat ke muc deflate nao ban chon, vi cac byte da duoc nen. Mot thu muc xuat CSV co the thu nho 8x o muc 9 vs 7x o muc 6. Tac vu "nen xuong duoi 25 MB" cua ban do do khong phai mot lua chon muc deflate - la mot cau hoi ve noi dung nao ban dat vao.

Luong lam viec hai buoc thuc su dat duoc mot tran

Cho 90% cac truong hop "toi can cai nay duoi N MB cho email / tai len / mot cong", duong di la: thu nho cac dong gop lon nhat truoc khi ZIP, sau do ZIP cac phien ban nho hon. Thu tu quan trong - ZIP truoc va sau do co gang thu nho ZIP la duong di cham vi ban mat toi uu hoa moi tep ma compress-image va heic-to-jpg co the cung cap.

Buoc 1: xac dinh va thu nho cac dong gop lon nhat

Mo thu muc. Sap xep theo kich thuoc, giam dan. Ba tep o tren cung gan nhu luon chiem >80% tong so. Chung thuong la anh, PDF quet, video, hoac nguon HEIC tu mot iPhone. Ap dung cong cu dung cho moi loai tep:

  • Anh JPG / PNG. Chay Compress Image. Slider chat luong di tu 1 den 100; cho "du tot de gui email" dai 60-75 ha mot JPG 4 MB xuong 600-900 KB ma khong co mat mat thay duoc. Doc Nen JPEG khong mat chat luong - chat luong vs kich thuoc ve hanh vi cua slider.
  • Anh HEIC tu iPhone. Chay HEIC to JPG. HEIC da hieu qua, nhung hau het cac client email tu choi dinh kem .heic hoan toan; chuyen sang JPG o chat luong 75 danh doi 5-15% tang kich thuoc cho kha nang mo duoc pho quat. Doc Cach chuyen HEIC sang JPG tung buoc cho luong moi tep.
  • PDF quet / nhieu hinh anh. Mot PDF quet la anh trong mot wrapper PDF. Xuat lai o DPI thap hon hoac nen lai hinh anh truoc. Cac cong cu PDF cua trang bao quat cach lam phang PDF va thang chinh sua PDF cho thu tu cong cu dung.
  • Video. ZIP video gan nhu khong lam gi. Ma hoa lai qua Video Converter voi bitrate thap hon truoc; MP4 vs WebM cho web bao quat su danh doi codec.
  • Van ban thuan / CSV / log. Khong can buoc truoc; nhung cai nay nen 5-10x ben trong ZIP o muc 6 roi.
Buoc 2: ZIP cac tep nho hon

Mo ZIP File, keo vao cac tep da thu nho, va tai kho luu tru ve. Muc mac dinh (6) la lua chon dung cho gan nhu moi truong hop - muc 9 cho ban them 1-3% tren noi dung nen duoc va them thoi gian CPU ban khong lay lai duoc. Doc Cach chon muc nen neu ban co ly do cu the de day muc 9.

Bay gio kiem tra kich thuoc kho luu tru voi tran cua ban.

Neu kho luu tru van vuot tran: chia, dung thu lai

Neu luong lam viec hai buoc cua ban dat kho luu tru o, vi du, 32 MB nhung Gmail tran o 25 MB, KHONG cu tang muc deflate - ban toi da chi thieu 1-2 MB va muc 9 se khong cuu duoc ban. Hai duong di tu day:

  1. Chia kho luu tru thanh cac phan multi-volume. Hau het cac trinh luu tru desktop (7-Zip, WinRAR, Keka) viet ZIP / 7z multi-volume ma nguoi nhan ghep lai tu dong. Cho ZIP thuan, dat kich thuoc volume thanh tran cua ban (25 MB cho dinh kem Gmail / Outlook, 10 MB cho cac relay email cu hon, 4 GB cho USB FAT32). Huong dan online zip vs 7z vs rar goi ten dinh dang nao nguoi nhan co nhieu kha nang ho tro ngay tu dau.
  2. Bo dong gop lon nhat con lai. Sap xep noi dung kho luu tru theo kich thuoc lai - neu mot tep la 60% so byte, gui qua mot lien ket (Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer) va ZIP chi phan con lai.

Bat ky duong di nao nhanh hon va dang tin cay hon thu lai nen. Doc Cach nen thu muc cho email cho tham chieu day du theo tran-hop-thu.

Cong thuc cho tran cu the

Tran = 100 KB. Gan nhu chac chan mot bieu mau tai len (cong chinh phu, bai tap truong, don xin viec). O kich thuoc nay, ban khong nen - ban dang chon mot hoac hai anh va doi kich thuoc chung. Dung Photo Editor de doi kich thuoc len 1200-1600 px chieu rong, luu thanh JPG chat luong 60, va ZIP chi neu bieu mau yeu cau .zip. Mot JPG da doi kich thuoc thuong duoi 100 KB tu no; wrapper ZIP them 30 byte header.

Tran = 2 MB. Thong dung cho tai len cong, dinh kem email nhe. Chay Compress Image o chat luong 70 tren JPG, chuyen HEIC sang JPG o chat luong 70, sau do ZIP. Cho 5-10 anh, ket qua gan nhu luon ha xuong 1.4-1.8 MB.

Tran = 25 MB. Gioi han dinh kem mac dinh cua Gmail va Outlook. Chay luong hai buoc o chat luong 75-80 (it manh tay hon). Cho thu muc anh hon hop va vai tai lieu, cai nay ha duoi 25 MB trong lan thu dau trong hau het truong hop. Neu thu muc nguon >500 MB HEIC, mong doi chia thanh volume.

Tran = 10 MB. Mot so relay email doanh nghiep / legacy. Cung nhu 25 MB nhung chat luong 65 tren anh va can nhac bo nhung cai khong thiet yeu truoc buoc ZIP.

Nhung gi KHONG hieu qua va tai sao

  • "Cu zip hai lan." ZIP mot ZIP duoc 0 giam - ZIP ben trong da duoc nen deflate, va deflate khong the nen cac byte da nen. ZIP ben ngoai them ~30 byte header va do la tat ca.
  • "Chon muc nen cao hon va thu lai." Di tu muc 6 sang muc 9 tiet kiem 1-5% tren noi dung nen duoc va 0% tren noi dung da nen. Neu ban 30% vuot tran, muc 9 se khong dua ban den do.
  • "Dung dinh dang khac - 7z nho hon." 7z (LZMA2) thuc su nho hon ZIP (deflate) tren noi dung nen duoc - doi khi nho hon 30-50% tren van ban. Nhung tren anh / video / noi dung da nen, 7z cung khong tiet kiem gi, va nguoi nhan cua ban co the khong mo duoc .7z ma khong co trinh doc ben thu ba. Huong dan online zip vs 7z vs rar bao quat khi nao trao doi dinh dang dang gia.
  • "Ma hoa voi mat khau de no nho hon." Ma hoa khong thay doi kich thuoc tep - thuc te them ~30 byte overhead moi tep. Mot ZIP duoc bao ve bang mat khau co cung kich thuoc voi cai khong duoc bao ve (modulo cac byte header).

FAQ

Tai sao cung thu muc zip thanh 18 MB tren laptop cua toi va 22 MB truc tuyen?
Cac trien khai deflate khac nhau dua ra lua chon hoi khac nhau o cung muc. macOS Finder, Windows Explorer, 7-Zip va cac cong cu truc tuyen moi cai co mot thu vien deflate chon cac tham so match-finder hoi khac nhau. Su khac biet hiem khi hon +-5%. Neu ZIP truc tuyen cua ban lon hon vat chat, cong cu truc tuyen co the dang buoc muc 1 (nhanh nhat) hoac dong goi lai theo tung tep.

Toi co the bao cong cu ZIP "dung o 25 MB" khong?
Khong cong cu ZIP tieu chuan nao phoi bay mot flag kich thuoc-muc tieu - dinh dang khong ho tro. Cai ban co the lam la yeu cau cong cu chia dau ra thanh cac volume 25 MB (ZIP multi-volume). Hau het cac trinh luu tru desktop phoi bay dieu nay; nhieu cong cu truc tuyen khong.

Thu muc la 1.2 GB anh HEIC. Toi co the gui email duoi dang mot ZIP khong?
Khong duoi bat ky tran nao. ZIP sau khi chuyen HEIC sang JPG o chat luong 60-70 va ban co the ha xuong gan 200-400 MB. Cai do van can chia se lien ket, khong phai dinh kem email. Dung Drive / Dropbox / WeTransfer.

Neu toi xoa tep tu ZIP sau khi tao, kich thuoc co giam khong?
Co. ZIP ho tro xoa moi tep. Hau het cac trinh luu tru (7-Zip, WinRAR, Keka, cong cu online ZIP File) cho phep ban loai bo cac tep ca nhan tu kho luu tru hien co; kho luu tru duoc viet lai ma khong co chung va kich thuoc giam tuong ung. Day la cach sua nhanh nhat khi ban 1-2 MB vuot tran va mot tep khong thiet yeu.

Tai sao "nen tep zip xuong 100kb" la mot thu neu gan nhu khong bao gio hieu qua?
Cau truy van phan anh cac gioi han bieu mau tai len thuc te (cong e-gov, don xin dai hoc, mot so bieu mau doanh nghiep van tran dinh kem o 100-200 KB). Cau tra loi thuc te la ban khong nen - ban dang chon mot hoac hai tep va doi kich thuoc chung xuong kich thuoc do. Wrapper ZIP la buoc dong goi, khong phai buoc thu nho.
