Join PDFs from multiple files merges two or more PDFs into a single document while preserving bookmarks, internal links, and page order.

  1. Upload two or more PDF files from your device.
  2. Confirm the merge order (filenames sort alphabetically; rename first to override).
  3. Click Merge and download the combined PDF.

Note: The merge runs in the browser - uploaded files never leave your device, and nothing is stored after the session. Tip: To reorder pages within a single PDF after merging, use Split PDF by range and re-merge.
