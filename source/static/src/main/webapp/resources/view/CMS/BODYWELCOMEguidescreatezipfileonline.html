If you arrived here looking for a way to create a ZIP without installing anything on your computer, the creator above is the right answer: drop a folder or a selection of files onto the upload zone at /zip-tools/zip-file.html, name the archive in the Settings modal, optionally add a password, then click Convert and download the result. The whole round trip is a few seconds for most folders, and the source files do not stay on the build service - they are removed after a short retention window.
