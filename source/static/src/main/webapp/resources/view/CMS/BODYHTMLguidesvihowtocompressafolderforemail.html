Cach Nen Mot Thu Muc Cho Email - Tung Buoc (2026)

Da xem lai lan cuoi 2026-04-24. Hoat dong tren Windows 10/11, macOS 13+ (Ventura va sau), va trong trinh duyet qua Zip File khi mot quy trinh shell-zip nguyen ban khong co san.

Cau tra loi 30 giay. Nhap chuot phai vao thu muc → "Gui den thu muc ZIP (nen)" tren Windows (hoac "Nen" tren macOS). Dinh kem .zip ket qua vao email. Neu ZIP vuot qua gioi han cua nha cung cap (thuong 20-25 MB, xem bang kich co email ben duoi), thi (a) chia nguon thanh hai thu muc va ZIP moi cai, hoac (b) tai len mot dich vu truyen file va dan lien ket vao than email.

Tai sao nen mot thu muc cho email?

Cac tep dinh kem email duoc do bang byte. Cac thu muc khong nen khong the duoc dinh kem - cac khach hang email yeu cau mot tep duy nhat, khong phai mot thu muc. Nen sup do thu muc thanh mot archive ZIP duy nhat bao ton cau truc ben trong, ten tep va ngay sua doi, trong khi thu nho tong kich co thong qua nen LZ77 / DEFLATE. Cho cac thu muc noi dung hon hop (tai lieu, hinh anh, bang tinh), mong doi giam kich co 20-50%; cho cac thu muc da bi chi phoi boi cac dinh dang nen (JPG, MP3, MP4), mong doi chi vai phan tram vi cac dinh dang do da duoc ma hoa entropy.

Ba ly do thuc te de ZIP truoc khi gui:

  • Gioi han kich co nha cung cap. Gmail, Outlook va iCloud deu gioi han tep dinh kem rieng le. Mot ZIP co the vua duoi gioi han khi thu muc tho khong the.
  • Bao ton cau truc. Nguoi nhan nhan cay thu muc nguyen ven sau khi giai nen - khong phai 47 tep roi o goc cua thu muc Downloads cua ho.
  • Toan ven trong khi van chuyen. ZIP luu tru CRC-32 cho moi tep; cac ban tai xuong bi hong tu danh dau khi giai nen de ban co the thu lai thay vi nhan im lang cac byte bi hong.

Tung buoc tren Windows 10 va 11

  1. Xac dinh vi tri thu muc trong File Explorer. Neu ban dang nen mot tap con cua cac tep thay vi toan bo thu muc, tao mot thu muc moi trong va sao chep cac tep vao do truoc - ZIP khong the "chon-va-luu" cac tep roi nguyen ban ma khong nhom chung lai.
  2. Nhap chuot phai vao thu muc. Tren Windows 11 menu shell moi xuat hien; neu ban khong thay tuy chon ZIP ngay lap tuc, nhap "Hien thi them tuy chon" o duoi cung de tiet lo menu co dien.
  3. Chon "Gui den" → "Thu muc nen (zip)". Windows tao <ten-thu-muc>.zip trong cung thu muc.
  4. Doi ten ZIP neu muon (mac dinh ke thua ten thu muc).
  5. Dinh kem ZIP vao email cua ban. Keo tha vao cua so soan thao hoac su dung nut clip / "Dinh kem tep".

Windows 11 da them ho tro 7z va tar nguyen ban vao nam 2023; cho tep dinh kem ZIP cu the, gan voi .zip vi macOS va moi panel xem truoc webmail giai ma no ma khong can tai xuong. Neu nguoi nhan xac nhan 7-Zip hoac tuong duong duoc cai dat, chuyen sang .7z voi LZMA2 co the cao them 10-20% tu kich co voi chi phi cua mot buoc nen cham hon.

Tung buoc tren macOS 13+ (Ventura, Sonoma, Sequoia)

  1. Chon thu muc trong Finder.
  2. Nhap chuot phai (hoac Control-nhap) → "Nen '<ten-thu-muc>'". macOS tao <ten-thu-muc>.zip trong cung thu muc.
  3. Doi ten archive neu can. macOS bao ton metadata tep va resource forks ben trong thu muc __MACOSX, ma mot so nguoi nhan Windows coi la rac; xem FAQ ve cach loai bo chung ben duoi.
  4. Dinh kem vao email qua cach keo tha tieu chuan hoac hanh dong Dinh Kem Tep.

macOS cung ho tro luu tru dua tren Terminal voi zip -r archive.zip thu-muc/ (BSD zip) hoac ditto -c -k --sequesterRsrc thu-muc archive.zip (lenh ditto cua Apple). Ditto la nhung gi Finder goi ben duoi va duoc uu tien cho bat ky thu muc nao chua resource forks cu the cua macOS.

Gioi han tep dinh kem email chan mot thu muc da ZIPed trong 2026

Cac gioi han cu the cua nha cung cap gay ra hau het cac loi "ZIP thu muc cua toi khong se gui." Bo chan hau nhu luon la tran cua nguoi nhan, khong phai gioi han gui cua ban - biet ca hai truoc khi ban nen, vi mot ZIP thu muc 30 MB ma Gmail cua ban gui tot van bi tra lai chong lai mot nguoi nhan Outlook 25 MB. Cac thu muc bi anh huong dac biet vi cac archive thu muc co xu huong dong goi hang chuc muc cung mot luc; mot anh qua co ben trong thu muc du de day toan bo ZIP qua gioi han cua nguoi nhan.

  • Gmail: gui 25 MB / nhan 50 MB. Cac tep dinh kem lon hon duoc tu dong chuyen huong qua Google Drive.
  • Outlook (Microsoft 365): gui mac dinh 20 MB; nhan 25 MB cho hau het cac goi. Cac thue moi doanh nghiep co the duoc cau hinh den 150 MB nhung cac nguoi nhan ben ngoai thue moi thuong van thay 25 MB.
  • Outlook.com (mien phi): gioi han gui 10 MB truoc khi chuyen huong OneDrive.
  • iCloud Mail: 20 MB; Mail Drop cua Apple kich hoat cho cac tep dinh kem lon hon (may chu iCloud luu tru tep trong 30 ngay).
  • Yahoo Mail: 25 MB.
  • SMTP cong ty / tuy chinh: thay doi nhieu (10-150 MB). Hoi quan tri vien cua ban hoac thuc hien mot lan gui kiem tra voi mot tep ~15 MB.

Khi ZIP vuot qua gioi han cua nguoi nhan, thong bao bounce thuong noi "thong diep qua lon" hoac "554 5.7.1." Neu ban thay loi do, dung gui lai voi cung ZIP - chuyen sang chien luoc lien ket truyen file.

Khi nao them mat khau vao ZIP

Them mat khau khi thu muc chua bat cu thu gi trong: tai lieu phap ly, hop dong da ky, du lieu nhan su, ho so tai chinh, thong tin dang nhap hoac bat cu thu gi tuan theo thoa thuan bao mat. ZIP duoc bao ve bang mat khau ma hoa noi dung tep voi AES-256 (mat ma "truyen thong" ZIP 2.0 bi vo va khong nen su dung). Nguoi nhan can mat khau qua mot kenh rieng - khong dat mat khau trong cung email voi tep dinh kem.

Con duong trinh duyet nhanh nhat la Zip File tao ZIP duoc bao ve mat khau ma khong cai dat phan mem. Neu ban chi can mo mot ZIP da duoc bao ve mat khau ma ban kiem soat, cong cu phu hop la Xoa Mat Khau ZIP. Cho ma tran quyet dinh ngan gon ve ai nen biet gi, xem huong dan lien quan ve cac loai mat khau PDF: chu so huu vs nguoi dung - cung phan loai ap dung cho quyen truy cap vs chinh sua ZIP.

Khi nao bo qua ZIP va su dung dich vu truyen file

ZIP la cau tra loi dung cho cac thu muc duoi tran tep dinh kem cua nguoi nhan. Tren do, cac quy trinh dua tren tep dinh kem tro nen mong manh - nguoi nhan tra lai, bo loc spam danh dau, han ngach hop thu cong ty day. Ba tin hieu rang mot dich vu truyen file (Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, WeTransfer hoac hosting cua ban) thang mot tep dinh kem:

  • ZIP lon hon 20 MB di den bat ky tai khoan email tieu dung nao.
  • Nguoi nhan tren email cong ty han che (phap ly, tai chinh, y te thuong co bo loc kich co va noi dung manh me).
  • Thu muc nhieu phuong tien (video, anh tho) noi nen se khong giam kich co dang ke - hosting hieu qua hon dinh kem.

Neu ban can nguoi nhan co the xem noi dung ma khong tai xuong truoc (xem chi doc), mot lien ket co the chia se den mot thu muc dam may thang mot tep dinh kem ZIP tren moi truc: co the xem truoc, luon cap nhat va co the thu hoi rieng le.

Giam kich co ZIP truoc khi gui

Neu thu muc cua ban ZIP den ngay tren gioi han kich co, bon giam thuc dung truoc khi chuyen sang truyen file:

  • Nen anh truoc. JPG o chat luong 85 thuong khong the phan biet duoc voi nguon va nho hon 30-50%. Chay cac anh roi qua Compress Image truoc khi ZIP.
  • Chuyen doi anh HEIC tu iPhone. Cac tep HEIC, mot khi o trong ZIP, khong the duoc xem truoc tren nhieu nguoi nhan Windows. Chuyen qua HEIC to JPG truoc - JPG lon hon moi tep nhung mo o moi noi ma khong can cai dat codec. Xem HEIC vs JPG vs WebP cho quyet dinh dinh dang day du.
  • Loai bo metadata macOS. Tren macOS, cac archive Finder bao gom tieng on __MACOSX va .DS_Store. Lenh ditto loai bo nhung cai do; ngoai ra, nen tren may Linux / Windows khong bao gio san xuat chung.
  • Chia thanh nhieu ZIP. Nhieu khach hang email chap nhan 2-3 tep dinh kem mot cach lich su hon mot ZIP qua co. Nhom theo chu de (taillieu.zip + anh.zip) thay vi theo ranh gioi byte tuy y.

Cac Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

Tai sao ZIP cua toi van vuot qua gioi han sau khi nen?

Vi thu muc nguon bi chi phoi boi noi dung da nen. JPG, PNG, MP3, MP4, DOCX va XLSX deu da duoc nen ben trong; thuat toan DEFLATE cua ZIP co it entropy bo sung de loai bo. Cho cac thu muc noi 80%+ cua cac byte den tu cac dinh dang nay, mong doi chi vai phan tram giam. Trong nhung truong hop do, chuyen sang mot dich vu truyen file thay vi co gang ep ZIP duoi gioi han.

Toi co the bao ve mat khau mot ZIP thu muc tren Windows ma khong co phan mem bo sung khong?

Lenh "Gui den → Thu muc nen" cua shell Windows khong ho tro bao ve mat khau nguyen ban. Ban co ba lua chon: (a) su dung cong cu dua tren trinh duyet Zip File cua chung toi de tao mot ZIP duoc bao ve mat khau, (b) cai dat 7-Zip va chon "Them vao archive..." voi mot mat khau, hoac (c) su dung PowerShell voi cmdlet Compress-Archive cong them mot buoc ma hoa ben thu ba. Cong cu trinh duyet la lua chon ma sat thap nhat vi khong yeu cau cai dat.

Neu nguoi nhan noi "ZIP bi hong" hoac "trich xuat that bai" thi sao?

Ba nguyen nhan pho bien: (1) trinh quet virus cua nha cung cap email da sua doi archive trong khi van chuyen (khong pho bien nhung xay ra voi cac cong antivirus cu hon); (2) ZIP duoc tao voi Windows Explorer va chua ten tep khong-ASCII ma trinh trich xuat cua nguoi nhan khong the giai ma; (3) tai xuong bi cat ngan (it co kha nang tren HTTP hien dai nhung van co the tren mang di dong khong on dinh). Sua: re-nen thu muc tren mot OS khac (neu duoc tao macOS, thu lai tren Windows hoac su dung ditto voi --sequesterRsrc), hoac tai len lai qua mot dich vu truyen file xac minh checksum o phia may chu truoc khi phoi bay lien ket tai xuong.

Tom tat

Nen mot thu muc cho email la mot thao tac shell nguyen ban 10 giay tren Windows va macOS. Quyet dinh thuc su la liec ZIP ket qua co vua duoi tran tep dinh kem cua nguoi nhan khong - neu co, gui di voi menu shell nhap phai; neu khong, chon giua chia thanh cac ZIP nho hon va chuyen sang mot lien ket truyen file. Them mat khau bat cu khi nao thu muc chua bat cu thu gi duoc quy dinh hoac bao mat, va nho rang HEIC va phuong tien da nen se khong thu nho nhieu - chuyen doi hoac host nhung cai do rieng biet.

San sang tao ZIP cua ban? Bat dau voi Zip File cho nen dua tren trinh duyet voi bao ve mat khau tuy chon. Cho huong nguoc lai, cong cu Xoa Mat Khau ZIP mo khoa cac archive ma ban da co quyen mo.
