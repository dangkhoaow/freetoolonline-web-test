Cac loai mat khau PDF - mat khau chu so huu vs nguoi dung giai thich

Xuat ban 19 thang 4 nam 2026

Ma hoa PDF ho tro hai loai mat khau khac nhau, va chon sai loai la nguon goc thuong gap cua hoi tiec "mat khau cua toi khong bao ve gi het". Huong dan nay giai thich moi mat khau khoa cai gi (va khong khoa cai gi), khi nao dat mot hoac ca hai, cach chon mat khau chong duoc cac cuoc tan cong hien dai, va khung phap ly khi go mat khau ma ai do giao cho ban.


Hai loai mat khau nhin nhanh

Moi PDF tu PDF 1.4 (Adobe Acrobat 5, phat hanh 2001) co the mang den hai mat khau rieng biet trong lop ma hoa:

  • Mat khau nguoi dung (doi khi goi la mat khau mo). Can de mo tep. Neu khong co, khong trinh doc nao hien duoc noi dung. Trinh xem PDF tu choi giai ma cac luong trang cho den khi mat khau nguoi dung duoc nhap.
  • Mat khau chu so huu (cung goi la mat khau quyen hoac mat khau master). Tep mo tu do cho bat ky ai, nhung trinh doc ap dat mot bo co quyen: khong in, khong sao chep van ban, khong sua doi, khong chu thich, khong dien bieu mau, khong tach trang. Mat khau chu so huu can de bo nhung han che do va chinh sua tai lieu.

Ban co the dat mot, hai, hoac ca hai tren cung mot tep. Lua chon phu thuoc vao cai ban dang co gang ngan chan.

Khi nao dung mat khau nguoi dung

Mat khau nguoi dung la lua chon dung khi noi dung tu no la bi mat va ai khong co mat khau khong duoc doc bat ky thu gi. Cac truong hop dien hinh:

  • Hop dong khach hang va NDA. Neu noi dung PDF nhay cam, mat khau nguoi dung bao dam tep bi chan (chuyen tiep nham dia chi, lay tu may tinh xach tay bo lai tren tau) khong the mo duoc.
  • Phieu luong va ho so thue. Phieu luong nhan vien gui qua email nen mang mat khau nguoi dung ngay ca khi email duoc gui qua kenh bao mat - tep co the duoc chuyen tiep lai.
  • Ho so y te chia se voi benh nhan. Mat khau nguoi dung giu ho so rieng tu ngay ca khi hop thu cua benh nhan sau do bi xam pham.
  • Ban thao noi bo phat cho nhom nho. Chia se mat khau qua kenh rieng (Signal, cuoc goi dien) han che rui ro ro ri tu mot kenh duy nhat.

Khi nao dung mat khau chu so huu (mat khau quyen)

Mat khau chu so huu dung khi ban muon noi dung duoc doc rong rai nhung khong muon nguoi ta in, sao chep, hoac chinh sua. Cac truong hop dien hinh:

  • Whitepaper va tai lieu marketing. Ai cung mo va doc duoc, nhung sao chep dan van cua ban vao slide doi thu kho hon.
  • Chuong trinh su kien va thuc don. Doc duoc, khong sua duoc - giam rui ro ban sao cuc bo duoc tai xuat ban duoi thuong hieu cua nguoi khac.
  • Bieu mau co the dien duoc xuat ban de xem xet. Chan sua doi cac truong bieu mau cho den khi phat hanh phien ban da sua.

Luu y: mat khau chu so huu duoc trinh doc thuc thi, khong phai lop ma hoa. Cac cong cu desktop bo qua co quyen (nhieu tien ich PDF ma nguon mo lam vay) van co the tach, in, hoac chinh sua noi dung. Hay coi mat khau chu so huu nhu mot yeu cau lich su chu khong phai ranh gioi bao mat.


Khi nao dung ca hai

Dat ca hai mat khau la lua chon that lung va day deo cho cac tai lieu bi mat ma cung phai chong chinh sua sau khi mo hop phap. Dung ca hai khi:

  • Mot hop dong duoc chia se voi doi tac doi tac nen doc duoc nhung khong duoc sua.
  • Mot bao cao tai chinh duoc phat cho nha dau tu doc duoc nhung khong chuyen tiep tung trang.
  • Mot ho so y te duoc cap cho chuyen gia can no nhung khong nen in them ban sao de tren van phong chia se.

Chon mat khau thuc su bao ve duoc tai lieu

Ban than ma hoa PDF (AES-128 tu PDF 1.6, AES-256 tu phan mo rong PDF 1.7 muc 8) la vung chac - tan cong hien dai tap trung vao mat khau, khong phai vao cipher. Mat khau co the brute-force trong vai ngay tren rig GPU thue khong phai la bao ve.

  • Do dai thang do phuc tap. Muoi hai ky tu hon hop chu hoa/chu thuong, chu so va dau cau, hoac bon tu tu dien khong lien quan (kieu correct-horse-battery-staple), chong duoc tan cong GPU hien dai trong nhieu the ky.
  • Tranh bat cu thu gi xuat phat tu tep. Tieu de tai lieu, ngay xuat ban, ten khach hang, ten tac gia - tat ca deu la nhung phong doan dau tien trong cuoc tan cong nham muc tieu.
  • Chia se mat khau ngoai dai. Tep co the qua email; mat khau nen qua Signal, cuoc goi dien, hoac quan ly mat khau co quyen vao kho chia se. Mat khau dinh kem email pha vo ma hoa ngay ngay dau tien.
  • Xoay vong cho luu tru dai han. PDF ma hoa nam 2015 voi mat khau sau ky tu la tai lieu mo nam 2026. Ma hoa lai tep luu tru moi vai nam voi cum mat khau dai hon khi suc tinh toan ngay cang re hon.

Go mat khau PDF hop phap

Go mat khau hop le khi ban co mat khau va duoc phep lam viec voi tep da giai ma. Truong hop pho bien: mo sao ke ngan hang de gop vao ho so ca nhan; giai ma kho luu tru noi bo de xuat ban lai; lay lai tai lieu ban da ma hoa ban dau nhung gio can chia se voi nhom. Remove PDF Password yeu cau mat khau ban da biet; no khong bypass hay be khoa mat khau khong biet.

Neu ban hop phap quen mat khau tren tep do ban tao, kiem tra quan ly mat khau truoc, sau do email hoac tai lieu noi mat khau ban dau duoc chia se. Voi tai lieu nhan tu doi tac, lien he nguoi gui - dung bao gio thu brute-force tai lieu nhan duoc; tai nhieu khu vuc phap ly dieu do qua ranh gioi truy cap trai phep ngay ca khi tai lieu da o trong tay ban.
