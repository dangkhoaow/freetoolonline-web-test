Paste any JavaScript into the editor on the left, click Minify, and the compressed bundle appears on the right ready to copy. The step runs in your browser - no upload, no build server, no account - so a draft you have not saved yet never leaves your laptop.

Wondering whether minify is the only step you need? A minifier strips whitespace and comments while keeping every identifier intact; an uglifier renames identifiers; a tree-shaker removes unreachable code. The three are not interchangeable. The decision tree lives in CSS minifier vs uglifier vs tree-shaking (the same logic applies to JS), and the cold-start framing is in How to minify CSS/JS for Cloud Run cold start.
