Trinh Chuyen Doi HEIC vs JPG: Khi Nao Moi Cach Tiep Can Thang (2026)

Da xem lai lan cuoi 2026-04-25. Bao gom iOS 18 (HEIC voi gain map), iCloud Photo Library, xuat macOS 13+ Photos va trang thai codec HEIF Windows 10/11 vao thang 4 nam 2026.

Cau tra loi 30 giay. Chuyen sang JPG khi anh roi khoi phan cung Apple va ban khong the xac nhan thiet lap cua nguoi nhan - chay qua HEIC to JPG. Giu HEIC khi anh van trong he sinh thai Apple, ban muon tep nho nhat cho dong bo iCloud, hoac ban van co the chinh sua anh (Live Photo, Che do Chan dung, gain map HDR deu o trong HEIC va bi loai bo khi xuat JPG). Khong duong nao dung khi ban thuc su can mot master san sang in - cho dieu do, xuat TIFF hoac DNG tu Photos va bo qua ca hai dinh dang.

HEIC va JPG thuc su la gi

HEIC la container mac dinh ma iPhone hien dai su dung cho dau ra camera. No bao boc mot anh duoc ma hoa HEIF (pixel HEVC, do sau 10 bit, chroma 4:2:0) voi metadata EXIF, va tren thiet bi gan day mot gain map cho mapping ton mau HDR. Ket qua la khoang nua kich co tep cua mot JPG tuong duong o chat luong tuong duong ve mat hinh anh. JPG (JPEG) la dinh dang anh pho quat - 8-bit baseline, 4:2:0 mac dinh, khong co gain map, duoc ho tro boi moi he dieu hanh va khach hang email phat hanh trong the ky nay. Su danh doi la kich co: mot HEIC 3 MB tipically la mot JPG 4-6 MB o chat luong 85, hoac 6-10 MB o chat luong 95. Lua chon dinh dang do la mot quyet dinh tinh tuong thich-vs-kich-co, khong phai mot quyet dinh chat luong.

Khi nao chuyen HEIC sang JPG

Chuyen khi nguoi tiep theo hoac he thong ma anh cham vao khong the doc HEIC. Nam tac nhan kich hoat dang tin cay:

  • Bieu mau tai len web tu choi HEIC. Hau het CMS (WordPress, Shopify), cong nhan su va dich vu in anh chap nhan chi JPG va PNG. Chuyen mot lan thang viec that bai theo tung nen tang.
  • Nguoi nhan Windows 10 hoac 11 khong co codec HEIF. Xem truoc Windows can goi HEIF Image Extensions (mien phi trong Microsoft Store, nhung hau het nguoi nhan khong chu chinh khong cai dat no). Gui JPG va anh mo trong trinh xem mac dinh.
  • Tep dinh kem email hoac tin nhan. Gmail, Outlook, Slack va iMessage tat ca deu xem truoc JPG noi tuyen. Tep dinh kem HEIC hoac hien thi hinh thu nho bi hong hoac duoc giam quy mo lang le xuong xem truoc do phan giai thap.
  • Chinh sua trong phan mem phat hanh truoc 2020. Photoshop, Illustrator va hau het trinh chinh sua anh tieu dung cua thoi do khong mo HEIC mot cach tu nhien. Chuyen sang JPG (hoac PNG, cho khong mat) truoc khi nhap.
  • In thong qua bat ky dich vu khong phai Apple nao. Hau het cac phong thu nghiem in truc tuyen tu choi HEIC khi tai len. Chuyen doi va kiem tra truoc khi dat hang.

Chay chuyen doi qua HEIC to JPG trong trinh duyet. Metadata EXIF duoc bao ton theo mac dinh; bo chon tuy chon EXIF trong Cai dat neu ban dang chia se cong khai va muon loai bo du lieu GPS hoac ID camera.

Khi nao giu HEIC goc

Chuyen doi loai bo thong tin ma HEIC mang va JPG khong the. Giu HEIC bat cu khi nao bat ky dieu nao sau day ap dung:

  • Dong bo iCloud Photo Library. iCloud luu HEIC o khoang nua kich co JPG. Chuyen doi cuc bo truoc tang gap doi hoa don luu tru iCloud cua ban ma khong co loi ich tuong thich trong cac ung dung cua chinh Apple.
  • Live Photo, Che do Chan dung hoac gain map HDR. Clip chuyen dong, ban do do sau va gain map deu song ben canh HEIC va bi loai bo khi xuat JPG. Xuat-sang-JPG la mot cua mot chieu.
  • Di-vong-lai trong Apple Photos hoac Affinity Photo. Ca hai chinh sua HEIC mot cach tu nhien va bao ton du lieu gain map khi luu. Ma hoa lai JPG them artifact tren moi chu ky luu.
  • Khan gia chi iPhone. AirDrop, Tin nhan giua nguoi dung iPhone va album duoc chia se tat ca xu ly HEIC tu dau den cuoi. Chuyen doi truoc dot khong gian dia va lich su chinh sua ma khong co loi ich cho nguoi nhan.
  • Bao ton nguon. Neu anh la mot anh ma ban co the re-chinh sua trong nam nam, HEIC la tai san gan nguon hon. Chuyen doi JPG chap nhan duoc cho viec gui mot ban sao; ban goc o lai.

Ba cach tiep can ngoai "trinh chuyen doi trinh duyet"

Trinh chuyen doi trinh duyet la cau tra loi dung cho hau het cac chuyen doi mot lan. Cho cac truong hop cu the, cac lua chon thay the nay tao ra mot ket qua tot hon voi it no luc hon:

  • Xuat macOS Photos nguyen ban. Mo Photos tren Mac → Chon anh → Tep → Xuat → Xuat Goc Khong Chinh Sua (giu HEIC) hoac Xuat ... → Loai Anh: JPEG (chuyen doi khi xuat). Photos xu ly xuat hang loat hang nghin HEIC o toc do cap he dieu hanh va bao ton gain map khi xuat sang dinh dang HEIF. Tot nhat cho xuat thu vien day du.
  • Cai dat xuat iOS nguyen ban. Cai dat → Camera → Dinh dang → "Tuong thich nhat" lam cho Camera iPhone chup trong JPG va H.264 thay vi HEIC va HEVC trong tuong lai. Su dung dieu nay neu iPhone cua ban chu yeu tao anh cho quy trinh lam viec khong phai Apple va ban muon tranh hoan toan buoc chuyen doi.
  • Chuyen doi tu dong Photos khi chuyen. Cai dat → Photos → Chuyen den Mac hoac PC → "Tu dong" chuyen doi HEIC sang JPG khi chuyen den muc tieu khong phai Apple qua cap Lightning hoac ung dung Files. HEIC goc o lai tren iPhone; dich nhan JPG. Tot cho cac chuyen ad-hoc noi thu vien nguon nen van la HEIC.

Trinh chuyen doi trinh duyet thang khi: ban can 1-50 anh duoc chuyen doi, cac tep nguon da ngoai iPhone (trong Files, Drive, Dropbox) va ban khong muon cham vao cai dat iPhone hoac Mac. Cho 100+ anh, xem cach chuyen doi 100 anh HEIC sang JPG.

Khi nao khong HEIC cung khong JPG la cau tra loi dung

Ca HEIC va JPG deu la dinh dang cap hien thi. Chung sai cho hai truong hop cu the:

  • Master in o A3 hoac lon hon. JPG luong tu hoa sang 8-bit va ap dung chroma subsampling; HEIC giu 10-bit nhung ap dung ma hoa entropy HEVC ma in co the khuech dai. Cho kich co in cao cap, xuat TIFF (16 bit moi kenh, khong subsampling) tu Photos: Tep → Xuat → ... → Loai Anh: TIFF.
  • Luu tru dai han cua du lieu goc tu camera. Neu anh se duoc xu ly lai trong 10 nam dua tren cac thuat toan mapping ton mau tuong lai, khong dinh dang nao bao ton du thong tin. Quay phim hoac xuat duoi dang DNG (container RAW mo cua Adobe) thay vi the. Hau het cac camera hien dai va iPhone gan day (15 Pro+ trong che do ProRAW) ho tro dieu nay.

Ngan gon: HEIC vs JPG la cau hoi cho anh hang ngay. Master in va luu tru quy mo thap ky can mot cuoc tro chuyen khac.

Trang thai tuong thich vao thang 4 nam 2026

Tinh tuong thich HEIC da cai thien trong nam nam ke tu khi Apple gioi thieu no, nhung van khong dong deu:

  • iOS / iPadOS / macOS: nguyen ban, khong cai dat. AirDrop, Tin nhan, Photos, Mail tat ca xem truoc HEIC noi tuyen.
  • Windows 10: can HEIF Image Extensions va HEVC Video Extensions tu Microsoft Store. Mien phi, ~30 giay de cai dat moi may.
  • Windows 11: giong nhu Windows 10 (Microsoft cung khong dong goi HEIF trong 11).
  • Android: Android 10+ co xem HEIC nguyen ban. Android 9 va thap hon can mot phong thu thu ba.
  • Trinh duyet web (xem truoc mot tep dinh kem khong tai xuong): Safari va xem truoc macOS co; Chrome, Firefox, Edge khong xem truoc HEIC noi tuyen vao thang 4 nam 2026.
  • Khach hang email: Apple Mail va iCloud xem truoc HEIC; Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo hien thi mot bieu tuong tep chung chung.
  • Widget tai len CMS: WordPress core ho tro tai len HEIC tu 6.4 (thang 10 nam 2023) nhung viec render phu thuoc vao chu de. Hau het CMS khac (Shopify, Wix, Squarespace) van yeu cau JPG hoac PNG.

Neu khan gia cua ban deu tren iOS hoac macOS, gui HEIC. Neu no hon hop hoac khong xac dinh, JPG la mac dinh an toan.

Cac Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

Ban sao JPG se trong te hon HEIC goc khong?

O chat luong 95, JPG khong the phan biet duoc voi HEIC ve mat hinh anh cho hau het cac anh tren mot man hinh duoc hieu chuan. O chat luong 85, ban co the thay banding nhe tren gradient muot (troi, ton da) vi JPG luong tu hoa sang 8-bit va HEIC luu tru 10-bit. Neu HEIC mang mot gain map cho HDR, xuat JPG loai bo no; mot man hinh co kha nang HDR se render JPG ma khong co highlight HDR. Cho chia se hang ngay, chat luong 85 la su can bang dung; cho dau ra chat luong in, su dung 95.

Chuyen doi HEIC sang JPG co loai bo metadata GPS hoac camera khong?

Khong theo mac dinh trong HEIC to JPG - EXIF (timestamp, GPS, mo hinh camera, ISO, toc do man trap) duoc bao ton tren JPG theo mac dinh. Bo chon tuy chon "Bao gom EXIF" trong Cai dat de loai bo metadata truoc khi tai xuong. Loai bo duoc khuyen nghi khi chia se cong khai (dien dan, danh sach truc tuyen) va khong khi luu tru (metadata la mot phan cua lich su anh).

Neu toi giu HEIC, toi noi gi voi nguoi nhan Windows khong the mo no?

Hai lua chon: (1) yeu cau ho cai dat HEIF Image Extensions mien phi tu Microsoft Store - mot lan, ~30 giay, sau do HEIC mo trong Photos va Explorer nhu bat ky hinh anh nao; (2) chi can gui ban sao JPG lan nay qua HEIC to JPG va tranh cuoc tro chuyen cai dat codec. Lua chon 2 thang cho nguoi nhan mot lan; lua chon 1 thang neu ban thuong xuyen gui HEIC cho nguoi do va cai dat tra lai trong vai thang.

Tom tat

HEIC vs JPG la quyet dinh tinh tuong thich, khong phai chat luong. Chuyen sang JPG khi anh roi phan cung Apple den nguoi nhan hoac he thong khong the doc HEIC. Giu HEIC khi anh van trong Apple, ban muon tiet kiem luu tru iCloud, hoac ban co the chinh sua lai va can gain map nguyen ven. Cho in cao cap hoac luu tru quy mo thap ky, khong dinh dang nao thang - xuat TIFF hoac DNG tu Photos.
